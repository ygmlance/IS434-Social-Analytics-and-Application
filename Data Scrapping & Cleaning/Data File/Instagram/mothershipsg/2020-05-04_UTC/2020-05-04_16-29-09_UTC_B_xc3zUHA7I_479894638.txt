As of May 4, 9:55pm, there are close to 3.6 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with almost 250,000 deaths.

Russia continues to report a surge in new daily cases, with over 10,000 new cases reported in the past 24 hours. Meanwhile, certain European nations such as Spain, Italy, France and Germany have reported significantly fewer new cases.

Singapore reported 573 new cases on May 4, five of which are Singaporeans/PRs. There were no new deaths reported. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
