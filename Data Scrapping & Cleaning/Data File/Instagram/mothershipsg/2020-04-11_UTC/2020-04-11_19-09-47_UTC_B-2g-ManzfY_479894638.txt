As of April 12, 12:35am, the total number of Covid-19 cases in the U.S. has exceeded 500,000. The death toll in the U.S. is also the highest in the world, edging out Italy, with over 19,600 deaths reported.

On April 11, Singapore reported 191 new cases of Covid-19, with 51 cases linked to dormitory clusters. A 90-year-old Singaporean man, identified as Case 1,142, became the 8th person to die of Covid-19 in Singapore.

Three new clusters were identified, all of which are located at foreign worker dormitories. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
