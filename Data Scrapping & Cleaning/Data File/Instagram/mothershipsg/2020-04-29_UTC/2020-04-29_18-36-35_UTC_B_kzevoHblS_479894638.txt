As of April 30, 12:33am, there are close to 3.2 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with over 220,000 deaths. Close to a third of the patients globally have recovered from the disease.

On April 29, Singapore reported 690 new cases of Covid-19, crossing the 15,000 mark for the first time. 5 new clusters have been identified, including 2 dormitories.
