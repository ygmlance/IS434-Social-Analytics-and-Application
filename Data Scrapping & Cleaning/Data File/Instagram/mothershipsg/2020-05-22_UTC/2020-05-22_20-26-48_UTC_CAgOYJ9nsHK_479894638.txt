As of May 22, 11:44pm, there are over 5.2 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with over 300,000 deaths.

The U.S., Russia and Brazil continue to have the most number of cases, while new cases in Europe have begun to slow down to three-digit numbers, with the notable exception of the UK.

On May 22, Singapore reported 614 new cases, bringing the total to 30,426. There are four Singaporean cases. [Please see page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
