JUST IN: On Apr. 22, Natureland spa and massage parlour put up a notice on their site, informing customers of their continued closure due to circuit breaker measures. "In light of the new government announcements and precautionary measures; we will continue to close until 01 June 2020 for all our outlets and home services. We seek your kind understanding on our new arrangements and apologise for any inconveniences caused.

We wish everyone to be safe and may everyone ride through this crisis together. Again thank you for your continuous support and patronage in the near future." Natureland has seven outlets in Singapore.
