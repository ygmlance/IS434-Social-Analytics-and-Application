As of May 3, 12:05am, there are over 3.4 million Covid-19 cases worldwide. China, which was the original epicentre of the virus, is no longer the top 10 most infected country.

On May 2, Singapore reported 447 new cases of Covid-19, four of which are Singaporeans/PRs. A 76-year-old Singaporean man also became the 17th person to die of the virus in the country. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
