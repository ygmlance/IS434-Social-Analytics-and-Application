As of May 27, 12am, there are over 5.6 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with close to 350,000 deaths.

The U.S. continues to report the most number of Covid-19 cases, with over 1.7 million total cases, and a death toll that is approaching 100,000.

On May 26, Singapore reported 383 new cases of Covid-19, bringing the total number of cases to 32,343. 706 more cases have been discharged, with over 16,400 cases, or around half of the total cases discharged thus far. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
