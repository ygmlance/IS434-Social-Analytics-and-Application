As of April 11, 1:05am, the total number of Covid-19 cases in the world has exceeded 1.6 million, with about 100,000 deaths.

Malaysia has announced that its lockdown measures will be extended till April 28, while Jakarta has started its own partial lockdown on April 10.

On April 10, Singapore reported 198 new cases, all of which are locally transmitted. An 86-year-old female Singaporean, identified as Case 987, became the 7th person to die of Covid-19 in Singapore. Three new clusters have emerged, including one at ICA Building. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
