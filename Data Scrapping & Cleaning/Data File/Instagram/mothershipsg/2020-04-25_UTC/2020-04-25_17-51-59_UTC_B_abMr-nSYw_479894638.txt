As of April 26, 12:05am, there are close to 2.9 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with about 200,000 deaths.

The total number of cases in the U.S. approach 1 million, with more than 50,000 deaths. The nation reported four times more cases than Spain, the next highest country on the list.

On April 25, Singapore reported 618 new Covid-19 cases, a drop from previous days. Most of the cases are linked to foreign worker dormitories. A new cluster involving seven cases at Northpoint City, Yishun, has been identified. [Please see Page 2 for SIngapore's Covid-19 situation.]
