As of May 19, 11pm, there are close to 5 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, 2 million of which have since recovered.

Brazil has overtaken the UK, with over 13,000 new cases announced, totalling over 257,000 cases. Russia is also the most infected country in Europe, with close to 300,000 cases.

As of May 19, Singapore has a total of 28,794 Covid-19 cases, with 451 new cases. Over 10,000, or more than a third of all patients in Singapore have been discharged from hospitals and community facilities. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
