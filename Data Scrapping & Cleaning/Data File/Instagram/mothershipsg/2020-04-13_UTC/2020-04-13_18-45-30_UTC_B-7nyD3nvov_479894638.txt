As of April 14, 12:35am, there are over 1.8 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with over 110,000 deaths.

On April 13, Singapore reported 386 new cases, which is the largest single-day increase thus far. 280 cases are linked to migrant workers' dormitories, and imported cases are now down to zero. A 65-year-old Singaporean man became the 9th person to die of Covid-19 in Singapore. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
