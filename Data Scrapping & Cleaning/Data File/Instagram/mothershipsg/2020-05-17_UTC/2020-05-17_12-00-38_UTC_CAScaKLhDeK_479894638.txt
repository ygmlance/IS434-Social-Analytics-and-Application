"This CB is really testing relationships..."
"This CB will make or break your relationship..." Because of Covid-19, many of us haven't seen our loved ones, friends, and significant others in over a month. For those in relationships, this means suddenly entering into what feels like a "long-distance relationship", where they are only able to spend time together virtually, not physically.

We speak to a couple to find out how they've been doing since the circuit breaker started, and a couples' therapist for tips on how to maintain your relationships during this CB period.
