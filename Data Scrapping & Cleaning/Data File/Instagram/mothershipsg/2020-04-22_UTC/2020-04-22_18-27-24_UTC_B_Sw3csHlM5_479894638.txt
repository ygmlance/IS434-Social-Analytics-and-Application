As of April 23, 12:05am, there are around 2.6 million Covid-19 cases in the world, with about a third of them from the U.S.

As the rate of infection begins to slow down, certain European nations are easing its Covid-19 measures, while some remain cautious.

On April 22, Singapore reported 1,016 new Covid-19 cases, bringing the total number of cases over the 10,000 mark for the first time. An 84-year-old female Singaporean became the 12th person to die of Covid-19, 19 days after being confirmed with the virus. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
