As of April 17, 1:08am, the total number of Covid-19 cases in the U.S. has exceeded 650,000, with over 33,000 deaths.

The total number of cases in Japan is close to 10,000, prompting Japan's Prime Minister Shinzo Abe to declare a nationwide state of emergency. Meanwhile, Taiwan reported no new cases for the second time this week.

On April 16, Singapore reported 728 new cases of Covid-19, another record-high increase. The total number of cases in Singapore has now surpassed 4,000. Of the new cases, 81% were linked to known clusters, while the rest are pending contact tracing. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
