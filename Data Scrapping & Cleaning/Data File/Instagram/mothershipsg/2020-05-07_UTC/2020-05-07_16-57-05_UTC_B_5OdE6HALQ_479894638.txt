As of May 7, 11:15pm, there are over 3.85 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with over 265,000 deaths.

Russia has overtaken both France and Germany in terms of the total number of cases, after several days of reporting over 10,000 new cases daily.

Singapore reported 741 new cases of Covid-19 on May 7, adding up to a total of 20,939 cases for the nation. According to an infectious diseases expert, the total number of cases in Singapore could hit 40,000 by the end of the month. [Please see Page 2 for Singapore's Covid-19 situation.]
