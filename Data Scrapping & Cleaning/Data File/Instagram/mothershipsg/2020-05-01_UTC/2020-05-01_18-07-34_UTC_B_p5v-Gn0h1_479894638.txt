As of May 2, 12:30am, there are over 3.35 million Covid-19 cases worldwide, with over 237,000 deaths.

On May 1, Singapore reported 932 new cases of Covid-19, bringing the country's total to over 17,000. A 60-year-old male Singaporean became the 16th person to die of the virus. The patient had a history of hypertension and hyperlipidaemia.
