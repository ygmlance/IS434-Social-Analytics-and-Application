Work from home. Home-based learning. No bubble tea. We’ve been adjusting to one big change after another because of COVID-19. But do some people find it hard to accept the changes and how does one stay resilient in unusual times like these?

We talked to some health experts on how to cope with our fluctuating lives and accept the reality check that things will probably never go back to the way things were before. Among some helpful tips to stay resilient include establishing a daily routine to reduce anxiety, engaging in a particular activity or hobby to help us be in control and focused, and to stay socially connected with others – even if it’s online for now.
