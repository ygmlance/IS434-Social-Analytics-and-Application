Anyone who grew up in the 90s would remember watching Robin Leong a lot on TV, with shows like Triple Nine and The Challenge.

He then went on to establish a martial arts studio called Ch’i Life Studio in 2002, where he conducts classes for parents and kids alike.

You can now add “children’s book author” to his resume, after publishing The Kung Fu Force And The Tower Of Doom. It’s the first in a series that follows a group of animal characters who vow to protect the world with their kung fu skills. But just to be clear, Po and Master Shifu aren’t those characters.
