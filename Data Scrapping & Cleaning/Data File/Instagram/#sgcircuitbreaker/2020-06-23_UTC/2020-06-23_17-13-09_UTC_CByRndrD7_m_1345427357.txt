#ArdhaUttanasana

In Sanskrit
Adhas = down 
Mukha = face 
Svana = dog

Ardha = half 
Uttana = intense stretch 
Asana = pose

Benefits of this pose

Relieves back pain.

Relieves tension in the shoulders, chest and lats.

Increases leg flexibility and lengthens the muscles of the legs and back

corresponding muscle(s) focus:
Arms and Shoulders
Lower Back
Biceps and Triceps
Core (Abs)
Hamstrings
Pectoral Muscles Lats
