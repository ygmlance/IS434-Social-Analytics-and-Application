In Sanskrit
Ardha = half 
Pincha = feather 
Mayura = peacock 
Asana = pose

Benefits of this pose

Builds upper body strength.

Opens chest, forearms and shoulders.

Stretches the entire leg.

strengthens and tightens the core muscles.

Good for women, who are pregnant and fighting symptoms related to menopause.

Improves digestion.

Improves the spinal alignment.

Energises the body bringing in fresh blood to the head.

Activates the various glands, leading to a better balance of the body.

corresponding muscle(s) focus:
Arms and Shoulders
Lower Back
Biceps and Triceps
Core (Abs)
Hamstrings
Quadriceps
