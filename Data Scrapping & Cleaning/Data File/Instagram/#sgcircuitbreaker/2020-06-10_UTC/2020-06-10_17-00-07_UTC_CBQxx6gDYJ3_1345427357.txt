#UpavisthaMarjaryasanaBitilasana

In Sanskrit
upavistha = seated
marjari = cat 🐱
bitila = cow 🐮
asana = pose

Benefits of this pose

Improves the flexibility of muscles.

Stretch releases the tension of neck and upper back.

Strengthens spine.
