The 10-Day Love Challenge is one that focuses on caring for yourself, your partner, your family and friends. A mix of fitness and wellness activities will guide you along as you achieve that! ⁠
⁠
In order to care for others around you, start first by caring for yourself. Often times, we tend to forget about the most important person, which is ourselves. ⁠
⁠
Here are some Self Care Tips: ⁠
😊 Get 7-8 hours of sleep a night ⁠
😊 Be grateful, express forgiveness and be mindfully aware⁠
😊 Be Assertive, Let people know what you need or when something upsets you ⁠
😊 Eat a balanced meal and drink sufficient water daily⁠
😊 Exercise regularly ⁠
😊 Enjoy life - do things that you love and brings you joy! ⁠
