𝙄𝙣𝙜𝙧𝙚𝙙𝙞𝙚𝙣𝙩𝙨: (1 serving)
80g papaya (firm & ripe, skin & seeds removed)
15g peach gum
8g rock sugar
3 red dates, pitted & halved
200ml cool boiled water
3 scoops of Similac Mum vanilla milk powder
.

𝙈𝙚𝙩𝙝𝙤𝙙𝙨:.
1. Soak peach gum in water overnight till it expands and is soft to touch. Remove the impurities and rinse clean.
.
𝙉𝙤𝙩𝙚: Higher grade quality peach gum will have lesser impurities.
.
2. Bring a pot of water to boil. Put soaked peach gum, papaya, rock sugar, red dates, and 100ml water into a double boiler pot. Cover and double boil for 1hr 30 mins.
.
3. In a separate cup, mix 3 level scoops of Similac Mum milk powder with 100ml of water. Add into the double boiler and cook for 2 more minutes and it’s ready to serve.
.
*Comparison among all maternal milk in Singapore as of January 2020 both per 100 g of powder and per serving.
