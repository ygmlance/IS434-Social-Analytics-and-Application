Last cooking project for the SG Circuit Breaker period - we travel to Italy 🇮🇹 and experience Pork and Frutti di Mare Risotto, reduced in Tiger Beer!

We didn’t have white wine at home, so I had to improvise and use the alcohol that we can always find in the fridge (hello @tigerbeersg !). As we also ran out of chicken stock, I made a new stock from scratch using onions, carrots, parsley, and saffron. Luckily everything turned out nice!
