In Sanskrit
uttana = stretched out
shishu = puppy
asana = pose

Benefits of this pose

Great for increasing the overhead shoulder flexion range and extending the thoracic spine.

Relieves tension and insomnia.

Stretches the shoulder muscles.

Stretches and lengthens the spine.
