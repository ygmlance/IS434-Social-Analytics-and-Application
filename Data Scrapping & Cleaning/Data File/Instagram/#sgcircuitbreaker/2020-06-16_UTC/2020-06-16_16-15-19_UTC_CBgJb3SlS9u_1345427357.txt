#SuptaPadangusthasana B

In Sanskrit
supta = reclining 
pada = foot 
angusta = big toe 
asana = pose 
Benefits of this pose

Increases hip mobility and flexibility.

Opens the hips and provides a powerful stretch for the adductor muscles, groin and hamstrings.

Releases tension in the inner thighs and groin.

Stretches the chest.

Relieves lower back pain.

Improves blood circulation, especially in the legs.

corresponding muscle(s) focus:
Lower Back
Feet and Ankles
Hamstrings
Hips
