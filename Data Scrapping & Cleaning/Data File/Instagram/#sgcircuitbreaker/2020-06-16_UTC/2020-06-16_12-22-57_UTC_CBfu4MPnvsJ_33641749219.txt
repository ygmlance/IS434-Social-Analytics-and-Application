Centella Asiatica, aka Cica, is a natural botanical herb that grows primarily in Asia. It is also commonly known as gotu kola, tiger grass or Indian pennywort. 
For years, it has been used as an anti-inflammatory ingredient to treat wounds as well as dermatological conditions.

Cica has shown to display potent antioxidant properties and is an excellent hydrating ingredient that helps soothes and repair irritated skin.
