Benefits of this pose

Tone the obliques muscles.

Creates more flexibility of the spine and opens the tight muscles around the base of the spine.

Creates a deep stretch at the psoas muscles while also opening them.

Gently massages the internal organs like the stomach, spleen, kidneys, liver, etc.

Good for healing lower back aches and pains.
