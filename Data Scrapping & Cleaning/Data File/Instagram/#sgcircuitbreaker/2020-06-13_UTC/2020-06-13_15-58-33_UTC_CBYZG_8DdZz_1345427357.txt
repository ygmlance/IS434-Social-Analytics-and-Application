#DoubleVPose

Benefits of this pose

Stretching the shoulders (trapezius muscles) with the crossing of the arms in front, on the floor.

While the upper back muscles are stretched, the front upper chest (pectoral muscles) is contracted and compressed. Hence is a good practice to open the back of the lungs.

Relieves tension in the rhomboids, trapezius, latissimus dorsi, shoulders, and neck.

Increase range of motion and prevent injury.

corresponding muscle(s) focus:
Arms and Shoulders
Neck
