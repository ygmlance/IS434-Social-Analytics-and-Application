Decent shoes are a must regardless of the workout that you're doing - whether it's running, at the gym, various sports, etc. Injury caused by inappropriate shoes can needlessly derail your exercise regime. ⁠
⁠
Investing in quality shoes can help you to prevent foot and ankle damage, and make your workout a more pleasant and comfortable experience! The most important part of find the right pair of shoes is the right fit. ⁠
⁠
Here are some tips on getting a good fit: ⁠
👟 try on shoes in the afternoon, evening, or after your workout ⁠
👟 Make sure the heel is not slipping⁠
👟 Factor in some space as your feet swell during exercise ⁠
👟 Wear your exercise socks when trying on new shoes⁠
👟 Laces should be tight so the shoe doesn't move, but not so tight that you don't feel your feet⁠
👟 Make sure the breath ability of the shoe you choose matches the weather you are planning to run in⁠
👟 Bend the shoe to check flexibility and pick the right one for your activity⁠
⁠
And there you have it, time to get new shoes if yours doesn't meet the criteria above! ⁠
