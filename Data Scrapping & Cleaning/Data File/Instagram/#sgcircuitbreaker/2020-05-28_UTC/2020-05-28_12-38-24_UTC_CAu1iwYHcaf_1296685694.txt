1) Steam fish for 15-25mins depending on the size of fish till cooked.
2) Pour 450ml water and Singapore Curry Paste into a big pot. Mix well and bring to a boil. 
3) Add in the tomatoes, brinjal, lady fingers and taupok. Mix well and bring to a boil. 
4) Mix Singapore Curry Premix with 200ml water. Add in the pot and simmer for about 7 mins without covering the pot, stirring occasionally. 
5) Place the cooked fish on top of the cooked vegetables together with fried tau kee. Bring to a boil and simmer for 2-3 minutes. 
6) Serve hot with rice. Enjoy! .
