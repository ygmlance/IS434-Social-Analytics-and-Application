~LOW CARB KETO PRETZELS~
These Low carb pretzels ðŸ¥¨ make a great snack. I hope you enjoy it!! The recipe is as follows... INGREDIENTS:
* 1 cup almond flour
* 1 tablespoon baking powder * 1 teaspoon garlic powder
* 1 tablespoon mixed fried herbs
* 1/2 cup mozzarella * 1/2 cup freshly made paneer or cream cheese
* 1 large egg, lightly beaten
* 1 egg yolk for brushing
* Salt to taste
INSTRUCTIONS: * In a medium bowl, whisk together the almond flour, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, mixed herbs.
* In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine the mozzarella cheese and paneer .Microwave until completely melted.
* Quickly add the almond flour mixture and the beaten egg. Mix to incorporate, then knead until smooth. Add little water if needed.
* Divide the dough into 4 equal portions. Roll each portion into a thin rope, then shape the rope into a pretzel. * Brush the tops of the pretzels with the egg wash. * Bake the pretzels until golden brown, 15-18 minutes in the preheated oven at 170/c.
