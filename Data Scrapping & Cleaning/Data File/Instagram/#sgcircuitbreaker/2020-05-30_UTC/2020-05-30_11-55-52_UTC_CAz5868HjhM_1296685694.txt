This simple pizza dough doesn’t require any proofing at all and can be cooked straight away! How convenient right?

𝑰𝒏𝒈𝒓𝒆𝒅𝒊𝒆𝒏𝒕𝒔: (makes 9 thin 7” pizza dough, less if you make thicker or bigger size dough).
400g self-raising flour
360g plain Greek-style yogurt.

𝑫𝒊𝒓𝒆𝒄𝒕𝒊𝒐𝒏𝒔: (Pls watch cooking video above for reference).
.
1. Mix ingredients together and knead until a soft pliable dough is formed.
2.On a floured surface, divide dough into portions and roll out round to your desired size and thickness. (I prefer thin crust as you can see in the video how thin & crispy my pizza crust is!).
*Use fork to poke holes all over the surface of the dough to prevent bubbles when baking.
3. Pre-cooked dough in preheated oven 200°C fan forced for 5 mins.
.
𝑵𝒐𝒕𝒆: Pre-cooked pizza base can be stored in the freezer for future use.
. . . 【Easy Margherita Pizza Recipe shared by Baking Taitai】.
.
𝑰𝒏𝒈𝒓𝒆𝒅𝒊𝒆𝒏𝒕𝒔:.
1 can tomatoes, crush or puréed
1 block parmesan cheese
1 block Mozzarella cheese, cut into 1/4 inch cubes
extra virgin olive oil
fresh basil leaves, whole or chopped.
.
𝑫𝒊𝒓𝒆𝒄𝒕𝒊𝒐𝒏𝒔:.
1.Preheat oven at 200°C.
2. Brush the outer circumference of dough with olive oil.
3.Spread a large spoonful of tomato sauce evenly on the surface of pizza crust.
4. Grate parmesan cheese generously over the tomato sauce.
5. Distribute the cube mozzarella evenly over the whole pizza.
6. Baked until the cheese is bubbling and caramelized and the edges of the pizza are golden brown.
7. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil and serve immediately. Enjoy!
