Poached Eggs | Garlic Aioli 🥚🧄
.
🍴Overall ease: 7/10
.
🛒Ingredients: eggs, garlic, lemon, oil, bread
.
👩🏻‍🍳Process: 
Garlic aioli - blend together egg yolk, roasted garlic, lemon juice & zest, salt, and pepper before whisking in lots of oil (used both olive & canola oil) till the mixture becomes thick & creamy
Poached eggs - carefully add the eggs to simmering water and poach for 3 minutes
.
😋Taste: 8/10. #garlicaioli is a lighter & yummy alternative to the richer hollandaise sauce and paired well with the eggs, vegetables, and bread to make for a simple and tasty brunch
