Let’s support local F&B 💪🏼 With our Telegram bot, you can easily find F&B outlets near you!

How to use our Telegram bot:
1. Search for @SGLocalShops_bot on Telegram or simply click the link in our bio
2. Click on START and say Hello! 
3. Set your location, then select ‘takeaway’ or ‘delivery’
4. Choose an outlet you want to know about
5. Order through their website or contact number!

We've also put together a step-by-step in our Story Highlights for your convenience~
