Combination hobs are usually the hob of choice for apartment kitchens due to their compact size. Combination hobs can intermix; a gas hob, a grill hob, and an induction hob are installed side-by-side for different cooking needs.
.
An advantage of these hobs is that they can be easily replaced to fit lifestage changes. For instance, an induction hob can replace the gas hob for the safety of young kids in the family.
