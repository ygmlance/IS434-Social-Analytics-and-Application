On this #ThirstyThursday, we bring you Green Day, another Crafted at Westin signature cocktail that you can remake in the comfort of your home. 
Here's what you'll need:
- 45ml of gin
- 20ml of lemon juice
- 10ml of sugar syrup
- 3 sprigs of basil
- 5 green grapes
- A slice of cucumber

Add three grapes, 30ml of lemon juice, 10ml of sugar syrup, and muddle. Then, add gin and shake well. Pour into a chilled glass and top it off with a slice of cucumber on the rim. Enjoy!
