#AromacookeryRecipeChickenMushroomStew
â€¢
~ Mix 6 small drumsticks (approx. 500gm) with 1 tsp sugar, 2 tsp light soy sauce, 2 tsp oyster sauce, 1 tsp potato starch & 1 tsp sesame oil. Marinate overnight in the fridge.
~ Bring chicken to room temperature before cooking.
~ Heat up some oil in a stew pot.
~ Saute sliced ginger (as much or as little as you like), one small onion (cut into wedges), and one big can of button mushrooms.
~ Remove and set aside.
~ In the same pot, briefly sear chicken drumsticks.
~ Add 2 tsp each of light soy sauce, oyster sauce & dark soy sauce.
~ Add sufficient water to cover the chicken. Place veggies on top of chicken.
~ Cover pot and simmer till chicken is tender.
Optional: Garnish with spring onion.

Note: Actual amount of water will depend on the kind of pot you use. Some retain moisture/heat better, so won't require as much water. Adjust accordingly.
