[Yan Cantonese Cuisine] - The restaurant celebrates the dragon boat festival with a trio of Cantonese-style rice dumplings, which includes a special vegan-friendly dumpling. It is available for delivery or takeaway, customers can choose to purchase the dumplings frozen or steamed (ready-to-eat).
.
The trio rice dumplings are
- Abalone and Fish Maw Rice Dumpling (鲍鱼花膠粽)
- Shrimp and Scallop in XO Sauce Rice Dumpling (带子虾球极品酱)
- Preserved Vegetables with Heura Chicken Rice Dumpling (Vegan) 梅菜纯植物Heura肉粽 (純素)
