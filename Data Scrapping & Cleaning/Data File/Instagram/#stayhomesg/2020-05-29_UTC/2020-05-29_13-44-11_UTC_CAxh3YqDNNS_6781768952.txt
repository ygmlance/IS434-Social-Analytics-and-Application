15 Mins Sesame Bowl (for 1)
1 portion diced cucumbers
1 portion edamame
1 chicken breast fillet diced and cooked
140g noodles (I used soba)
Sauce (whisk and pour over bowl): 1/4 cup sesame paste, 1/4 cup warm water, 3 tbs soy sauce, 1 tbs sesame oil, 1 tbs rice wine vinegar, 1 clove garlic grated and 1 tbs sugar 
Sesame seeds to garnish
