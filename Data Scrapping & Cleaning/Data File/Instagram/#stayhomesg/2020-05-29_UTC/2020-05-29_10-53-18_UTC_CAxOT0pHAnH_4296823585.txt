How can I reduce cravings and snacking at home? 🤔TIP 1.
.
.
Stop eating sugar. Sugar is addictive. Reduce your consumption and your cravings will reduce too. If you can work towards cutting out sugar completely, you will be surprised at how little you crave it. Avoid using artificial sweeteners as a sugar replacement as these can increase your desire for something sweet and increase your appetite.
