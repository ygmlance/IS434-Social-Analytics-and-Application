3 Ingredients No Oven Chocolate Fudge Cake Recipe you must try!! 😱
If you are new to baking and would like to make a nicely densed fudge cake for a sinful craving, you got to try it!
🎥 Full recipe and step by step video in my YouTube Channel, go check in out! #ZaTaYaYummyRecipe

Ingredients:
Chocolate cake:
250 grams of oreo
250 ml of milk
1 1/4 tsp of baking powder

Fudge sauce:
100 grams semi sweet chocolate
30 ml of milkInstructions:

Instructions:
Blend oreo, milk and baking powder in a blender.
Pour into a 6 inch greased and line cake pan and steam on high for about an hour.
If you are making the fudge sauce to go along, melt chocolate with milk then pour on the top as finishing.
The cake taste best when it's warm, so reheat it in the microwave if you need to!
