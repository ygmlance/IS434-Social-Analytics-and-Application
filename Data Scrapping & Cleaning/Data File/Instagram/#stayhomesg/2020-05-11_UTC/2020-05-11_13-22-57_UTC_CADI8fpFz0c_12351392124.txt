Misinterpreting religion

With various countries implementing lockdowns, many people are spending more time on the Internet. Extremist groups seize the opportunity to propagate their narratives online. 
In this 4 minutes video, our RRG member, Ustaz Tengku Mohd Fouzy emphasises the importance of being vigilant when seeking religious knowledge. This topic aims to ask the Muslim community to reject any form of extreme interpretation or narrative. --- Menyalah tafsir agama

Semasa #covid19, beberapa naratif pelampau tersebar luas di dunia maya mahupun di kalangan masyarakat. Topik ini bertujuan untuk mengajak masyarakat Islam menolak segala bentuk tafsiran atau naratif yang melampau. Ustaz Tengku Mohd Fouzy juga akan menekankan pentingnya mempraktikkan agama Islam dengan betul.
