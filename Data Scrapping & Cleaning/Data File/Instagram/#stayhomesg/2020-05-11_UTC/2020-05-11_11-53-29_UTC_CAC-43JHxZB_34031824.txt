Children's favourite and it's easy to cook.

Ingredient:
1 pack naibai 
Slice pork
Garlic
Onion
Salt
Oyster sauce
2tps @liloikanbilis mushroom powder
Water

Instruction:
1.Cook garlic & onion together in the pan
2.Add Slice pork in and cook 3/4 done
3.Add naibai(veg) in & fry 1min 
4.Put 1 tsp salt
5.Put 1½ Oyster sauce
6.fry well
7.Add water(own preference)
8.Add in @liloikanbilis powder
9. Cover lid to slimmer till soften
10. Done while serving
