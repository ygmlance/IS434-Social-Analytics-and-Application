In the midst of this COVID-19 Pandemic, fear, worry and negativity shroud the world.

Our Team at Music Box wish to join the ranks of many other Musicians in the world to spread positivity and keep the spirits high for everyone.

So, here's our rendition of the classic song by various Chinese Artistes - 明天会更好!

Tomorrow Will Be Better!
Sing Along with us, help us Like & Share to Spread the Love!
