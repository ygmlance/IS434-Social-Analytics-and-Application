Our immune system is important as it protects our body from viruses and bacteria. It is especially crucial to keep yourself healthy during this period, so here are some immunity-boosting foods to help you stay healthy: 
1. Drink enough water
2. Food and drinks that are high in vitamin C like golden kiwi, pineapple, broccoli, green peppers, and orange juice 
3. Food that is high in vitamin E is known for its antioxidant properties to help prevent cellular damage like cooked egg, almonds, spinach and avocado
4. Food that is rich in omega-3 like salmon, sardines, and mackerel
