Chanko Nabe 🍲
.
🍴Overall ease: 10/10
.
🛒Ingredients: for the soup base - dashi, mirin, sake, garlic, ginger juice; for the ingredients - diced chicken, plethora of vegetables, tofu, shrimp, anything else you fancy / have in your fridge
.
👩🏻‍🍳Process: extremely effortless! Simmer the broth, and then assemble all your ingredients. Serve it hotpot style for even less effort! (Note: this was during ingredient prep and broth testing stage. Our subsequent meal was done steamboat style!)
.
😋Taste: 9/10. So easy, yet tasty and nutritious! Perfect for a cold rainy afternoon, and also for days where you feel guilty from unhealthy eating. Use any leftover broth to cook noodles soup or porridge the next day to minimise wastage
