In the universe,
There is an earth.
On the earth, there is a city.
In the city, there is a street. 
In the street, there is a house.
In the house, there is a room.
In the room, there is a child.
In the child, there is a heart.
And in the heart,
There is the universe.
~R. Tamminga~
