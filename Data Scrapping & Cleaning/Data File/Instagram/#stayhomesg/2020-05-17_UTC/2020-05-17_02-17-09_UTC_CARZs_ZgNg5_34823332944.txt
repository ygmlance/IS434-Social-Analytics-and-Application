Have a flippin’ Great Sunday ahead!! Here are the ingredients and steps that I used to create this flippin’ 🥞✍🏻 .
💁🏻‍♀️Part 1 
1. 2 egg yolks
2. 3-4 tablespoons of milk
3. 2 tablespoons of olive oil
4. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
5. 40 to 50grams of plain flour.

Mix ingredients 1 to 4 together with 💕, slowly add in the sieved plain flour. Add the flour until it is flowly not too gluey then leave it to rest.
.
💁🏻‍♀️Part 2
1. 3 egg whites
2. 1 tablespoon of white vinegar 
3. 2 tablespoon of sugar

Mix ingredient 1 & 2, beat with a electric beater and gradually add in the sugar. Beat the contents until it turns into a fluffy cloud ☁️ foam form. 
Lastly fold part 2 into part 1 and the pancake mixture is ready to hit the pan 👩‍🍳 .
