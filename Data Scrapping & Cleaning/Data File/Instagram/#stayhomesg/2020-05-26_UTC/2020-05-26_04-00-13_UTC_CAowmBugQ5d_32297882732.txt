Piled high on cheese with a subtle tanginess of tomatoes, these baked mussels are packed with flavors. 👉🏻 You can find our YouTube video link in bio.

Method
1️⃣ Prepare your ingredients and chop up the tomatoes
2️⃣ Fill individual mussel shell with the tomatoes
3️⃣ Pack on as MUCH cheese as possible by pressing it down, but without squashing the mussels
4️⃣ Set the air fryer for 180 °C for 8 minutes. If you are using an oven or a toaster oven, watch till the cheese melts and turns golden brown before you remove it

Ingredients
5  x Mussels
5  x Cherry tomatoes
100g x Shredded Cheese
