⁣⁣Crispy clam noodles- I don't really know what this is really. It's half local Tzechar, half Japanese, but all kinds of delicious and 100% the kind of food I love to eat these days. ⠀⠀
⠀⠀
Thick beehoon is crisped in a hot wok with a little bit of oil. As you press it down, the starch in the noodles will help the noodles to adhere together almost like a thin pancake. ⠀
⠀
The ankake(thick starchy sauce) consists of sautéed garlic, red chillis, leeks, clams, white pepper, saké and dashi, then thickened with kuzu starch and finished with some spring onions for freshness.
