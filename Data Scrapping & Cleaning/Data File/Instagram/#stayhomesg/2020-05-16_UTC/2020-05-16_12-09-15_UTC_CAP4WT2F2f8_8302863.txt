Ingredients:
2tbsp honey (origins organic raw honey)
50gr unsalted butter (@afp.sg)
150gr cornflakes (@kelloggssingapore)
Sprinkles

Methods:
1. Melt honey and butter over gentle heat, until frothy. Off the stove.
2. Add in the cornflakes and stir well until the cornflakes are evenly coated.
3. Spoon cornflakes into individual mini baking cases. Add sprinkles.
4. Bake on preheated oven 150deg celcius for 10-12 mins or until golden brown.
