{Crispy Airfried Salmon with Garlic Butter Lemon Sauce ~ #hlfunbake172 } ⁣
Salmon is rich in omega-3 and using airfryer is the easiest, without ending up with oily kitchen. ⁣
⁣🍋
⁣🍋
Ingredients:⁣
* Salmon fillet (I bought 1 whole salmon 3.24kg @fairpricesg for only S$64.48 ⁣
* Salt & black pepper⁣
* Minced garlic⁣
* Lemon juice⁣
* Lemon slice for garnish⁣
* Butter⁣
* Fresh parsley (Chopped)⁣
⁣🐟
⁣🐟
Instruction:⁣
1. Rub salt & pepper on room temperature salmon fillet.⁣
⁣
2. Airfry salmon with skin up at 200 degree for 13 mins.⁣
⁣
3. Meanwhile, prepare sauce. In a saucepan , melt butter over medium heat. ⁣
⁣
3. Add garlic and saute, pour in lemon juice. ⁣
⁣
4. Plate salmon and drizzle sauce, sprinkle parsley and garnish with lemon slice. ⁣
⁣
🍽 Serve immediately!⁣
⁣
⁣🔴🔴 The trick to crispy #asmr worthy skin is, airfry to about 10 mins, tear off the skin carefully and continue airfrying to the 13 mins. Every airfryer is different so you open to check.
