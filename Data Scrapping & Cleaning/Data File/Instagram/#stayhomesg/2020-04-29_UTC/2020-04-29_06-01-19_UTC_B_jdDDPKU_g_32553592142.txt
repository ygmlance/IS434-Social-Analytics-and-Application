What you need:⠀
💕 A Chair⠀
⠀
ACTIVATION:⠀
1️⃣ Glute Kickbacks (10 reps per side)⠀
2️⃣ Controlled Flutter Kicks (20 reps)⠀
⠀
Repeat both exercises until 4 mins is up.⠀
⏰ 30 secs REST⠀
⠀
CIRCUIT:⠀
⚡️Single Leg Bench Squats (8 reps per side)⠀
⚡️Tricep Dips + Calf Raises (10 reps)⠀
⚡️Leg Extension (10 reps per side)⠀
⚡️Banded Calf Raise (10 reps per side)⠀
.⠀
⏰ 1 min REST⠀
⠀
BURNOUT⠀
🔥 Crunch + Leg Abduction⠀
1 min NO rest!⠀
