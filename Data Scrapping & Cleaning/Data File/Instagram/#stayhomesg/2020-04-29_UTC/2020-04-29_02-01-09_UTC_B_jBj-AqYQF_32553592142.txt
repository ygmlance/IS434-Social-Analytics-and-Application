What you need:⠀
💕 Towel⠀
⠀
ACTIVATION:⠀
1️⃣ Side Knee-tucks (10 reps per side)⠀
2️⃣ Plank Leg Abductions (10 reps)⠀
⠀
Repeat both exercises until 4 mins is up.⠀
⏰ 30 secs REST⠀
⠀
CIRCUIT:⠀
⚡️Single Leg Knee Tucks (8 reps per side)⠀
⚡️Pikes (10 reps)⠀
⚡️Ab roll-out (8 reps)⠀
⚡️Drag your butt (30 secs)⠀
.⠀
Repeat circuit for 4 sets!⠀
