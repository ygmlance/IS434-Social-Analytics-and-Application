SESAME BOBA LATTE?!
Decided to add some Matcha cookies & cream ice cream to my drinks because the weather is so hot lately! Triple Dosage of Matcha- Matcha, Matcha Cookie Ice cream and Matcha powder
.
Steps
1️⃣ Prepare 15g of grounded Sesame Powder
2️⃣ Add 60ml of Hot water to dissolve it
3️⃣ Add a teaspoon of Sugar for some sweetness
4️⃣ Pour in the boba pearls prepared beforehand
5️⃣ Add ice cubes to the boba until it’s 80% full
6️⃣ Add 180ml of Fresh Milk
7️⃣ Pour 3️⃣ into the glass and it’s ready to serve
.
