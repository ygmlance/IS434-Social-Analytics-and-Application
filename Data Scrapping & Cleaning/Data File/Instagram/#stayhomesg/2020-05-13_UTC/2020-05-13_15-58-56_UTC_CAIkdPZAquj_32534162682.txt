Recipe by @maangchi with additional ingredients 💓
👩🏻‍🍳: 𝘏𝘰𝘮𝘦𝘤𝘰𝘰𝘬𝘦𝘥
🍳𝗜𝗻𝗴𝗿𝗲𝗱𝗶𝗲𝗻𝘁𝘀:
⬇️⬇️⬇️ For 4-5 pax-
2 cups of kimchi (chopped) 
½ pound of pork belly (sliced into bite sized pieces) 
1 mini bowl of pork mincemeat 
1 bowl of wombok cabbage (chopped) 
1 packet of tofu (cut into bite sized cubes) 
2 stalks of spring onions (chopped) 
2 tablespoons of gochujang (hot pepper paste) 
1 teaspoon of sugar
5 cups of water
😋😋😋😋😋
𝗜𝗻𝘀𝘁𝗿𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻𝘀:
⬇️⬇️⬇️
1. Heat up pot to high heat and add kimchi to stir. 
2. Add pork belly and mincemeat and stir. 
3. Add gochujang and sugar and stir.
4. Add wombok and water. 
5. Boil for 30 mins on high heat. 
6. Add tofu and stir. 
7.  Let it cook for another 10 mins on low heat. 
8. Add spring onions and you're ready to eat!🔥Goes well with rice 🍚💯
