INGREDIENTS

MARINADE for chicken:
1 tsp light soy sauce
1 tsp oyster sauce

1 tsp corn starch

METHOD
1. Remove skin from boneless chicken leg. Cut into cubes. Mix well with marinade ingredients. Cover and leave for at least 30 minutes.
‚Ä¢
2. Wash and rinse the rice well. Leave in a colander to let water drain through.
‚Ä¢
3. Peel and cut Japanese yam into bite-sized chunks. Set aside.
‚Ä¢
4. In a wok or non-stick pan, heat oil and stir-fry ginger until fragrant. Add chicken, and stir-fry until half done. Do not overcook the chicken while in the wok.
‚Ä¢
5. Add drained rice and stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until rice grains are dry and separate.
‚Ä¢
6. Transfer rice and chicken into rice cooker. Add salt and water. Place Japanese yam on top.
‚Ä¢
7. Around 12 minutes before the rice is done, place chopped spinach on top of rice to steam-cook.
‚Ä¢
8. When the rice is cooked, use a spatula to mix all ingredients. Stir gently to avoid smashing the Japanese yam. Optional: Add some pre-soaked goji berries if you like.
‚Ä¢
9. Cover the lid for another 5 minutes. Dish up, serve hot and enjoy! üçöüòã
