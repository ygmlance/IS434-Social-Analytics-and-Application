The Hanshin Electric Railway has released 10 colouring books featuring iconic scenes along their line, which links Kobe to Osaka for free so you can stay at home and imagine yourself traveling along the rail route’s famous sites, such as Amagasaki Castle and Port of Kobe. You can choose to download and print either the full booklet (PDF) or just the image (PNG).⁣
⁣
The website also features videos of colouring tips for guidance so it’s suitable for complete beginners as well!⁣
