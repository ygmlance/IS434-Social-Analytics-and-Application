An ode to @capellasingapore's rich heritage, Bob's Bar's Old Fashioned is a rendition of a classic, international cocktail with hints of Asian spices, made with our bespoke Navegante Rum. We're sharing the recipe so that you can shake up your at-home cocktail repertoire!

Ingredients:

50ml Navegante Rum
5ml Housemade Allspice Dram
5ml Sugar Syrup
3 Drops Angostura Bitters
1 Drop Tobacco Tincture (Optional)

1. Place a large ice cube in an Old Fashioned glass. 
2. Combine rum, syrup, angorustura bitters, allspice dram and cigar tincture.
3. Garnish with orange twist.
