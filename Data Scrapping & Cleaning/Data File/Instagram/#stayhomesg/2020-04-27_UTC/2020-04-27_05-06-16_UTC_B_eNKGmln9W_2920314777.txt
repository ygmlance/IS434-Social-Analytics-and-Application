Meatless Monday. 'Green' Mango Appetizer ...
Forgotten I left a green Mango (for my rojak) in the refrigerator. By the time, I saw it, it is no longer so green. Waste not. Shredded it, season with salt, sugar, fruit vinegar. Sprinkle chilli flakes, sesame seed and crushed peanut. Still delish.
