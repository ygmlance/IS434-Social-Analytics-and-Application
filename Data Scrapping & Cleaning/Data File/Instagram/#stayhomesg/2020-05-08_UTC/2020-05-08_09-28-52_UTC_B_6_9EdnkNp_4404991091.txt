This Mother’s Day, concoct your own aperitivo of love with Graffiti Sky Bar’s 𝘔𝘪 𝘈𝘮𝘰𝘳 𝘊𝘰𝘤𝘬𝘵𝘢𝘪𝘭 – an original creation by our bartender, Joel. Simply follow the four-step recipe below: ⁣
⁣
𝐌𝐢 𝐀𝐦𝐨𝐫 𝐂𝐨𝐜𝐤𝐭𝐚𝐢𝐥 ⁣
𝐈𝐧𝐠𝐫𝐞𝐝𝐢𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐬 ⁣
30ml Absolut Vodka ⁣
20ml Strawberry purée ⁣
15ml Peach 1883 30ml Yuzu juice ⁣
15ml Triple Sec ⁣
15ml Egg White ⁣
⁣
𝐌𝐞𝐭𝐡𝐨𝐝 ⁣
1. Begin by pouring the Absolut Vodka, strawberry purée, Peach 1883, yuzu juice, Triple Sec and egg white in a shaker. ⁣
2. Add ice and shake the concoction well so as to create a good foam upon pouring. ⁣
3. Be sure to pour over a chilled coupe glass, otherwise known as a Champagne Saucer. ⁣
4. Garnish the drink with dried rose petals and serve. ⁣
