Ingredients:
150g Self Raising Flour
40g Sugar
1/4 tsp Pink Salt
100g Bonsoy / Fresh Milk
10g Homemade Pandan Extract
1 Egg
15g Extra Virgin Coconut Oil
.
Steps
1. Mix flour, sugar and salt in mixing bowl
2. Add beaten egg, milk, pandan extract and coconut oil.
3. Use hand whisk to mix all ingredients and rest for 10-15 mins. (Refer to video for consistency of batter)
4. Scoop batter onto non-stick pan (without oil) to pan fry until golden brown on both side.
.
