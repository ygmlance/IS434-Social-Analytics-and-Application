Ingredients:
1 ripe banana
1 tbsp milk
50g soft butter
75g plain flour
75g caster sugar
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp baking powder
1 egg
50g chocolate chips

Steps:
1. Mash up banana
2. Pour in all the ingredients (except choc chips)
3. Use electronic mixer to beat ingredients together
4. Pour in choc chips and mix well.
5. Pour into baking tray.
6. Bake in preheated oven of 160°C for 40 mins.
