Garlic Stir fry (Serving Size 2~3 minions or 1 GIANT)⠀
Baby Nai Bai - 300g⠀
Garlic - 7 clove⠀
Chilli Padi Red - 5⠀
Salt - UP TO YOU!⠀
Oil (Sesame & Olive) - UP TO YOU!⠀
⠀
⠀
Heat oil > add garlic and chilli, stir fry in low-medium heat > add veggies, stir fly in high heat for about 2 minutes > add salt, stir fry for another minute or 2 (i like to add a tiny bit of water to have some gravy but u don't have to) > done!⠀
