Japanese somen with tuna and assorted vegetables (Recipe adapted from the manga, What Did You Eat Yesterday?) Ingredients: Somen noodles, canned tuna (mix with some mayonnaise), chopped tomato, julienned cucumber, shredded shiso leaves, umeboshi (remove the seed and chopped into small pieces), toasted sesame seeds and chopped spring onions
Sauce for noodle: Grated ginger, mentsuyu, some water

Method: Cook the somen according to package instructions, drain with colander and wash under running water. Mix mentsuyu with grated ginger and some water, adjust the amount of water to your liking. 
Place the noodle in a bowl, topped with the assorted vegetables and sesame seeds, pour the mentsuyu sauce on the noodles. Enjoy!
