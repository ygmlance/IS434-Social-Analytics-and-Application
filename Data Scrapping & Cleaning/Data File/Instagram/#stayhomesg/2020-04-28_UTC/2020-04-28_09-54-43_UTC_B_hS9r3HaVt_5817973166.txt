#Didyouknow, our abalones can be stored both in the fridge for 2-3 days or in the freezer for about 2 weeks to retain its freshness after opening the cans. If you need to store them for more than 2 weeks (but not longer than 1 month), we recommend you to allow the abalones to naturally defrost first in room temperature and heat up before serving. With these #tipsfromoceanus, you now know what to do when you have unfinished abalones the next time :) P.S. Leave us a 👍🏼 if you would like more of such tips from us!
