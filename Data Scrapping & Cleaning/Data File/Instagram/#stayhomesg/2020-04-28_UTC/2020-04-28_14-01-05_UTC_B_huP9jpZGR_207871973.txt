Tomato Egg Noodles! 🍅🍳🍜
Today’s recipe is a homely comfort food that warms you up from the inside. I kept the ingredients simple & healthy so everyone can give it a shot! Recipe below ❤️ #ShersVideoRecipes
—
Ingredients (Serves 4)
4 eggs
2 diced tomatoes
2 largely chopped tomatoes
Handful of garlic and onions
1 cup of diced chicken
1/4 sliced mushrooms
2 TBS oyster sauce
2L boiling water
A lot of veggies
500g of your preferred noodles
Diced cucumbers and spring onion for toppings (optional!) #ShersFoodJournal #stayhomesg #stayhomewithascott
