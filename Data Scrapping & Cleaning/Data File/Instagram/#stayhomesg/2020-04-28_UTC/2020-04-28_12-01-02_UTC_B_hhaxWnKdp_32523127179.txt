Sourdough | Matcha | Honey 🍞
.
🍴Overall ease: 2/10
.
🛒Ingredients: matcha, honey, flour, water, salt
.
👩🏻‍🍳Process: 
1) Feed rye starter twice, wait for it to double
2) 1 hour autolyse of flour (400g bread flour, 50g whole wheat) and 350g water
3) Fold in 80g active rye starter, 10g of salt, a truckload of matcha powder; rest for 30 minutes
4) Stretch and fold 4 times, rest for 30 minutes each time
5) Shape into boule, put into banneton, and leave in fridge overnight 
6) Morning: score and bake!
.
😋Taste: 10/10. Didn’t know if this #matchahoney combination would work for sourdough (didn’t see any recipe online), but it turned out a winner! Would make this again, all the time.
