Braised Pork Belling in Soy Sauce (Tau Yu Bak)

Tau Yu Bak (Braised Pork Belky in Soy Sauce) is extremely flavourful after taking time soaking in the soy sauce and spices. its fats melting into the gravy making it such a savoury dish. .
.
Hard boiled eggs, dried bean curd (tau pok) and bean curd (tau kwa) are added in to make this dish complete.
