Fruit Tartlets with Custard Filling
Recipe by: @tintinrayner
Rebake by: @monika_aja
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Crust Ingredients:
150gr unsalted butter
1.5tablespoon sugar (adjust to your preferred sweetness)
250gr all purpose flour
2egg yolks
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Methods:
1. Mix cold butter, sugar and flour with wooden spoon/fingertips just until crumbly. Add egg yolks. Mix until just combine.
2. Shape into small balls (I weighed mine about 25gr each). Press and shape into the mould.
3. Poke several holes into the base of the crust with a fork.
4. Bake on preheated oven, 170deg celcius for 20-25minutes or until golden brown.
5. Let cool and remove from mould.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Custard Filling Ingredients:
500ml milk
85gr sugar (Adjust to preferred sweetness)
50gr custard flour
2egg yolks
1teaspoon vanilla extract/rum extract
1tablepoon butter
A pinch of salt
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Method:
1. Dissolve custard powder in milk. Add sugar and salt.
2. Heat up in a saucepan over medium heat. Keep on stirring until frothy.
3. Take 1/3 of the mixtures to a bowl of egg yolks and combine well.
4. Pour step 3 mixtures back into the saucepan and keep on stirring until thickened and bubbled. Off the stove.
5. Add butter and vanilla/rum extract.
6. Cover with plastic if not immediately used. Before using, whisk again until smooth.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Assemble the tartlets and top them up with fresh fruits :) 🍓🍇🍒🥝🍉
Refrigerate before serving (optional)
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Enjoy!😋
