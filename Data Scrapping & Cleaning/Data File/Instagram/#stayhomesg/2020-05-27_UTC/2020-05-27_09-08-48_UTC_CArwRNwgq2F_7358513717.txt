Leatherjacket Chowder
[Ingredients]
- 500g leatherjacket fish @superseafoodsg
- 1 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp salt to marinate fish
- A dash freshly ground white pepper
- 1/4 cup sake
- 2 litres water
- 1 tbsp unrefined cooking oil
- 1 cup corn
- 1 carrot (diced)
- 1 large potato (chopped)
- 1 yellow onion (chopped)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- A few slices of ginger
- 1 tbsp cornstarch (mix w/ 2 tbsp water)
- A bunch of lettuce
[Steps]
- Defrost, clean, and marinate fish with sake, pepper, and 1/2 tsp salt. Remove fish bones if you prefer
- Heat pot with oil. SauteÃÅ onion, ginger, and garlic until onions becomes transparent
- Add corn, carrot, potato, and water. Bring to boil and simmer for 15 mins
-Add salt and fish including its marinate. Let simmer for 10 mins. Do not stir fish if your fish has bones. Gently flip it
- Add cornstarch mixture
- Add lettuce. Season with more salt or pepper if needed
- For your safety, serve fish separate from soup if your fish has bones in them
