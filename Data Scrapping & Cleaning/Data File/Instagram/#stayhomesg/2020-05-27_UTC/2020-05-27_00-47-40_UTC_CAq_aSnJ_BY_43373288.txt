For those of us looking for an easy way to make your own stylish and safe face mask, follow along with @mariechoo with this easy DIY tutorial—no sewing required! If you don’t have a buff or neck gaiter, all you need is any stretchy, breathable fabric that can wick sweat away, a pair of scissors, and you’re good to go.
