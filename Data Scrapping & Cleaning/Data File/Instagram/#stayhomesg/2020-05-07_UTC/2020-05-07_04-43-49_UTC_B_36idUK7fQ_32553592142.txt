1.	5 x WALL HANDSTAND HOPS ➡️ 20 x WALL MOUNTAIN CLIMBERS⠀
⠀
2.	REVERSE FORWARD FOLD TAPS: 10-15 REPS⠀
⠀
3.	SINGLE LEG LATERAL PLANK SLIDES: 10-20 REPS⠀
⠀
4.	KNEE TUCKS: 10 SLOW REPS⠀
⠀
5.	LOLASANA LIFTS: 10 REPS⠀
.⠀
☝🏼 Repeat 3-4 times... Remember quality over quantity, I’d rather you did 3 rounds with really strong technique than 4 where you cannot hold your shape safely 🤓⠀
