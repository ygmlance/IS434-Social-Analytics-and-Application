BAND BODYWEIGHT WORKOUT💪🏻🔥⠀
This full body, bodyweight, HIIT workout using a resistance band will keep you lean and toned (abs and butt focused). All of these movements can be done without the resistance band 💯⠀
•⠀
2-3 rounds of each Set / 30 seconds for each exercise / Rest at end of each Set

Set 1:
(Band above knees)

2-3x

Jump Squat w/ heel tap 30 sec •
•
(Tip: Jump doesn’t have to be too high, can also modify with a regular squat)

Lateral Steps 30 sec •
•
(Tip: Stay low, half squat, force your knees outward)

Side Plank w Abduction 30 sec •
•
(Tip: modify by holding a side plank for 30 seconds followed by abduction for 30 sec)

Set 2:
(No Band)

2-3x

Sumo Squat to Calf Raise 30 sec •
•
(Tip: feet wider than shoulder width, toes pointed at a 45 degree angle, squeeze your butt as you come up on your toes)

Reverse Lunge to Curtsy Lunge R 30 sec

Reverse Lunge to Curtsy Lunge L 30 sec •
•
(Tip: Stay low, try not to come up as you transition from the reverse Lunge to to the curtsy lunge, curtsy lunge is similar to the reverse lunge just at a 45 degree angle)

Dive Bomber Push Up 30 sec •
•
(Tip: use your momentum to push through the downward dog position into the push up)

Set 3:
(Band at feet)

2-3x

Half Dead Bug 30 sec •
•
(Tip: legs at 90 degree angle, drive your knee into your chest, keep your toes flexed upward to keep the band in place)

Crunches 30 sec •
•
(Tip: squeeze your stomach, push your hips into the ground)

Leg Raise w Abduction 30 sec •
•
(Tip: modify by placing your hands under your butt, extend legs as wide as you can at the end)

Abduction Pulse 30 sec •
