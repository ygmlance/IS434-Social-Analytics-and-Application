[Cafe Coco] - @cafecocosg celebrates their 1st anniversary with a series of bottled brews as part of their charity initiative to raise funds for Singapore Red Cross. Each box comes with: Creamy White Latte, Smooth Black Americano, and Cherry Blossom Latte (non-coffee) or the Popcorn Quarantini (alcoholic latte). 100% of the proceeds from the sales of their brews during 11 - 31 May will go to assisting local vulnerable elderly amid the COVID-19 outbreak.  #COCOgivesback
