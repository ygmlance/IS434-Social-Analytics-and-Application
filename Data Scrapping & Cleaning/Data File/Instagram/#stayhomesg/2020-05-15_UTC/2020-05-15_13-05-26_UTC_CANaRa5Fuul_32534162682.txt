⬇️⬇️⬇️ For 4-5 pax-
4 cups of Penne pasta
1 jar of tomato pasta sauce
1 big carrot (diced)
2 tomatoes (sliced) 
1 mini bowl of pork mincemeat
2 strips of parsley
1 bowl of salad leaves
Parmesan cheese (taste and moderation) 
Black pepper (taste and moderation) 
Salt (taste and moderation)
Parmesan cheese (taste and moderation) 
@jamieoliver 's Hot chilli pepper (taste and moderation)
3 cups of water
😋😋😋😋😋
𝗜𝗻𝘀𝘁𝗿𝘂𝗰𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻𝘀:
⬇️⬇️⬇️
---- PASTA ----
1. Boil penne pasta until cooked.
2. Drain the water and run it under cold water. 
3. Mix some olive oil around it to make it less sticky.
---- SAUCE ----
1. Heat up pan with oil on high heat. 
2. Sauté the diced carrots and sliced tomatoes until a bit soft. 
3. Add tomato pasta sauce and stir. 
4. Add 3 cups of water and stir. 
5. Add pork mincemeat and stir. 
6. Add some black pepper, salt and hot chilli pepper. Taste test and moderate if needed!
7. Add parsley and some parmesan cheese, and stir. 
8. Let it simmer under medium heat for about 10 mins. 
9. Ready to eat!🔥Can add more parmesan cheese and salad leaves!
