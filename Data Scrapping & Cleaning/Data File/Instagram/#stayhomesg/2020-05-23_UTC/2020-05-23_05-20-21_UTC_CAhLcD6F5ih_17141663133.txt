When in Singapore, cook as the Singaporeans do! Our Japanese colleague made yummy beehoon goreng and fried carrot cake for this week’s JCC kitchen! We’re delighted to see two popular local dishes make way into a Japanese kitchen! ⁣
⁣
⁣
For beehoon goreng, rice vermicelli was stir fried with garlic, onion, dried shrimps, sambal and spinach, then seasoned with salt and sugar, tomato ketchup, soy sauce, chili sauce and topped off with an egg omelette cut into strips.⁣
⁣
⁣
To make fried carrot cake, she stir fried ready made radish cake and eggs with garlic, sambal, soy sauce, sweet sauce, oyster sauce and topped off with spring onions.⁣
