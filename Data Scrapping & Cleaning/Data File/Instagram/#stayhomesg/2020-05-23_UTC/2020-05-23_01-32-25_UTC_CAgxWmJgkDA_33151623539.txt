#COLORFULL_GODDESS_DRESSING
Ingredients:
#Potato 🥔 #Carrot🥕 #Parsley 🍃#Peppermint #Coconut_Oil 🥥#Sugar #Salt 🧂 #Soya_Sauce #Ketchup #cumin_seed (optional)

Procedure:
1. Steam 1 potato and 1 carrot
2. Blend the cooked potato and carrot, add a handful parsley, peppermint, 3 full spoons of coconut oil, bit of sugar, seasalt, soya sauce, ketchup, blend to well mix and smooth.
