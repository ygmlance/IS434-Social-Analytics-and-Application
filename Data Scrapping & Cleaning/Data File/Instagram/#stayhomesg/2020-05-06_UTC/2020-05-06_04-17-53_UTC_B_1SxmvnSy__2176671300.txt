Ingredients: 
3 ripe bananas 
250g dried Organic Mulberries
340g Organic Baby Oats
80ml Organic Sunflower Oil

Preheat the oven to 180 deg Celsius. 
Mash the bananas. 
Mix all the ingredients in a bowl together. 
Put aside for 15 minutes. 
Drop teaspoonfuls onto a parchment paper / non-stick baking sheet. 
Place some chocolate chips on top of cookies (optional).
Bake for 20 minutes. 
Remove from the oven and allow to cool. 
Store in an airtight container, eat within 24 hours
