Newrecipe 24— Turkish bread with tawa paneer

That was a perfect dinner .. these Turkish breads are very very soft and fluffy even if it’s not hot. 🙌🏼🙌🏼 Must try once.
All u need is , 500 gms flour, 2 tsp dry yeast , 150 ml milk and 150 ml water . Mix well all together and let it sit for 1 hour. 
It will doubles up. 
Spread and knead , cut te dough into equal parts. And prepare like rotis. 
Enjoy it wit paneer it chicken .🍗 #foodie #newrecipes #cookinglove #circuitbreaker #stayhomesg
