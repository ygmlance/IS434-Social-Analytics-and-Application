"Masked Bike Portraits" by @motorgrapher .
Our creative spotlight falls on Barry Seah, aka The Motorgrapher, who presents this dramatic moto-portrait series inspired by Singapore's Circuit Breaker times. He says, "The headlights represent the hope that shines through past the masks and community restrictions. I chose four different genres of motorcycles to stand for the unity among Singapore's major races in the fight against the virus." Here's to staying inspired while we #stayhomesg !
