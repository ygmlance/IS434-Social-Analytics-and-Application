Let’s make some Organic Oatmeal Cookies using Nature’s Glory range of products. ⁣
⁣
Pompompurin Oatmeal Cookies (8 and 10 round cookies) ⁣
𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤 ⁣
100g  Nature’s Glory organic unbleached lite flour (plain flour)⁣
100g Nature’s Glory organic baby oats⁣
1 tsp baking soda⁣
60g sugar⁣
1/2 tsp salt⁣
1 egg (65g)⁣
40g melted butter⁣
20g oil⁣
⁣
𝕊𝕥𝕖𝕡𝕤 ⁣
1. Melt the butter on a stove. Set aside to cool.⁣
2. In a mixing bowl, add flour, oats, baking soda and mix. ⁣
3. In another mixing bowl, add egg, melted butter, sugar, salt and oil. Mix well.⁣
4. Add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Mix till combined.⁣
5. Shape the cookies.⁣
6. Bake it at 170C for 20 minutes at middle rack.⁣
7. Let cool.⁣
8. Melt some chocolate and draw the eyes, nose and caps of the Pompompurin.”
