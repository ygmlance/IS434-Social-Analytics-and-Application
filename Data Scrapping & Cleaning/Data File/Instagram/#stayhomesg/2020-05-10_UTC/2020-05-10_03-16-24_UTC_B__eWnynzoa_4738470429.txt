🍔Spinach & Cheese Stuffed Burger
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
📲𝐄𝐬𝐭𝐢𝐦𝐚𝐭𝐞𝐝 𝐦𝐚𝐜𝐫𝐨𝐬 with 200g patty ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ =60p 30c 40f⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ (Fats macro varies depending on ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ the choice of your meat) ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣𝐈𝐧𝐠𝐫𝐞𝐝𝐢𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐬: ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔x2 Burger buns ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔x1 tablespoon of grass-fed butter ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔200g of minced meat ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔x1 Egg yolk ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔x1 slice of cheese⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔Onions and lettuce⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔Spinach and garlic⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔Mustard + Sriracha sauce ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
✔Salt & pepper ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣𝐇𝐨𝐰 𝐭𝐨: ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
👉🏽1) Cook garlic and spinach with 1 teaspoon of olive oil ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
👉🏽2) Stuffed meat preparation: ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ a) Season 200g of minced meat ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ with salt, pepper, x1 egg yolk⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ and mix them well together. ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ b) Add cooked spinach and cheese ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ c) Roll the minced meat well to ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ cover up spinach and cheese⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ d) Cook stuffed minced meat with a ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ drop of olive oil⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ (3-4 minutes a side) ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
👉🏽3) 𝘉𝘶𝘯 𝘱𝘳𝘦𝘱𝘢𝘳𝘢𝘵𝘪𝘰𝘯:⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ Spread 1/2 tablespoon of grass-fed butter on each bun and toast it for 30s. ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
👉🏽4) 𝘍𝘪𝘯𝘢𝘭 𝘗𝘳𝘦𝘱𝘢𝘳𝘢𝘵𝘪𝘰𝘯: ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ a) Spread your favourite sauce on ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ the bun (In this video we used ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ ½ tbsp of mayonnaise mixed ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ with sriracha sauce) ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ b) Add lettuce and onions ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ c) Top it up with stuffed minced ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ meat ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ d) Spread your sauce on the patty ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ and add more onions ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ e) Cover it up with 2nd bun ⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣ and voila!⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
👉🏽5). Enjoy!!! 😋⁣⁣⁣⁣⁣
