Who We Are✨
We’re an initiative to promote local F&B businesses to help them through this tough period of the Covid-19 situation.
#SGLocalShops #stayhomesg #sgunited

What We Do✨
Our Telegram bot (link in bio) recommends you local F&B outlets near you and tells you how to contact them for orders! We believe in bringing convenience to you as you explore food options!

Through this, we hope that you, our Local Shoppers, will also help to support them by trying their delicacies! We will be sharing these merchants with you in the subsequent posts, so be sure to stay tuned for more!
