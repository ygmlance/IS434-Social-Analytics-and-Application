Melted cheese and ham on a buttered charcoal bread - what's more to say for breakfast? All you need is a cookie cutter to make this iconic Mickey head. 👉🏻 You can find our YouTube video link in bio.

Method
1️⃣ Cut out bread, cheese and ham with preferred cookie cutter
2️⃣ Butter the bread
3️⃣ Layer on the ingredients; cheese, ham and sliced tomatoes
4️⃣ Finish off with a slice of buttered bread
5️⃣ Pop into air fryer at 180 °C for 4 minutes

Ingredients
4 slices x Bread
4 slices x Cheese
2 slices x Honey Baked Ham
4  x Cherry Tomatoes
Pat.  x Butter
