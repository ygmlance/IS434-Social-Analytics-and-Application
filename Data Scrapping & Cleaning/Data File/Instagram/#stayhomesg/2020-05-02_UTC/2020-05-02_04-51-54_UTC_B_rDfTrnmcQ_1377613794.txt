02.05.2020 - Still in wraps.

Singapore's circuit breaker (stay home, work-from-home for non-essential business, no outside dining) came into effect on 07 April. It was initially till 04 May. 
On the 21 April, due to the raising Covid-19 cases (foreign workers' dormitories), the circuit breaker will continue till 01 June.
