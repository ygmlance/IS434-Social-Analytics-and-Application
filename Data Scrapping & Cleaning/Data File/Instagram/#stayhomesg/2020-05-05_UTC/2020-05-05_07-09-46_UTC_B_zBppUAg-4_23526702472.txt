We generally recommend drawers instead of swing cabinets for the kitchen base units.
.
Drawers, especially deep ones, provide a better user experience in the kitchen. This is especially so during the mise en place or when cooking. Whether it is taking out a pot to place at the stove or reaching for a sprinkle of salt - these are a hand's reach away.
