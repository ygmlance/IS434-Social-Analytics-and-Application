Silky smooth steam egg eggs with egg tofu & prawns. 蒸水蛋. 
Eggs to water 1:1.5 (in weight). Lay a layer of tofu on base of plate, sift in lightly beaten egg, cover with cellophane steam for 5min. Then add season (sesame oil, soya sauce, pepper, corn flour) prawns on TOP, steam another 2 min. Done. Serve with mixed soya sauce,water & sesame oil over over the TOP. Quick & easy. ========================
