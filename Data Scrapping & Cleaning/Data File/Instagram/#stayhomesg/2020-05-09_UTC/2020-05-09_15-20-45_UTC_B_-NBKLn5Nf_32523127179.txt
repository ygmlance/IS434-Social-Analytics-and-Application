Smoked Duck | Baby Spinach | Red Wine Vinaigrette 🥗🦆
.
🍴Overall ease: 10/10
.
🛒Ingredients: baby spinach, smoked duck (from @fassler_gourmet); for the dressing - evoo, red onion, mustard, red wine vinegar, honey.
.
👩🏻‍🍳Process: mix together the ingredients for dressing till it emulsifies, and lightly toss the salad leaves with it. Serve with smoked duck.
.
😋Taste: 9/10. Had smoked duck we wanted to use up, and this salad was the perfect opportunity! The dressing had a good balance of sour and sweet. A great appetiser / accompaniment to our seafood pasta.
