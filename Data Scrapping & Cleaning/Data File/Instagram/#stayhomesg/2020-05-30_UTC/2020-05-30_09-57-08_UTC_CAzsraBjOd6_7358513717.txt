Eggplant Tofu Lasagna
[Ingredients]
12 pieces lasagna pasta sheets
750g mushroom pasta sauce
500g zucchini
300g eggplant
1 large yellow onion (chopped)
1 tbsp olive oil
2 block pressed tofu
2 tsp salt
1 cup basil (chopped)
1 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
2 cups basic vegan cashew mozzarella* (optional)
[Steps]
-Preheat oven to 175C. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add noodles and cook just until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water
-Slice zucchinis (0.5cm thick) and cut eggplants into thick strips (5cm long)
-Heat olive oil and brown onions. Add eggplants and zucchini
-Stir in pasta sauce. Add 1 tsp salt. Cover and simmer on Low heat for 10 mins until eggplants soften. Turn off the heat and set aside
-Next, in a large bowl crumble tofu. Mix in 1 tsp salt and chopped basil. Set aside
-Prepare to layer with a 9x13 inch baking dish
-Spread about 1.5 cup layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of the baking dish. Place layer of pasta on top of the sauce
-Add a layer of sauce and top with a layer of tofu mixture. Repeat this step twice (3 layers of pasta layer)
-For the top layer (4th layer) of pasta, top off with pasta sauce without fillings and vegan c-she mozzarella
-Cover baking dish with foil and bake lasagna for 30 mins at 175C
-Uncover, increase oven temperature to 200C. Bake for another 15 mins
-Serve warm. Sprinkle with more basil or nutritional yeast flakes
*for instructions on how to make cashew mozzarella https://yupitsvegan.com/vegan-cashew-mozzarella/
