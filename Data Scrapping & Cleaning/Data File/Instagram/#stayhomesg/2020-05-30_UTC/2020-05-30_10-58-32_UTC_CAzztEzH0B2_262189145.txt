Vegetable salmon stew for dinner(2 person serving ) - @ann_journal , stir fry garlic & French onion till fragrant, add cubed carrots & potatoes stir fry. Add vegetable stock(400ml) or chicken cube stock + water (200ml), simmer for 25-30min till Veg soften. Add broccoli cook a few min. Add whip cream(100ml) & some cornstarch to thicken it. Add soft boiled egg (optional). Off fire. Add cube salmon. Cover lid for 5 min. Serve with bread or just eat as stew. ====================================
