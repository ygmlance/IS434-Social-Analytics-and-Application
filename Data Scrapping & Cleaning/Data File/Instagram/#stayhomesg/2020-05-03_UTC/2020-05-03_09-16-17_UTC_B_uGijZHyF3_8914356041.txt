SOUP OF DAY
🍲 Cabbage Tofu Chicken Soup
1/4 - 1/2 portion chicken
150g round cabbage, cut into pieces
1 small carrot, cut into chunks
5 red dates
1 big dry scallop
3 slices ginger
1 pack silken tofu (optional), cut into cubes
1.2L water
.
Method
1. Blanch chicken (or replace with 1 or 2 whole chicken bone frame), rinse and set aside
2. Bring 1.2L water to boil add in all ingredients except cabbage and tofu
3. When mixture comes to boil, lower the heat and simmer for 15 minutes on low heat
4. Add in cabbage and continue to cook for another 10 minutes or until cabbage is soft to your liking
5. Lastly add in silken tofu and season to taste before serving
.
.
📝 note
- you can use pre-cooked chicken stock or packed chicken broth to cut short the cooking time
- reheat the stock,  add carrot and cabbage then simmer for 15 minutes before adding tofu or fishballs
.

MAIN
🍗Roast Chicken Rice
Marinate the chicken legs overnight then air-fried until crips and golden brown before serving with fragrant chicken rice cooked with @primataste chicken rice paste and blue peas flower infused rice
.
Ingredients
2 chicken legs with thighs
1 tbsp cooking wine
1 tbsp honey 
1 tbsp light soy sauce
1 tbsp prima taste chicken rice paste
1/2 tsp sesame oil
1/4 tsp 5 spice powder, optional
1/8 tsp dark soy sauce
1/8 tsp ground pepper
.
Method
1. Oven baked or air-fried thaw (5-10 minutes before cooking) chicken legs in 170 degree for 15 minutes
2. Flip over then for another 6-8 minutes on 180 degree till cooked through
.
.

- next remove 2-3 tbsps of rice to a bowl

- soak at least 30 mins to an hour
