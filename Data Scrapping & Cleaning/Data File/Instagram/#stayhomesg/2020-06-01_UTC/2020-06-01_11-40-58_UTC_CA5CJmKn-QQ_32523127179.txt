Pandan chicken 🐓
.
🍴Ease of cooking: 7/10
.
🛒Ingredients: chicken (remove bones), oyster sauce, bbq sauce/ sesame oil, soya sauce, pounded ginger & onion, garlic, pandan leaves
.
👩🏻‍🍳Process:
1. Marinate the chicken with the above condiments to your preferred taste profile
2. Stir fry the chicken with garlic for about a minute and leave to cool
3. Wrap the chicken up in pandan leaves
4. Deep fry the chicken until it turns golden brown
.
😋Taste: 7/10. Tasty and tender chicken meat. However, it could do with more pandan flavour so we’d probably add some pandan juice to the marinade in the future
