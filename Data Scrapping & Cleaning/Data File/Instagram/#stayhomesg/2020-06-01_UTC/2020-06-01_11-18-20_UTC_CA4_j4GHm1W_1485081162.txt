Bak Chor Mee

Bak Chor Mee / Mee pok is a noodle dish with Chinese noodle characterized by its flat and yellow appearance, varying in thickness and width. The dish is of Teochew origin.

This home made version using Mee Kia ( thin egg yellow noodles )
