Single mother of three Tanti Yani Zaini, 33, lost her job as a zone coordinator for an information technology company in early March, just as the #covid19 pandemic began to escalate in Singapore. 
Ms Tanti has been staying at her brother’s home in Woodlands since the incident happened and said that she was still traumatised by the situation. “But I feel blessed to see the amount of love and blessings I have been receiving. There are no words to describe how I feel. The community has come forward to provide so much help,” she said referring to the outpouring of donations and offers of assistance from members of the public. #linkinbio to read more.
