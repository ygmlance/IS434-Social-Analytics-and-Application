The Ministry of Health (MOH) announced 1,426 new cases on April 20 bringing the total number of #covid19 infections in Singapore to 8,014.⁣
⁣
1,369 cases are those who are residing in the dormitories.⁣
⁣
There were 25 cases in the community with 18 Singaporeans or Permanent Residents, and 7 work pass holders.⁣
⁣
MOH said that the number of new cases in the community has decreased, from an average of 39 cases per day in the week before, to an average of 29 per day in the past week.⁣
