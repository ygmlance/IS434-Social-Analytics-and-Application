“I can still see maids walking their dogs together and people running without masks from our hotel window,” Mr Chris Tong, 34, told @todayonline by telephone from the Regent Hotel in Cuscaden Road where is serving 14 days of isolation. Mr Tong is dismayed and in disbelief at the scenes outside the window of the hotel. It is a far cry from the empty streets he observed outside his apartment in Wuhan.⁣
