The Ministry of Health on Saturday (April 25) announced 618 new cases of Covid-19, bringing the total number of infections here to 12,693.⁣
⁣
This is the second day in a row where the daily tally is under 1,000, after four straight days where the number of new cases was above the mark.⁣
⁣
The majority of the new patients are work permit holders residing in dormitories housing migrant workers.⁣
⁣
Of the new cases, 7 cases are Singaporeans or Permanent Residents.⁣
