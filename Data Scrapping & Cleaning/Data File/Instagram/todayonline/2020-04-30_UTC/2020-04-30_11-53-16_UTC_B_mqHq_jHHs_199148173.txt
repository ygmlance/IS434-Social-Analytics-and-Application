In a Facebook post, McDonald’s announced that it will not resume operations on May 5, as earlier proposed, and its outlets will remain closed for “just a little longer”.⁣
⁣
The fast-food chain said this decision had been taken for the “safety and well-being of our employees and customers”, though it did not specify how much longer its outlets would remain shuttered.⁣
