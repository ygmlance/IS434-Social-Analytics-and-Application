Thailand has loosened restrictions on some businesses after progress in containing the coronavirus.⁣
⁣
Ms Wannika Naphon, happy to be getting out for a meal for the first time in weeks, said she was confident businesses would be careful with distancing.⁣
⁣
"I'm sure Yaowarat will return to normal with a lot of tourists soon," she said, referring to the street by which Bangkok's Chinatown is known.⁣
