As Singapore battles the #covid19 pandemic, TODAY’s #heroesunmasked series highlights those who are doing their bit to spread kindness and compassion during this period. In this instalment, we speak to five junior college graduates who provide tuition in exchange for donations to charities and non-profit organisations.⁣
⁣
The five teenagers launched Project Circuit, a website where students taking their A-Level examinations this year can register for free tuition lessons from former junior college students — as long as they “pay it forward” with a donation of at least S$20 to a charity or non-profit organisation.⁣
