Have you seen Spot around? You might hear it before you see it.

The four-legged robot has been deployed on a two-week trial at the park and will broadcast a recorded message to remind park visitors to observe safe distancing. It will also be fitted with cameras to estimate the number of visitors at the park, though the authorities said these cameras will not be able to track or recognise specific individuals or collect personal data.⁣
