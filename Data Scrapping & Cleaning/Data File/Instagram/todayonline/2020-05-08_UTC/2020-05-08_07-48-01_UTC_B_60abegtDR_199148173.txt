Singapore has confirmed 768 new cases of Covid-19, said the Ministry of Health on Friday (May 8).⁣
⁣
This brings the total number of cases here to 21,707.⁣
⁣
The vast majority of the new cases are work permit holders residing in foreign worker dormitories. ⁣
⁣
10 cases are Singaporeans and Permanent Residents.⁣
