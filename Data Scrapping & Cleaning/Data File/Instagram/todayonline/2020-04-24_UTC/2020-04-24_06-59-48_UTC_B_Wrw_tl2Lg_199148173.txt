A site at Tanjong Pagar Terminal is being set aside to make space for a Covid-19 isolation facility should the need arise, the authorities said.⁣
⁣
This is in addition to existing community isolation facilities at D’Resort NTUC in Pasir Ris, the Singapore Expo Convention Hall and Exhibition Centre and, soon, the Changi Exhibition Centre. ⁣
