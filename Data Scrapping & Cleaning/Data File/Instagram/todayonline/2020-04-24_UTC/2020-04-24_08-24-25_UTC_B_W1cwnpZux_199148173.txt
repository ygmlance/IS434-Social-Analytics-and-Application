Tay Chun Hsien was charged on Friday (April 24) under the Infectious Diseases Act with leaving his place of isolation while under a quarantine order.⁣⁣
⁣⁣
Tay allegedly left his Chua Chu Kang flat between 11.30am and 12pm on March 22, just as his quarantine order was about to end, according to court documents. ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
If convicted, Tay could be jailed up to six months, fined up to S$10,000, or both.⁣⁣
