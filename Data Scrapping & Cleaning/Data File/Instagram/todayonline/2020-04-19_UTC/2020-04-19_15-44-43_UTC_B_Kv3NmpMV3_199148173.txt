Some essential workers over the weekend had little choice but to ignore safe distancing markers on the train as the authorities’ move to reduce the frequency of services resulted in some relatively crowded trains.

A social distancing ambassador, whose job is to ensure that people keep a 1m distance from one another, told @todayonline that it was “quite difficult” to strictly adhere to safe distancing rules when she travelled to work at about 9am.
