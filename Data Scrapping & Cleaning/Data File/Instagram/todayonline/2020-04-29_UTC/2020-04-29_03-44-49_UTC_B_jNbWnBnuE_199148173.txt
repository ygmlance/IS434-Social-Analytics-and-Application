Exercising and dog walking are not permitted within the common areas of condominiums as part of the circuit breaker measures to help curb the spread of Covid-19, said the Building and Construction Authority (BCA). ⁣
⁣
Residents may exercise or walk their dogs in public areas that are freely accessible, but not in condominiums' common areas, added the BCA.⁣
