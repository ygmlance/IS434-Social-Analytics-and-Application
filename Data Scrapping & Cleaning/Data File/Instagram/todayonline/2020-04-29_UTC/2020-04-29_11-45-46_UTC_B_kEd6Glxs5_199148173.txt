“I keep wondering about what could have happened. Was it because of the virus? How did he end up (at the staircase)? Did he fall because he fainted? Until now, I cannot conceive how he died,” said Ms A Panjali, the wife of the late Alagu Periyakarrupan.⁣
⁣
He was found motionless at a staircase landing of Khoo Teck Puat Hospital and Ms Panjali had no clue that her husband had even been hospitalised for Covid-19 until she was informed of his death last Thursday (April 23).⁣
