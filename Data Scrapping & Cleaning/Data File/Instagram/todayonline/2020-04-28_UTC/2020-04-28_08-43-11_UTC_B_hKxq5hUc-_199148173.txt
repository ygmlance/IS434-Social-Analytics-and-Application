Singapore has confirmed 528 new cases of Covid-19, said the Ministry of Health on Tuesday (April 28).⁣
⁣
This brings the total number of cases here to 14,951.⁣
⁣
The vast majority of the new cases are work permit holders residing in foreign worker dormitories.⁣
⁣
8 cases are Singaporeans and Permanent Residents.⁣
