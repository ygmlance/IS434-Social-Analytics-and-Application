The easing of the circuit breaker measures will pave the way for certain businesses to resume operations and small groups of students to return for face-to-face lessons, a multi-ministry task force dealing with the Covid-19 outbreak said on Saturday (May 2).⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣
The measures are being eased as community transmission has decreased significantly over the past month, but National Development Minister Lawrence Wong, who co-chairs the task force, said that Singaporeans must stay disciplined and vigilant.⁣⁣⁣
