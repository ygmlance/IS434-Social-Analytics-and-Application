The Government will provide a one-off sum of S$100 to each household with at least one Singaporean citizen to help them to offset their utility bills. ⁣
⁣
This credit will be given out to households living in all property types and will be credited in either the July or August utilities bill.⁣
⁣
The utility rebate is part of the support measures rolled out for households under the S$33 billion Fortitude Budget announced by Deputy Prime Minister Heng Swee Keat in Parliament on Tuesday (May 26). ⁣
