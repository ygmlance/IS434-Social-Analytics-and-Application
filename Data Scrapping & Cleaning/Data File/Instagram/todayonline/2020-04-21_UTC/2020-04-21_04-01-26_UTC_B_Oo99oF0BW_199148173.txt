The superhero quintet, dubbed the "Virus Vanguard", were meant to galvanise the country in its battle against the Covid-19 pandemic. However, they were grounded not long after their public debut drew mixed reactions. ⁣
⁣
“We have received quite a lot of feedback on the characters and we will be reviewing them. We are sorry if we offended anyone,” stated @gov.sg in a Facebook post. “As this is the first time we are exploring this content format, we appreciate your patience.”⁣
