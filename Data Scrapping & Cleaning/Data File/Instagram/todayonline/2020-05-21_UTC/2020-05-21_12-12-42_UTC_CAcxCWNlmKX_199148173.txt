When @todayonline arrived at Geylang Serai market at 8am, there were snaking queues at both its entrances. The line spanned across four rows, with about 20 people in each row.⁣
⁣
Twelve safe distancing ambassadors — some wearing a dark blue Certis Cisco lanyard — were seen ushering shoppers to the back of the line, which was moving swiftly.⁣
⁣
One of them, who declined to be named, said that he has seen the number of people flocking to the market double in the last two weeks leading up to Hari Raya.⁣
