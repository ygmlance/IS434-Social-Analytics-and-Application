As Singapore battles the Covid-19 outbreak, TODAY’s #heroesunmasked series highlights those who are doing their bit to spread kindness and compassion during this period.

In this instalment, we speak to Dr Skye Yeo who started a project to give out 3,000 portable MP3 players to elderly individuals from underprivileged backgrounds to brighten up their days during the circuit breaker period.
