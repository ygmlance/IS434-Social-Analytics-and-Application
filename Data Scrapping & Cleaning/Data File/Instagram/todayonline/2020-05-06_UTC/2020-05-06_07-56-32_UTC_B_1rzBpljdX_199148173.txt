Singapore has confirmed 788 new cases of Covid-19, said the Ministry of Health on Wednesday (May 6).⁣
⁣
This brings the total number of cases here to 20,198.⁣
⁣
The vast majority of the new cases are work permit holders residing in foreign worker dormitories.⁣
⁣
11 cases are Singaporeans and Permanent Residents.⁣
