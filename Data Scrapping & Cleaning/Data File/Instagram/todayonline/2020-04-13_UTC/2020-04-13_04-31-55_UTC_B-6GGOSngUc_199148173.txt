Few would have rated her chances of survival when 97-year-old Brazilian Gina Dal Colleto was hospitalised on April 1 with coronavirus symptoms.⁣
⁣
But survive she did, and her unexpected recovery has become a ray of hope in Brazil, where the coronavirus has laid bare a stretched public health system and exposed fierce political debate over how to best tackle the virus' spread and prop up the country's economy. Brazil has reported 1,223 deaths and 22,169 confirmed cases of #covid19 as of Sunday (April 12).⁣
