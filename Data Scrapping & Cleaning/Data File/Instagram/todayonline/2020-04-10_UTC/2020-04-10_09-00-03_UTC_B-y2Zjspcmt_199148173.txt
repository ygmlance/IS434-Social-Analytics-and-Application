As Singapore battles the Covid-19 outbreak, TODAY's Heroes Unmasked series highlights those who are doing their bit to spread kindness and compassion during this period. 
In this instalment, we speak to the non-profit group Engineering Good, which aims to make sure students from low-income families have a laptop to use for home-based learning. Started in 2014 by a group of engineers, Engineering Good has been empowering disadvantaged people in the community, by providing them with access to technology.

They have received more than 190 donated laptops from Singaporeans since Sunday (April 5), and the numbers continue to rise. #linkinbio to read more. #covid19 #heroesunmasked #singaporenews
