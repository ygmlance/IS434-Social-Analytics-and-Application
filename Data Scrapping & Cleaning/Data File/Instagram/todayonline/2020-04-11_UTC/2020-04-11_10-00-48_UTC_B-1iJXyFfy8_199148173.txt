Amid the Covid-19 outbreak, many employees in Singapore have seemingly been liberated from the conventional office space, especially after the Government’s “circuit breaker” measures. ⁣
⁣
No more jostling with the rush-hour crowd, having time to do laundry or even being able to attend virtual meetings in pyjamas are welcomed by many as perks of #wfh .⁣
⁣
