Petai with Mince Meat RECIPE
200gm petai (stink beans)
200gm minced meat (pork or chicken)
1 tsp Sinlong Sambal Balacan (or 1tbsp if you like it to be spicy) 
1 large clove garlic
¾ tsp salt
1 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp light soy sauce
1 tsp corn starch
1/3 cup wate
Some cooking oil

1. Heat up the wok with cooking oil.
3. Put in chopped garlic and sauté until fragrance. 
4. Put in minced meat and cook until it turn opaque, put in salt.
5. Continue to cook until the meat is no longer releasing juices, but seem to be drying up.
6. Turn heat to medium and put in Sinlong Sambal Balacan, sugar and soy sauce and cook until the meat seems dry and fragrant.
7. Put in split petai beans and cook for 20- 30 seconds.
8. Mix cornstarch with water and pour in bit by bit, stir and cook until the liquid is all absorbed.
