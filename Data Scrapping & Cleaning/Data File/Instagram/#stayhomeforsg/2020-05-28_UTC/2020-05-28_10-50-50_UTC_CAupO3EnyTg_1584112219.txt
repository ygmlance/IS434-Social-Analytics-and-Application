Recipe for Gong Bao Prawns .
Ingredients

300 g prawns (shelled)
3 tablespoons cooking oil
8 dried chili pepper, soak for half hr, cut into half
1 teaspoon Sichuan peppercorn
3 garlic cloves, chopped 
1 thumb-size old ginger, sliced
1/2 green pepper, cut into small pieces 
3 stalks spring onion (white part)  sauce

1 teaspoon dark soy sauce
1 tablespoon light soy sauce
1 tablespoon black vinegar
2 teaspoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon sesame oil
3 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon sugar

Prawns Marinate

1 teaspoon sea salt
Dash of pepper 
Cooking steps 
Wash and clean the prawns. Marinate the prawns with white pepper and seasalt. 
Mix all the ingredients for sauce in a small bowl.

Heat oil in a pan and fry ginger, garlic, Sichuan peppercorn and dried chili pepper until aromatic. Then add the prawns in. Fry for 20 seconds over high fire.

Add green peppers and spring onions. Continue frying until the peppers are slightly soft (around 20 seconds). Pour in the sauce mixture and fry till sauce thickens. Off fire and serve on the plate.
