Airfry Frozen Marbled Beef Ribeye with Fresh Rosemary. ⁣
(Just realize this is a meltique beef whereby they actually inject vegetable oil to introduce more fat to the meat aka larding/ pique (French). Beef from Australia. )

No oily kitchen recipe. ⁣
Ingredients:⁣
200g Marbled Ribeye⁣
Pink salt⁣
Black pepper⁣
Fresh rosemary ⁣
⁣
⁣
Instructions: ⁣
1. Season ribeye with salt and pepper. ⁣
2. Place it inside a glassware with steamer rack. (Shown on photo) ⁣
3. Place rosemary on top of ribeye. ⁣
4. Airfry 200 degree for about 8 mins to 10 mins for medium rare. (No need to flip for 200g)⁣
