Mushroom chicken soup + rice for dinner today! Mmmmm... This bowl of soup smells so good! Yummy yum yum! 😋

𝐼𝓃𝑔𝓇𝑒𝒹𝒾𝑒𝓃𝓉𝓈
- 1/4 chicken, cut into pieces
- 500ml of water
- 1 chicken stock cube
- 1 small onion, thinly sliced
- 4 pine mushrooms, cut into pieces
- 2 teaspoons of @liloikanbilis mushroom powder

𝐼𝓃𝓈𝓉𝓇𝓊𝒸𝓉𝒾𝑜𝓃𝓈
1. Pour in 500ml of water into the pot and start boiling.
2. Add in 1 chicken stock cube, 1/4 chicken, onion slices and pine mushroom slices into the boiling water. Continue to cook for another 30 mins.
3. Add in 2 teaspoons of @liloikanbilis mushroom powder and do some stirring. Continue to cook for another 5 mins.

Dinner is ready to be served! This is very simple to prepare, you can give it a try!
