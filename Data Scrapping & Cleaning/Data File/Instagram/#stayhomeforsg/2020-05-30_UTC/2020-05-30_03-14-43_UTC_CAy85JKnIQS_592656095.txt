防疫时期学会几件事 （一）

身体不动会更累，无法自由外出的日子也要尽量多运动。就算短短半小时，持之以恒也会有效果。

Online HIIT & Zumba classes have accompanied me during the circuit breaker period. Morning jogs around the neighbourhood have been surprisingly refreshing too. Unknowingly, we have all learned to adapt to new norms but good habits should stay after we enter post circuit breaker days.
