Breakfast 👨‍👩‍👧☀️🍴 with my LOVE 🥰! Stove Top Pita Frittata Burger 🔥🥯 with Blueberries 😋. Wasn't in time for @missionfoods.sg contest 🌟, but it's ok 👌, we still need to eat anyway 😊.⠀
⠀
📝 Ingredients:⠀
✔️ Pita Bread 🥯 x1⠀
✔️ Eggs 🥚 x2⠀
💎 KW Powder: Garlic 🧄 1/2 tsp⠀
✔️ Fresh Milk 🥛 2 🥄🥄 Tbsp⠀
✔️ Organic Pine Nuts, a handful⠀
✔️ Italian Herbs or Parsley 🌿⠀
✔️ Fillings: Organic Cherry Tomatoes & Organic Long Beans from @qforganic, Black Olives, Ham, a handful finely diced 🔪⠀
⠀
💡 For the fillings, you may add in any ingredients of your choice.⠀
⠀
💡 For NO MEAT ❌🐔 picky eater 🧒, you may add 1/2 tsp of 💎 KW Powder: SC/ *PC/ *AC/ ***BC.⠀
⠀
👩‍🍳 How to Cook?⠀
1. Crack Eggs into a mixing bowl, add in ingredients, mix 💫 well until all powder is dissolved.⠀
2. Heat a nonstick pan, add a little Butter & Oil, pour in egg mixture.⠀
3. Slice 🔪🥯 Pita Bread into 2 pieces (like a burger), place 1 piece on the egg mixture, press to bond them together, continue to cook for 1 to 2 minutes, flip to another side, brown slightly then transfer to serving plate.⠀
4. Melt a little Butter, place in another piece of Pita bread to warm it, optional to spread with Cream Cheese or Mayonnaise.⠀
5. Form a Pita Frittata Burger, cut 🔪 into quarters. Enjoy!⠀
