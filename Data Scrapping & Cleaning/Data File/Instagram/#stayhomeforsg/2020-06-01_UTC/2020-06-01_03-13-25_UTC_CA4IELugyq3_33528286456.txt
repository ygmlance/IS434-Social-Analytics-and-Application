Enoki Tempura with Spicy Mayo 🍄🌶 .
Ingredients:
Enoki Mushrooms
Tempura Flour
Mayonnaise 
Siracha Sauce
.
*Dredge mushrooms in tempura, and bring to fry till golden. Blot with kitchen towels before serving.
*For the sauce, simply mix mayonnaise with Siracha Sauce. 
Easy Snack! 😋
