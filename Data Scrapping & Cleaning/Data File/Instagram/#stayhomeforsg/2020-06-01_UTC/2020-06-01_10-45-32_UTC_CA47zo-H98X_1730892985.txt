Resistance band workout - Pt 1! 
7 quick moves to tone up your thighs and glutes:
1. 20 side leg raises (10/side)
2. 20 x jumps
3. 20 squats
4. 20 jump jacks
5. 20 donkey kicks (10/side)
6. 20 fire hydrants (10/side)
7. 20 in and out squats 
Repeat 2-4 rounds

The last one had my legs trembling like jelly but a workout’s only completed when you finish it 💪🏼
