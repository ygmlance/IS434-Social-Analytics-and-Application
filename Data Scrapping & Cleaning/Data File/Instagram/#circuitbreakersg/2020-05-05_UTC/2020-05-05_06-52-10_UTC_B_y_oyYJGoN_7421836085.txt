Today let's talk about Colours. Does colour make a different in Feng Shui?

In Feng Shui, colour only contributes to a tiny percentage of the whole outcome. With means, colour does not make a vast difference. 
Our advice when choosing colour for Feng Shui is to go for a colour that is easy on the eyes and avoid using very strong and bold colour.
