Rice Cooker Long Bean Rice (长豆饭) for dinner few days back. Marinated the pork belly with soy sauce, sesame oil and white pepper for 30 minutes or more and add in long bean to stir-fry. Transfer the stir-fried ingredients to the rice cooker and cook together with the rice. Taste the rice and adjust seasonings according to your preference. Close the cover and let the rice rest for about 10 minutes before serving. #hzcooks #ricecookerrecipe
