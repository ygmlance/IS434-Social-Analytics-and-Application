Circuit Breaker Day 29 // Added some Korean twist to Mr Bear's lunchbox today. Pan fried pork belly served with soba noodles, kimchi, blanched cabbage (hidden underneath the noodles), butterhead lettuce and hard boiled egg.
.
Marinated the pork belly overnight. For 130g of meat I added 1 tbsp of apple puree, 1 clove grated garlic, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp mirin and 1 tsp wine white vinegar. Drizzle a little oil in the pan and fry for about 2-3 minutes on each side depending on how thick the cut is.  Wrap it with lettuce and kimchi to enjoy it the Korean way.
.
