Here is the recipe: (reference from Spice N’ Pans with some modifications) ⁣
⁣
𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤:⁣
Serves 4 to 6 pax ⁣
1kg prime pork ribs⁣
16 cloves of garlic – remove skin but keep every clove as a whole⁣
1 tablespoon of sugar⁣
6 slices of ginger⁣
4 tablespoons of oyster sauce ⁣
2 tablespoon of dark soya sauce ⁣
2 tablespoon of light soya sauce ⁣
1 liter of water or enough to cover the pork ribs.⁣
4 tablespoon of chinese cooking wine (shaoxing)⁣
2 teaspoon of sesame oil.⁣
⁣
𝕊𝕥𝕖𝕡𝕤:⁣
1. Use a millet to pound the pork ribs to tenderize it. ⁣
2. Pour oyster sauce, dark sauce and light sauce into a bowl and set aside.⁣
3. Heat up a frying pan or pot with some oil. ( I use @lecruset.sg pot) ⁣
4. Brown the garlic and ginger. Set aside.⁣
5. Brown the pork ribs.⁣
6. Once the ribs are brown, add in the sugar.⁣
7. Add in oyster sauce,dark sauce and light sauce.⁣
8. Add in water, just enough to cover the ribs.⁣
9. Add in the garlic and ginger.⁣
10. Simmer for 40 minutes.⁣
11. After 40 minutes, the gravy would have dry up. Add in sesame oil and chinese wine.⁣
12. Sprinkles sesame seeds and spring onions and serve. ⁣
