INGREDIENTS⁣
▫️3 cloves garlic, minced⁣
▫️Seafood broth (see below)⁣
▫️Rice vermicelli (beehoon)⁣
▫️Prawns⁣
▫️Scallops⁣
▫️Fish slices⁣
▫️Green vegetables⁣
▫️2 eggs, beaten ⁣
⁣
Seafood broth⁣
▫️Homemade chicken stock⁣
▫️Prawns @9sseafood , deveined and cleaned⁣
▫️Fish @9sseafood , sliced⁣
▫️Scallops @9sseafood ⁣
▫️ @liloikanbilis scallop powder⁣
⁣
INSTRUCTIONS ⁣
1️⃣Boil the chicken stock together with prawns, fish and some scallops. Add scallop powder. Do not cook the prawns too Long. Dish out and set aside.⁣
⁣
2️⃣Heat up cooking oil and pan fried the scallops until lightly browned. Dish and set aside.⁣
⁣
3️⃣Sauté garlic until fragrant. Pour in the seafood broth and bring to boil.⁣
⁣
4️⃣When it has boiled, add the rice vermicelli. Allow the vermicelli to cook in the soup stock until they has softened.⁣
⁣
5️⃣Add eggs and scramble it.⁣
⁣
6️⃣Add green vegetables.⁣
⁣
7️⃣When vegetable has softened, add in cooked prawns, scallops and fish sliced. Mix well. ⁣
⁣
8️⃣Serve hot immediately ⁣
