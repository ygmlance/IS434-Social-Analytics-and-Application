PBJ chia pudding .
🍓super easy
🍓 5min prep time
🍓 protein packed
Ingredients: [1 serving]
🥥 1/2 cup coconut milk (or equal parts coconut cream and water)
🤎 2 tbsp chia seeds .
🥜 peanut butter .
🍓 strawberry jam
> mix chia and coconut milk in a jar and store it in your fridge
> wake up to a nice thick chia pudding
> layer it with your favourite jam and pb > top w a dollop of coconut cream
> dig in 😋
