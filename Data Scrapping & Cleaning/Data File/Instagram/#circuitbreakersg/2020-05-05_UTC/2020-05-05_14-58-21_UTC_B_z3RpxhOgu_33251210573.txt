Meet Dr Anand. He graduated from Grant Medical College (India) in 2009. Since graduating, he has worked in various government aided hospitals in Singapore like CGH, KTPH, TTSH, NUH and gained valuable experience in multiple specialties.

Dr Anand was subsequently selected into Family Medicine Residency training program under National Healthcare Group in 2013. During his residency, he rotated in a number of departments, such as General Medicine, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Paediatric Medicine, Geriatric Medicine, Emergency Medicine, General Surgery, Orthopaedics, Psychiatry, ENT, Ophthalmology and Dermatology.

He has practiced at Jurong polyclinic and many private medical group clinics for several years; gaining extensive clinical experience in Family Medicine.
