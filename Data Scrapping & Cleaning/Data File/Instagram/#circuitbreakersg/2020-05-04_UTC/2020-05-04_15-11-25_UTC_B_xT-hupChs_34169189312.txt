Satisfied our cravings for Japanese udon with this easy stir-fry curry udon! 🍥🙌🏻
Chop up some potatoes, carrots and tofu and let them simmer in the pan with Japanese curry paste (+ chilli padi as we like it extra hot 🌶😌) till soft and add in udon towards the end! Serve them up with edamame for some extra greens 🌱
