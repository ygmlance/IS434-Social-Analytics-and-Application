[Recipe] Homemade OmniMeat Meat Balls for the kids’ snack time this afternoon in support of Green Monday to go plant-based on Mondays and reduce carbon footprint.
.

Green Monday is a diverse social venture promoting nutritious, sustainable and innovative plant-based dining to tackle climate change, food insecurity, health issues and animal welfare.
.

OmniMeat is developed by Right Treat, a food tech company launched by Green Monday. It comprises of a proprietary blend of shitake mushroom, pea, Non-GMO soy and rice, delivering 12.5g of protein per serving. It’s a very versatile and nutritious meat suitable for all types of cooking, great alternative to replace minced meat. .
.

OmniMeat, the all-purpose and halal-certified plant protein, is available for purchase at FairPrice, Sheng Siong, HAO Mart, Prime, Mahota, Yes Natural, Four Seasons Organic Market and Redmart.
.

Here’s sharing the recipe:
.

1 pack 230g Omni Meat 
3 tbsp bread crumbs
7g finely chopped fresh chinese parsley 
10g minced garlic 
1 small egg
1 tsp sesame oil 
1/4 tsp salt 
1/4 tsp white pepper .

𝐌𝐞𝐭𝐡𝐨𝐝𝐬:.
.
1. In a big bowl, mix all ingredients together. Stir in clockwise directions and mixed until well combined.
.

2. Roll into balls and baked in a preheated oven at 200°C for 15-20 mins or until golden brown.
*Note: You may pan-fry it if you have no oven at home. .
