The dish of the hour was made by the hubs - Phat Kaphrao (Thai Beef Mince with Basil & Chilli)

Ingredients: 
600g beef mince
4 garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 onion, sliced
1 red chilli, sliced
1 cup Thai basil leaves (I got mine from @sgtastebuds , great price, super fresh) 
2 tbsp. vegetable oil
2 tbsp. fish sauce
2 tbsp. light soya sauce
2 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. dried chilli flakes (optional) 
Method:
Mix fish sauce, soya sauce, sugar and chilli flakes
.
In a hot pan add beef & garlic and fry. Abt 30sec later add onions and fry till beef is cooked.
.
Add sauces and fry till fully absorbed. .
Lastly add the Chillies and basil and fry till well mixed. Serve immediately.
.
Served it together with
Asparagus and Mushrooms
Tom Yum Barramundi Fish Belly from @kuhlbarra 
Store-bought Pandan Chicken
.
