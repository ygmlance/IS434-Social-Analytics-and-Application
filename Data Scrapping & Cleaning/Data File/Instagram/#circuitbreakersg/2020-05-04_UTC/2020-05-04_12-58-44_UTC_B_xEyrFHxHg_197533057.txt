This recipe makes 12 tartlets in muffin tins

What you need
👉🏼150g fresh spinach
👉🏼1 medium broccoli 🥦
👉🏼2 medium eggs
👉🏼100g grated chili cheddar(plain will work)
👉🏼1 tbsp basil pesto
👉🏼1 large puff pastry sheet (I used @jus_rol )
👉🏼1 pinch of nutmeg
👉🏼1 tsp ground black pepper
👉🏼1 tsp grated garlic
👉🏼Olive oil & Salt
👉🏼Cubes or feta (optional)

How to whip it up:
✅Cut thé broccoli into florets, toss in olive oil and salt , pepper and garlic. Roast for 15-20 mins at 175°C. Set aside to cool
✅Wash and dry the spinach leaves and pinch into smaller bits with your hand. Cook lightly in olive oil till slightly wilted.
✅In a large mixing bowl beat two eggs with a pinch of nutmeg, one tbsp of pesto, splash of milk. Now add the grated cheddar and mix well.
✅Add thé slightly cooled spinach and broccoli to make the filling
✅Thaw thé frozen puff pastry and then use a cookie cutters to make circles to fit a muffin pan.
✅layer the muffin tins with a strip of baking paper , followed by puff pastry circles and spoon a tbsp of the filling
✅Optional step: top with a cube of feta each
✅Bake for 13-15 minutes at 180°C in a fan forced convection setting. Cool and savor hot!
