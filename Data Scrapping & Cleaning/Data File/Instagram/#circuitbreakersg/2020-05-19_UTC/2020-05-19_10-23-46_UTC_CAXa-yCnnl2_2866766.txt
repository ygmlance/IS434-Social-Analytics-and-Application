~Salt & Pepper Calamari~
.
I use frozen squid 🦑 (clean & cut, for my convenience), bought from @theladyboymarketplace.
.
Ingredients:
- 600gr squid
- 10 cloves of garlic 🧄 (minced)
- 3 chili 🌶 padi (cut)
.
For squid marinate:
- 1 egg white
- 2 tbsp corn starch
- 1/2 tbsp all purpose flour
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1/2 tsp salt 🧂
- 1/2 tsp pepper .
Flour mixture for coating:
- 6 tbsp all purpose flour
- 3 tbsp corn starch
.
Cooking method:
➡️ Marinate squid for 15min
➡️ Coat squid in flour mixture
➡️ Deep fry with oil (medium heat) til golden brown & put aside
➡️ Heat 2 tbsp oil in a wok
➡️ Fry minced garlic (low heat) til light brown
➡️ add in 🌶 cut chili
➡️ add fried squid
➡️ add salt & pepper, toss evenly then serve
.
