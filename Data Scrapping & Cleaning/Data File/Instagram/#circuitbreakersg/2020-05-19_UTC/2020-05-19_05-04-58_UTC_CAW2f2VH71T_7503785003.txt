CIRCUIT BREAKER MEALS:
PORK RIBS PRAWN NOODLES

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED:
PRAWNS (APPROX 700G)
PORK RIBS (APPROX 600G)
IKAN BILLIS ( APPROX 40G)
KANGKONG
FISH CAKES
BEAN SPROUTS
YELLOW NOODLES
PORK FATS
FRIED SHALLOTS
ROCK SUGAR (APPROX 20-30G, ADJUST IF NEEDED) 
STAR ANISE (2-3 PCS)
WHITE PEPPER CORNS (2 TBS)
GARLIC (5-6 CLOVES)
GINGER (4 SLICES)
DARK SOY SAUCE (1 TBS)
FISH SAUCE (1 TBS)
PEPPER (1/4 TBS)
WATER (APPROX 2L, ADJUST IF NEEDED)

1. BLANCH THE PORK RIBS IN GINGER

5. TAKE OUT THE COOKED PRAWNS, LEAVE THE SHELLS AND AND HEAD IN THE POT

7. IN A SEPARATE POT BOIL KANGKONG, BEAN SPROUTS AND FISHCAKE, SET ASIDE
8. FRY THE PROK FATS TILL GOLDEN BROWN
9. BLANCH THE NOODLES
