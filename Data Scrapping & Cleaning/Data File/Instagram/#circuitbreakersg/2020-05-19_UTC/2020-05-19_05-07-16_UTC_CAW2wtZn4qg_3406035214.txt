#homemade char siew noodles. Noodle tossing sauce adapted from @rasamalaysia . I love the garlic oil!
. 
Char siew baked at 200C, for 20 mins, then u bua the marinade on both sides, then u flip the other side to bake. Then u bake another 20 mins, then bua the char siew sauce again and flip side and bake. Bake for another 10 mins. Den bua again with char siew sauce, flip side. Then bake another 5-10 mins or until cooked. Cool down before you slice the char siew.
