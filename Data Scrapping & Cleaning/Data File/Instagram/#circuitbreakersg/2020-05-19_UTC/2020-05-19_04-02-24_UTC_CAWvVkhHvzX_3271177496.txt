Craving for some prawn noodles and Traditional Famous Prawn Mee hits all the right spots. Ingredients are fresh and portions are generous, so it's easy to see why they sell out early on a typical day. /

However I was disappointed that they do not give any soup if you order the dry version. Having not tried the soup, I can't ascertain how highly it ranks among my list of favourite prawn noodles stalls. /

Let's #supportlocal businesses during this Circuit Breaker period. And stay home, stay safe and stay #sgunited.
