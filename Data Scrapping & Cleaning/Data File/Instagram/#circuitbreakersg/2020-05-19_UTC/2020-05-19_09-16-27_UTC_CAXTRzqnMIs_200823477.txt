Craving for a full of flavour and packed with nutritious goodness piping hot miso salmon fish soup? Light and healthy, yum......👍😋 Step 1: Using @ninjakitchensg #ninjafoodiop300, set to SAUTE function, add some oil (not too much) pan fry ginger slices till golden, add in patted dry salmon fish bones. Fried till golden brown. Add onion, spring onion and boiling water into the pot. Cook for a few mins and use a fine mesh, sieve any fish bones from the soup. 
Step 2: Once the soup is clean, add daikon radish, potatoes, carrots and negi (only the white part), set PRESSURE  function for 10mins.
Step 3:  After releasing of pressure, set to SAUTE function. Add tofu, negi (the green part), add pieces of salmon in to the boiling hot soup. Last season with some salt and lots of Yuzu Miso from @hinodeshotengai. Scoop out any fat, foam and scum you see floating on the surface of the soup. Serve hot and enjoy 😋
