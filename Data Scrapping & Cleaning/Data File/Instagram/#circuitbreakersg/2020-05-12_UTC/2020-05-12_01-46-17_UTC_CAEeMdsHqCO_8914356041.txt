🥞 Made using Morinaga Hotcake Mix
1. whisk 1 egg with 140ml cold milk and 1 tsp vanilla extract
2. next stir in 1 small pack (150g) of hotcake mix till it forms a smooth batter
3. cover with cling wrap and chilled for 30 minutes before using
4. preheat a non-stick skillet on medium low heat
5. pour 1/4 cup of cold hotcake batter into the skillet, cover and cook for a minute or so till many air bubbles appears
6. check the colour underneath and flip on, cover and cook for another 30-40 seconds
7. remove to cooking rack while continue with the remaining better
