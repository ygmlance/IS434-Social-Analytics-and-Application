Rain rain go away 
1. Print umbrellas, laminate, cut out and put magnets on the back so they will stick to your board. 
2. Use these Rain Rain manipulatives above heart beats to track the steady beat or draw a sol-mi or high-low chart on your board 
3. Have your kids to put the umbrellas on the board to show the highs and lows or sol’s and mi’s in the song.
