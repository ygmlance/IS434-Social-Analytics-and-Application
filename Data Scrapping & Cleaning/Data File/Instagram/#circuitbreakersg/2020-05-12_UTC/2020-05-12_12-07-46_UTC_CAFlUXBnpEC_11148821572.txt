Ever wonder how the color of the Carved Candles are being created? Swipe left for to check out the final product photo.⁠⠀
⁠⠀
The first step to creating a Beautiful Carved Candle is the formation of many layer of Colorful Color. These beautiful layer of color cannot be created by the traditional Candle Molding process.⁠⠀
⁠⠀
The layer of color are carried out by dipping the Core Candle into layers and layers of warm color wax. ⁠⠀
⁠⠀
Although there is only one shade of each color in wax warmer, Candle Artist are able to create different shade of Blue with their skillful hand.⁠⠀
