Cantonese style pan fried ang kah prawn (Gon Jin Har Lok) #干煎虾碌
🦐🦐
Simple and easy to prepare and cook. Clean the prawns and pat dry. Season with some salt and pepper. Heat the pan with some oil, pan fried all prawns till turned color and with a bit of char on the outside. Remove from pan and serve. Yummy!
