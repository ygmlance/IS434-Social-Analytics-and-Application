Ayam suir serundeng, shredded chicken with grated coconut
I've left over shredded chicken from lunch, I made a new dish out of the left over for dinner. 
Simply make paste from shallot, garlic, ginger, blue ginger and chilli, fry with little bit of oil, I use dried grated coconut so save me time to make it dry faster, season with salt and sugar.
