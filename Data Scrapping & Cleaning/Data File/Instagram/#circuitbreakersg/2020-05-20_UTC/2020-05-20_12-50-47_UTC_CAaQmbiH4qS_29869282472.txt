【Pork Fried Rice】⁣
⁣
A quick and delicious way to get dinner on the table fast! ⁣
⁣
INGREDIENTS (for 2 pax)⁣
▫️100g ground pork⁣
▫️2 cloves garlic⁣
▫️1 tbsp scallops sauce⁣
▫️1 tbsp light soy sauce ⁣
▫️1/2 cup water⁣
▫️Cooked rice for 2⁣
▫️Red pepper, cut into cubes⁣
▫️Green pepper, cut into cubes⁣
▫️Shiitake mushrooms @mushroomkingdom_sg ⁣
▫️1 egg⁣
▫️1 tsp @liloikanbilis mushroom powder ⁣
▫️Chopped green onion⁣
⁣
INSTRUCTIONS ⁣
1️⃣Marinate minced pork with #lilomushroompowder , light soy sauce and corn flour for 15 minutes.⁣
⁣
2️⃣Heat 1 tbsp cooking oil and 1 tsp sesame oil in a skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Sauté garlic until fragrant.⁣
⁣
3️⃣Add the ground pork. Stir and cook until browned, 2 minutes.⁣
⁣
4️⃣Add scallop sauce, light soy sauce and water. Stir and cook for 1 minute.⁣
⁣
5️⃣Add peppers and mushroom. Stir and mix well.⁣
⁣
6️⃣Add the rice. Cook and stir to mix everything together.⁣
⁣
7️⃣Move everything to one side of the pan. Add the beaten eggs. Let the bottom set for a couple seconds. Then scramble the eggs and use your spatula to cut them into small pieces. Then mix the eggs with the other ingredients.⁣
⁣
8️⃣Add mushroom powder to taste.⁣
⁣
9️⃣Serve hot. ⁣
⁣
