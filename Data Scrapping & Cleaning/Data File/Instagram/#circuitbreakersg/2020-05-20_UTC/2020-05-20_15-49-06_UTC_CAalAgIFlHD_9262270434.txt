Just few miles from city lies a series of beautiful Hindu shrines set in dramatic limestone caves called batu caves.
..
The Batu Caves are said to be about 400 million years old. It is a home to three main lime stone caverns, most popular temple cave.
..
Guarded by a monumental statue of Hindu deity Lord Murugan,140 feet tall golden painted statue was erected during 2006, a tribute adorning the feet of the divine 272 steps.
..
Batu Caves in Malaysia are one of the most important Hindu religious sites outside of India and a must-see place in Kuala Lumpur.
