Chessy Burger Cup-

For the Sauce :
1/2 cup Mayonaise
1/2 cup Tomato sauce
2 tsp Yellow Mustard

For the Burgers
3 @missionfoods.sg supersoft wrap onion and chive
250gr ground beef
1/2 chopped onion
6 slices cheedar cheese
1/2 cup Milk
Salt and Pepper -Preheat oven to 180'c
-Cut missionfoods wrap into 9cm circles and press into muffin pan, bake 5-8 minute until light browned.
-Cook the ground beef, onion, salt and pepper using a 12-inch skillet  over s medium-high heat, stir it occasionally, until the beef turn brown. -Pour the milk and cheese  and cook it over a medium heat, 
stir occasionally at about 5-7 minutes or until the cheese get melted.
-Spoon the meat mixture evenly into each tortilla cup. 
Top it each with sauce and sesame seed. 
Serve immediately.
