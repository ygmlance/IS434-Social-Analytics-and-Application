📌RECIPE>> Seafood Pasta 海鮮義大利麵
.
This seafood spaghetti has superseded marinara and pesto based pasta to be my favourite homecooked pasta. The flavour extracted from the prawn heads (you will need to use fresh prawns for this recipe, no compromise), sweetness of the seafood and tomato all comes together to give this dish it's natural umami.
.
