Instructions: 
1.	Preheat oven to (160¬∞ C). Grease a loaf pan with oil/butter and line with paper.
2.	Mash the bananas with a fork in a bowl so they still have a bit of texture then set aside. 
3.	Sift the self raising flour, baking powder and soda then Set aside.
4.	Heat the butter in a microwave for 20seconds or until it melts. 
5.	Add in cooking oil and mix well with the butter then set aside. 
6.	Whisk eggs and brown sugar by hand until they are foamy.
7.	Add in butter and oil to the mixture, mix well. 
8.	Add in self raising flour to the mixture, gently fold and stir with a spatula. Make sure the flour is fully dissolve. DO NOT over stir the batter. 
9. Fold in the mashed bananas into the mixture. Add in a handful of semi sweet chocolate chips (optional) 
10.	Pour the banana cake batter into the pan and add in 1 sliced cut banana on top for decoration. 
11.	Bake the banana cakes for 40-50 minutes or until the surface appears golden brown and a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean. 
12.	Remove the cake from the oven, leave it to cool for about 10 minutes before slicing and ready to serve.
