Hello everyone. The Singapore government has recently started the third mask distribution and has provided an instructional video on how to care for your reusable ULTRA Mask. However, as the video only provides visual description, we understand that it may not be accessible for persons with visual impairment. Here is a step by step guide on how to care for your mask.
.
How to care for your ULTRAMASK: 
1. Rinse the front and back with room temperature water 
2. Add a drop of soap and rub gently 
3. Carefully wash the soap away. Note, Kid’s masks can be washed with nose strip attached 
4. Hang dry your mask
.
Things to note: 
1. Do not twist or wring 
2. Do not use bleach, softener or detergent with fluorescent agent 
3. Do not iron Once your ULTRAMASK is dry, fold and store it in the ziplock packaging. You can wash your mask up to 30 times!
.

第一步 ：用清水淋湿口罩的正面和反面 
第二步： 加入清洁剂后，用手指轻轻搓揉 
第三步： 用清水冲走泡沫 
最后一步：将口罩挂在架子上晾干 注意的事项：在清洗时，切记不要扭和拧干口罩，而孩童尺寸口罩上的鼻梁条可一併清洗。
.
另外，也要记得不可熨烫 口罩，不可使用漂白剂、柔软剂，以及含荧光剂的洗涤用品。晾干后，将口罩折好收进拉链袋里。
