Maple soy drumlet rice bowl. Chicken marinated in soy sauce, maple syrup, grated ginger, sesame oil and pepper. Pan fried for 10 to 12 minutes and finally glazed with more maple syrup and remaining marinate. Love that combination of sweet and savoury flavours.
.
