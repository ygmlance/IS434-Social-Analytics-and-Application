CIRCUIT BREAKER MEALS:
CEREAL PRAWNS
#omxdefoodieVERSION

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED!
1) PRAWNS
2) CURRY LEAVES
3) CHILLI PADI
4) CEREAL PREMIX (1 PACKET)
5) EGG
6) POTATO STARCH
7) SALT
8) BUTTER

1) DESHELL AND DEVEIN PRAWNS

4) DOUBLE FRY ALL PRAWNS (JUST AWHILE), SET ASIDE
5) ADD BUTTER IN PAN
6) ADD CHOPPED CHILLI PADI AND CURRY LEAVES, STIR FRY TILL FRAGRANT
7) ADD IN CEREAL PREMIX, MIX WELL
8) ADD IN FRIED PRAWNS, MIX AND COAT THEM EVENLY AND SERVE!
