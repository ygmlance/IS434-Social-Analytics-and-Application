Circumstances have forced us all, adults and children alike, to be living more and more of our lives online. In a strange, perverse way, these changes leave me longing for the tactile, the physical, the sensuous. Studio time is particularly grounding these days. I am guessing a similar instinct also contributes, at least partially, to the surge in cooking as a hobby. The material experience brings about an understanding and appreciation of the world that is deeper than words and logic.
