Didn’t know what to eat for lunch so we decide to settle for “instant meal” using @primataste Laska La Mian.  Enhance the meal with extra blanched shrimp (cook in the gravy), beancurd puffs, fishballs and greens
.
今天的午餐来碗快速”叻沙”哦 + 糖水
.
Ingredients
2 pack Laksa La Mian
5 fishballs, halved
6 medium prawns
2 hard-boiled eggs
1 stalk lemon grass, optional
some beancurd puffs
some greens or beansprouts, blanched
.
Method
1. Sauté lemongrass (lightly bruised) with all the chili paste provided in the noodle packets till aromatic
2. Add in the amount of water stated on the instructions
3. When mixtures come to boil, add in beancurd puffs, fish balls and prawns
4. Remove the cooked prawns, peeled off the shell and set prawns aside
5. Cook noodles according to packet instructions, remove and place in serving bowls
6. Ladle hot soup over the noodles, arrange the ingredients and serve immediately .
.
