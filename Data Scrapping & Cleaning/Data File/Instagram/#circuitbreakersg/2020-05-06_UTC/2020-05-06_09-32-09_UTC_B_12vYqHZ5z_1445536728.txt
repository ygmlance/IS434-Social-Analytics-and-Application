Ingredients:
Black sago/tapioca pearls
Coconut brown sugar
Almond milk
Black tea
Ice cubes.
Heavy cream and coconut brown sugar for Topping on top.
.

How to make black pearl:
50gr bought store sago/tapioca pearls 
Water
Food coloring, black

Boil a small pot of water. When water is boiling add in the pearls. Reduce heat and simmer for 10-15minutes.
Occasionally stir the sides of the bottom of the pot to prevent sticking.
After 10-15 minutes the sago had turn partially translucent. Turn off the flame, cover the pot with lid and let it stand for 10-15 minutes. The sago will continue cooking by itself and turn fully translucent.
Rinse the cooked sago through a sieve and running water to remove the excess starch.
Add 2-3 drop black food coloring and brown sugar according to your desire, stir well and keep in the fridge overnight.
The pearls will ready to use in the next morning. .
