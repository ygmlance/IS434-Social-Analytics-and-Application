Burnt Cheesecake. 🍴🍰
- 500 Gram of cream cheese 🧀 (I used philadelphia )
- 50 Gram all purpose flour
- 40 Gram of sugar ( mine's not sweet, put at least 80 Gram for a better taste) 🧂
- 3 Eggs 🥚 🥚 🥚 🍳🍳
- 30 ML whipped cream 🍼
- 1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
- 1 pcs of Lemon juice 🍋 🍋🍋🍋
- 8 inc pan 
Mash / whisk the cream cheese 1st till it's diluted , then add in eggs, flour and stir till it's mixed. Put in the remaining of the ingredients and continue to mix em till a smooth silky texture.
Bake for 35 mins @ 240° . Check on it after 20 mins , if needed lower it to 200° to prevent it from over-charred.
