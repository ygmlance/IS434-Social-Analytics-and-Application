⁣⁣⁣⁣
Ingredients:⁣⁣⁣⁣
• 170g Oat Flour aka blended rolled oatmeal⁣⁣⁣⁣
• 4 Bananas⁣⁣⁣⁣
• 2 Eggs⁣⁣⁣⁣
• 85g Honey • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣⁣
Method:⁣⁣⁣⁣
1) Gather all ingredients into a mixing bowl⁣⁣⁣⁣
2) Mix it up till smooth batter forms⁣⁣⁣⁣
3) Feel free to add your own toppings/mix inside the batter - chocolate, nuts, seeds, matcha powder etc⁣⁣⁣⁣
4) Pour batter into GREASED/ line w parchment paper pan ⁣⁣⁣⁣
5) Bake in oven, 180 degrees,  30mins (varies for different oven types) or try the toothpick method! ⁣⁣⁣⁣
6) Wait cool and enjoy! ⁣
⁣
Others:⁣
• I have tried using rolled oats (without blending) & they turn out fine! just prefer the smoother texture u get from blending it - taste more like plain flour :)⁣
• I reduced amt of honey whenever i include milk chocolate so it wont be overly sweet
