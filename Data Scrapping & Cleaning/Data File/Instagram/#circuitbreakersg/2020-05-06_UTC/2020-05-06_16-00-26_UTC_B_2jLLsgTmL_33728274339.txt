Are you looking for ways to support our local arts scene? If so, do check out @apart.sg! With a mission to ensure that the Arts continue to thrive even during this Covid-19 period, apART.sg is a platform where artists and audiences can come together and be a part of something bigger, even though they may be physically apart.

Aiming to turn crisis into an opportunity, apART.sg aims to showcase Singaporean artists across different forms and genres, contribute to the digitisation of the Arts, and facilitate greater engagement and collaboration within our community. They hope that this virtual platform ensures everyone can continue to access and appreciate the Arts, no matter the times. 
Do check out their website, apART.sg for more information!
