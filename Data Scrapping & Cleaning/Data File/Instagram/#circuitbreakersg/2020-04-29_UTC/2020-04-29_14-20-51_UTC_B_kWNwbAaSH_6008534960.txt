#bonappetit

Ingredients
2 lamb rack

Methods
1. Stir fry garlic, ginger till fragrance
2. Add in lamp and sear both side of meat till preferred colour. Add in butter as well 
3. Pour everything into a metal plate and pop into oven @ 180DegC for 20min. 
Note
- remember to flip the meat to ensure even cooking.
- time can be reduced or extend for preference texture
