Not a fan of coffee? ☕️ Here is a Matcha version.  It is Keto friendly too because it is made with almond milk and it is sugar free!!! Using sugar-free organic almond milk from @naturesglorysg. 🥛🍃⁣
⁣⁣
⁣
𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤:⁣⁣
2 tablespoons premium match powder (I used @craftteafox)⁣
2 tablespoons stevia (I use @gaviasweet) ⁣
2 tablespoons hot water⁣⁣
50ml heavy whipping cream (I used Millac 38% fat. Yes, it is keto friendly without adding any sugar) ⁣
⁣Sugar-free almond milk @NaturesGlorySG⁣
⁣
𝕊𝕥𝕖𝕡𝕤:⁣⁣
1. Add matcha powder, stevia sugar and hot water in a small bowl.⁣ Stir and mix it. Set aside. 
2.Pour heavy whipping cream in a mixing bowl. 
3.Use a hand mixer or hand whisk and whip till light soft peak. (See video) ⁣
4.Add the matcha mixture into the  whipped cream and mix till combined.⁣
5.Add a few ice cubes in a glass, then pour almond milk till 3/4 of the glass. ⁣⁣
4.Spoon the whipped matcha cream on top.⁣⁣
5.You many sprinkles some matcha powder on top. ⁣⁣
6.Stir and Enjoy! ⁣⁣
