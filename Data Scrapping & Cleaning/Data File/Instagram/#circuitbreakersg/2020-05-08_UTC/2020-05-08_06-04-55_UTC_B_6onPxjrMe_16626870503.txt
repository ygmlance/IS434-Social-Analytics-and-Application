Wonderful cinnamon bun recipe by Chef Richard Bertinet and thanks to my dear friend @purpleccloud who shared this with me.⁣
⁣
Just Google for Chef Richard Bertinet cinnamon bun and you will get to see the best recipe video on YouTube 😁⁣
⁣
I use Thermomix for mixing dough and filling, so here are the steps if you are using Thermomix.⁣
⁣
𝐅𝐨𝐫 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐝𝐨𝐮𝐠𝐡⁣
Add milk, instant yeast⁣
2min/37Dc/sp2 (to set the right temperature for the yeast)⁣
⁣
Add rest of dough ingredients ⁣
Mix 5s/sp3.5⁣
Dough mode 2min (or stretch to total 3min) ⁣
⁣
Proof according to the video recipe⁣
⁣
𝐅𝐨𝐫 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐟𝐢𝐥𝐥𝐢𝐧𝐠⁣
Add Demerara sugar Turbo 2 secs to make it fine (if you are using soft brown sugar as per video recipe, skip this step)⁣
Insert butterfly⁣
Add all filling ingredients⁣
1 Min 30 secs speed 3.5⁣
⁣
Follow rest of video recipe.⁣
⁣
