Butter Cookies (soo 🧈 so good) 🍴 🍪
- All purpose flour 210 Gram
- Sugar 80 Gram 🧂
- Corn Starch 50 Gram 🌽🌽🌽
- 1 Tbsp Vanilla essence 🍼 🍦
- 1 block of butter 🧈 ( I used Buttercup)
- 1 Egg 🥚 
Mix sugar & butter , add in the remaining and continue stirring till it's smooth. 
Bake for 20 mins at 180°
