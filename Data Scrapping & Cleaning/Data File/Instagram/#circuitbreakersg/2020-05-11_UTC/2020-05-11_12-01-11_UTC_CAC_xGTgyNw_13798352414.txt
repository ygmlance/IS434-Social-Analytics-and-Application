Instant Noodle Aglio E Olio (Serving Size 1~2)⠀
Instant Noodle - 1 pkt⠀
Beyond meat - 1 patty⠀
Instant Chopped Garlic - UP TO YOU!⠀
Chilli Flakes - UP TO YOU!⠀
Parsley Flakes - for garnish⠀
Olive Oil - UP TO YOU!⠀
Salt & Pepper - UP TO YOU!⠀
⠀
⠀
Heat a generous amount of olive oil > add chopped garlic & chilli flakes > saute for 2 minutes until garlic slightly change color > add beyond meat > saute for 1 minute > add salt & pepper > saute till beyond meat cook through > add cooked noodles > saute till mix well > done!⠀
