Make sure to swipe till the end because there are 2 different variations that you have to do for this workout.⁣
⁣
You start each exercise with dumbbells then superset it immediately with the band. This is a great combination for building muscle strength & endurance . ⁣
⁣
The work 👇⁣
⁣
superset 1⁣
✖️Floor press / Fly ⁣
3 x 8 / 15⁣
⁣
Superset 2 ⁣
✖️Db rows / band rows ⁣
3 x 8 / 15⁣
⁣
Superset 3 ⁣
✖️Db Z press / band Z press ⁣
3 x 8 / 15⁣
⁣
superset 4 ⁣
✖️Db hammer curls / band inner curls ⁣
3 x 8 / 15 ⁣
⁣
Superset 5 ⁣
✖️DB kickbacks / band kickbacks ⁣
3 x 8 / 15 ⁣
⁣
⁣
👉 hit superset 1 back to back with minimal rest in between the exercises. Once you’ve performed each exercise once over , take 30 rest  and then repeat the superset 2 more times. Then go to superset 2 and do the same👌⁣⁣
