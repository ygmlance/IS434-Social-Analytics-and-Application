Here’s an idea, Let’s cook👩‍🍳.

Ingredients & steps:
1️⃣ Blanched Broccoli (with salted boiling water for 4mins, set aside)
2️⃣ 3 Egg whites (give a good whisk)
3️⃣ in a bowl combine & mix well
50ml Milk
1tbsp cornstarch
1/4tsp salt
1/2tsp sugar
1/2tsp @liloikanbilis Premium Scallop Powder
1 dried scalloped (soaked in water until softens 25-40mins)
▪️after mixing add in the whisked egg whites
◾️ stir fry it
4️⃣ Food plating: arrange broccoli 🥦, add in the egg white scallop in the middle
5️⃣ 1 Fresh Egg yolk (add on top)
6️⃣ 𝗦𝗲𝗿𝘃𝗲 𝘄𝗵𝗶𝗹𝗲 𝗶𝘁’𝘀 𝗵𝗼𝘁, 𝗘𝗻𝗷𝗼𝘆!
