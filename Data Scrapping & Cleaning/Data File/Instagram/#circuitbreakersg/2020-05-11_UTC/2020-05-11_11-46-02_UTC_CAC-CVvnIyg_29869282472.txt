【Mushrooms and Butternut Squash rice】⁣
⁣
Calling all butternut squash lovers! Delicious, hearty, and ready in a flash not to mention it’s all made in one pot! I added ham in it as my son’s request. ⁣
⁣
INGREDIENTS (for 2 pax)⁣
▫️Ham, sliced ⁣
▫️Butternut Squash @kidslovrsg , skin removed and cut into 1-inch cubes⁣
▫️Shiitake mushrooms @mushroomkingdom_sg , sliced⁣
▫️Uncooked rice⁣
▫️1 tbsp dark soy sauce ⁣
▫️1 tsp @liloikanbilis mushroom powder ⁣
▫️Homemade chicken stock, 1 cup⁣
▫️Chopped green onion⁣
⁣
INSTRUCTIONS ⁣
1️⃣Uncooked rice in the rice cooker. Add in chicken stock and dark soy sauce, mix well.⁣
⁣
2️⃣Add ham, butternut Squash, mushrooms into the rice cooker. Hit START button and start to cook.⁣
⁣
3️⃣Once it is cooked, sprinkle mushroom powder to add umami in the rice. Mix well.⁣
⁣
4️⃣Garnish with chopped green onion. Serve warm⁣
