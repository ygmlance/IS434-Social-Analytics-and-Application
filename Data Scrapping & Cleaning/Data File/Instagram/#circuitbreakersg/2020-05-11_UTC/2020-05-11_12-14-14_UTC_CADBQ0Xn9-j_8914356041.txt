ğŸ— Korean Spicy Chicken Legs
2 chicken legs with thighs
1 tsp Korean chili flakes (Gochugaru)
1 tbsp Korean chili paste (Gochujang)
1 tbsp honey
1/2 tbsp soy sauce
1/4 tsp dark soy sauce
1/2 tsp sesame oil
1/2 tbsp cooking wine
1/2 tbsp grated ginger juice
2 garlic cloves, lightly crashes
.
Method
1. Put all the ingredients into a ziplock bag, mix well then chilled and seasoned for about ah hour or overnight
2. Air-fry chicken legs (scoop marinade over the chicken) at 170 degree for 15 minutes, flip over and bake for another 8-10 minutes at 180 degree or until cooked through
3. Remove and serve
