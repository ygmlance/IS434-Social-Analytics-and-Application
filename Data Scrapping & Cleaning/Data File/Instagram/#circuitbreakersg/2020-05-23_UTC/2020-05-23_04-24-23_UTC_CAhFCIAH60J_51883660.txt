Stir fry spicy Korean baby octopus! 
Adapted without using gochuchang:
Sauce
- 2 tbl organic tomato sauce / homemade (original Korean gochuchang) - 1 tbl gf soysauce - 2 tbl sesame oil - 2 tbl chopped garlic
- 1 tsp chili powder
- (optional) 2-3d lime eo, I love some zesty citrus scent on it and lime eo helps to reduce some spiciness
. . .
- Clean and cut baby octopus, marinate in a bowl with the mixed sauce for at least 10 mins
- Heat up oil, fry any vegetable you would like to add for few minutes - Add marinated baby octopus, stir fry about 5 mins - Serve with sprinkle of sesame seed
It’s great to serve over a plate of hot white rice if you’re non keto! . . .
