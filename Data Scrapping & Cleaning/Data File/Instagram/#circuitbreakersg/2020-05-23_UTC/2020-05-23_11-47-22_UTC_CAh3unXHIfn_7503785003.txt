CIRCUIT BREAKER MEALS:
GULUYOK (SWEET & SOUR PORK)
#omxdefoodieVERSION 
INGREDIENTS YOU NEED:
1. ONION (1, CUT)
2. GREEN CAPSICUM (1, CUT)
3. PORK SHOULDER ( 2 PIECES)
4. TOMATO SAUCE
5. SUGAR
6. LIGHT SOY SAUCE
7. PEPPER
8. FIVE SPICE POWDER
9. OYSTER SAUCE
10. SESAME OIL
11. POTATO STARCH

TIME TO COOK!üßëüèª‚Äçüç≥üë®üèª‚Äçüç≥
1. CUT PORK SHOULDER INTO CUBES
2. MARINATE THE PORK CUBES WITH ITEMS LIGHT SOY SAUCE (1.5 TBS), PEPPER (0.25 TBS), FIVE SPICE POWDER (0.5 TBS), OYSTER SAUCE (1 TBS), SESAME OIL (0.5 TBS) AND LET IT SIT FOR AT LEAST 30MINS
3. COAT THE MARINATED PORK CUBES IN POTATO STARCH
4. FRY THE PORK, SET ASIDE FOR WHILE
5. REFRY THE PORK (TO ENSURE IT STAYS CRISY AFTER COATING IT WITH SAUCE)
6. STIR FRY ONIONS AND CAPSICUMS TILL FRAGRANT
7. ADD IN TOMATO SAUCE, STIR
8. ADD IN SUGAR (APPROX 1-1.5 TBS, DEPENDS ON YOUR PREFERENCE)
9. ADD IN FRIED PORK, MIX WELL
10. TIME TO ENJOY!
