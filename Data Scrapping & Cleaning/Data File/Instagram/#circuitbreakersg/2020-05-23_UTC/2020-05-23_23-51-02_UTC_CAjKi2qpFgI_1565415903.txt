Mapo tofu - but with less heat. Didn’t add extra chilli and peppers
Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and sauté 1 tbsp chopped garlic 1 min. Add 400g minced pork or turkey/chicken and stirfry 3-4 min til nearly cooked. Add 2 blocks of tofu, sliced into cubes. Stir in and add 1/4 cup water if it’s too dry. Then  let simmer 1-2 min, stirring. Season to taste.
