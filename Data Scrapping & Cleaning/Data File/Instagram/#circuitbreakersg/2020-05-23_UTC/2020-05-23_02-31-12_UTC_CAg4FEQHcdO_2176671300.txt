Had a bunch of overripe pesticide-free, organically grown bananas. Baked a banana bread with #organic ingredients from @NaturesGlorySG, except baking soda & powder, cinnamon.
.
This recipe is a keeper. 
Preparation & baking within an hour: .
▪️Preheat oven at 160 deg Cel
DRY INGREDIENTS: 
11/2 cup Hard Red Spring Wheat Flour
1/4 cup Wild Harvested Coconut Palm Sugar 
1/2 tsp Himalayan Pink Salt 
1/2 tsp baking soda 
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon .
▪️Mix them together
.
WET INGREDIENTS: 
11/2 cup mashed bananas 
1/4 cup Sunflower Oil
1/4 cup #HiFloAMWater 
2 Eggs, beaten
▪️Mix them together.
▪️Stir in the dry mixture till well mixed. ▪️Pour mixture in silicon loaf pan. ▪️Bake for about 40 minutes or till a toothpick in bread comes out clean
.
Serve with a dollop of vanilla ice cream. Yummilicious #kidsapproved
