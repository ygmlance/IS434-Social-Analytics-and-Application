>> MERCURY POISONING
Methylmercury toxicity in fish can cause irreversible damage to your brain, kidneys and heart. While all fishes contain trace amounts of mercury, some contain dangerous levels. All pregnant women and young children must avoid these fishes.
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Try to eliminate these fishes from your diet:
• Mackerel • Shark • Swordfish • Tuna (canned Albacore, Yellowfin, Bigeye, Ahi) • Grouper • Bluefish • Sea Bass
⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
Take care of yourself. Eat well, stay safe, y'all.
