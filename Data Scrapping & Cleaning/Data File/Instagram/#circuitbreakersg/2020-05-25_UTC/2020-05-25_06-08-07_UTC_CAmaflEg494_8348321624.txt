⁣Flourless pizza base⬇️
Ingredients: ⁣
20g cream cheese⁣
A slice of Brie cheese⁣
10g psyllium husk ⁣
Italian seasonings⁣
Dried parsley⁣
2 eggs ⁣
⁣
⁣
Instructions: ⁣
1. Mix them and fry on a greased pan.⁣
2. You may bake the pizza base 1st or immediately top with your toppings. ⁣
3. Bake at 170 degree for about 15 mins. ⁣
