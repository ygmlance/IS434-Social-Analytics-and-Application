INGREDIENTS YOU NEED:
1. LEFT OVER CURRY FROM PREVIOUS MEAL
2. FROZEN PRATA DOUGH
3. OIL

HOW TO MAKE?
1. ADD SOME OIL ON A PLATE AND LEAVE FROZEN DOUGH TO DEFROST
2. STRETCH THE DOUGH
3. ADD LEFT OVER CURRY ONTO DOUGH (CHECK OUT MY PREVIOUS POST ON HOW TO MAKE CURRY CHICKEN)
4. WRAP IT UP
5. OIL THE FOIL AND BAKE AT 200DEG TILL GOLDEN BROWN
