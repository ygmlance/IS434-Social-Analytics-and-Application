Sourdough#2

1. 100g levain, 7hours
2. 350g filtered water
3. 500g prima all purpose flour
4. 90min autolyse
5. 6g salt + 25g filtered water
7. 3 x stretched and fold every 25min
8. 2 x coiled fold every 25min
9. 10min rest
10. Shape dough, bench rest 15min
11. Preshaped into benneton
12. Proof in refrigerator 8hours

Note
- dough doesn't hold shape well after removed from benneton
- dough feels over proofed
- taste great but lack oven spring

suggest changes
1. BF after coiled fold till morning, proof in benneton 3-4 hours
