KIMCHI FRIED RICE (볶음밥)⁣
𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤:⁣
2 cup rice (cook in rice cooker and let cool)⁣
400g Kimchi (cut into small pieces) I used my home made kimchi. ⁣
150g bacon, cut into small square pieces⁣
1 pack shimeiji mushrooms (150g) root removed⁣
4 eggs, (65g) sunny side up ⁣
4 clove minced garlic⁣
50g kimchi juice (the liquid is from the kimchi) ⁣
1/2 Tbsp cooking oil⁣
1/2 Tbsp sesame oil⁣
Pink salt⁣
1 to 2 Tbsp sesame seeds (for garnish)⁣
1/2 stalk green onion, thinly sliced (optional)⁣
⁣
𝕊𝕥𝕖𝕡𝕤: ⁣
1. Add cooking oil.⁣
2. Add the garlic, stir a few times. ⁣
3.Then add the bacon and stir it until half of it is cooked.⁣
4.Add the Kimchi and stir.⁣
5.Add the mushrooms and mix them.⁣
6.Add the rice and pink salt and kimchi juice. Mix all of thoroughly. ⁣
7.Taste and adjust the seasoning accordingly if needed. You many sprinkles some more pink salt.⁣
8.Add the sesame oil and mix them well. ⁣
9.Remove from the heat. Serve the Kimchi fried rice on a plate. ⁣
10.Garnish with sesame seeds, green onion and seaweed strips. (Optional). ⁣
Place the sunny side up egg on top. Enjoy!⁣
⁣
Note: ⁣
Use kimchi that is at least 1 to 2 weeks old. If not, it will not be sour. (You can add 1 tablespoon of gochujang)⁣
