Chai Boey Soup. 🍴🥢
Not many have heard about this, but it is very famous from my home - Penang. Full of flavour and easy to cook. 🥣🥣
-Chinese mustard green 🥬🥬
-Carrot 🥕🥕🥕
-Tomatoes 🍅 🍅 🍅 🍅 🍅 🍅🍅
-Roasted Pork , Roast chicken (or any roasted meat) to enhance the taste. 🍗🍖🥩
- Shitake Mushrooms 🍄 🍄 🍄 🍄 🍄 🍄 -Dried Chili 🌶🌶🌶🌶🌶🌶 🌶🌶
Put in everything in a pot and boil for few hours till it turn into orange-ish.
