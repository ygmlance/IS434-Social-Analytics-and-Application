HONEY SOY GINGER PORK

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED!
1. PORK LOIN
2. GINGER
3. LIGHT SOY SAUCE
4. PEPPER
5. HONEY
6. SESAME OIL

1. THINLY SLICE THE GINGER, SET ASIDE
2. CHOP SOME GINGER
3. CUT THE PORK LION
4. MARINATE PORK LION WITH 3TBS OF SOY SAUCE, PEPPER, CHOPPED GINGER, 1TBS OF HONEY, MIX WELL
5. ADD IN 0.5TBS OF SESAME OIL TO PORK LOIN
6. FRY THE THINLY SLICED GINGER AND SET ASIDE
7. PAN FRY THE MARINATED PORK LOIN
8. PLATE THE PORK, TOP IT OFF WITH FRIED GINGER AND ENJOY!
