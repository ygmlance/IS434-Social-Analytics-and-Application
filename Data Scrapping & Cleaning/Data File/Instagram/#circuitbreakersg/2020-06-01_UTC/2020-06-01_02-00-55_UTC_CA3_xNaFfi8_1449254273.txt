[COMING SOON] Precious Is The Night《今宵多珍重》is a Taiwanese-Singapore thriller set in 1960’s Singapore. Directed by Wayne Peng 彭文淳, the film follows a 30-something doctor (Chuando Tan 陈传多) who is caught in a web of deceit, sex and lies. The murder drama revolves around the doctor making house calls to a mysterious wealthy family.

Starring Internet sensation model-photographer Chuando Tan 陈传多, Taiwanese model Nanyeli 南苗 and model-actress Chang Tzulei 张子蕾, Singapore actress Xiang Yun 向云 and her daughter Chen Yixin 陈一心, and actor Tay Ping Hui 郑斌辉.

即将上映 Precious Is The Night《今宵多珍重》是一部台湾新加坡惊悚片以1960年新加坡为背景。

彭文淳执导，电影描述一名约三十岁的医生(Chuando Tan 陈传多) 陷入了一场欺骗、性爱与谎言的迷网中。这部电影围绕着一名出诊医生给一户神秘并富有家庭看诊的故事。

网红兼模特、摄影师陈传多，台湾模特南苗，模特兼演员张子蕾，新加坡实力派演员向云与女儿陈一心，以及郑斌辉主演。
