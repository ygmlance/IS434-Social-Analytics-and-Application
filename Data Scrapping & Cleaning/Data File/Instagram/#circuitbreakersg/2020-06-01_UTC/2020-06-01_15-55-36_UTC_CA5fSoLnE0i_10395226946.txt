Its #angkukueh, a small round or oval-shaped Chinese pastry with soft, sticky glutinous rice flour skin wrapped around a sweet filling in the centre. It is molded to resemble a tortoise shell and is presented resting on a square piece of banana leaf. In this pic, 寿, meaning longevity, is imprinted on the skin.
