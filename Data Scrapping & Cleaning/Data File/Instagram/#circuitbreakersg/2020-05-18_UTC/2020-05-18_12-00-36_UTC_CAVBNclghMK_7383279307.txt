Comparable to Thai Moo Ping, Japanese yakitori and even local satay, Bun Cha is a Vietnamese dish of grilled pork that’s typically served with cold rice noodles and fresh herbs.⁠
⁠
Refusing to stick to the status quo, we take this humble meat dish from Hanoi a level up by using Berkshire (similar to Japanese Kurobuta) pork—a leaner, more flavourful choice of pork.⁠
