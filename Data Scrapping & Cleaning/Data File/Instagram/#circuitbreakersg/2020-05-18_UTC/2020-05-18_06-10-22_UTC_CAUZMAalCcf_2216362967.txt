Ingredients:
500g pork spare ribs, pre-blanched for 5mins
150g peanuts
2 lotus roots, yielded 15-20 slices
8 red dates
2 litres of water

Method:
Put all ingredients in a pot, let it come to a boil. Then turn heat down to simmer for at least 2 hours (3 hours will be best). Add a pinch of salt and serve!
