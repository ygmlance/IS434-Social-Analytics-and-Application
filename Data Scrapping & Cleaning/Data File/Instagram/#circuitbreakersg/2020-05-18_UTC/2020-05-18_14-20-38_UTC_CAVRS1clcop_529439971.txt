Are our seniors tech savvy? 
In an interview with the Sunday Times, Head of HASS Prof Lim Sun Sun shared on how seniors were embracing #technology to stay in touch with their friends and family during the #circuitbreakersg. At the same time, she also points out how some seniors who were living alone or did not own digital devices, could feel isolated from their loved ones.
