Ingredients:
Vegetable oil
Chopped garlic
Chopped onions
Chopped Cabbage
1/4 cup Water
1 tsp @liloikanbilis Premium Scallop powder
4-5 cups Overnight Rice
2tbsp Preserved Olive Vegetables
2 Eggs
2 tbsp Soy sauce (optional)
Spring onions (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat oil to a pan, add garlic, add onion (stir until fragrant)
2. Add in cabbage, then add abit of water (cook and stir) 
3. Add in @liloikanbilis scallop powder, stir
4. Add in the rice (cook and stir)
5. Add in the preserved olive vegetables (add more if u like), stir mix well
6. Break 2 eggs, cook at the side, stir abit and wait until half cooked
7. Add in soy sauce (stir & mix well)
8. Top it with spring onions if you have
