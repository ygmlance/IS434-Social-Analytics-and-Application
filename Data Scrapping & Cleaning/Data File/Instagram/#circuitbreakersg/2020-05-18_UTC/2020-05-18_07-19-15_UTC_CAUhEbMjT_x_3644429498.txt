Sambal Sotong (Squid) 叁峇苏东⁣
𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤⁣
2 pcs of sotongs (squids), cut into rings⁣
1 tbsp cooking oil⁣
2 cloves minced garlic⁣
2 shallots, sliced⁣
11/2 tsp Sambal Belachan Chili (add more if you like spicy)⁣
Some pink salt
⁣
𝕊𝕥𝕖𝕡𝕤:⁣
1. Heat up the cooking in a wok.  Add garlic and shallots and fry. Stir till fragrant and becomes soft.⁣
2. Add the sotongs(squids) and stir briefly. ⁣
3. Add sambal paste and some water. Sprinkles some pink salt. Stir the sambal with the sotongs(squids) till well combined and the squids are cooked. ⁣
4. Dish and serve hot.⁣
