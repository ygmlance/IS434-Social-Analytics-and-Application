Method 
1) Marinate the chicken with the marinade ingredients and let sit for 30 mins. 
2) SautÃ© the ginger, garlic, cilantro root, cinnamon stick and star anise in medium sturdy pot over medium Low heat till aromatic. Add in shiitake and stir fry a couple mins as well.
