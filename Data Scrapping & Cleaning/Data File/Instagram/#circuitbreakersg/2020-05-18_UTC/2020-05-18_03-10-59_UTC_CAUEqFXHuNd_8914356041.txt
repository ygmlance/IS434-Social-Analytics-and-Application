INGREDIENTS
1 pack (400-500g) flat Hokkien Noodle 
6-8 medium prawns, shells removed
3 frozen scallop, halved
1 medium fish cake, sliced
10 slices of sliced pork belly, optional
1/2 cup beansprouts, tail removed
4 leaves Chinese cabbage, cut into bite size 
1-2 eggs
3 clove garlic, minced 
1 stalk scallion, cut into sections
250ml water or chicken stock
extra sambal chilli, optional
.
SEASONINGS 
2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
1 tbsp light soy sauce
1 tbsp dark soy sauce
1/2 tbsp oyster sauce, optional
.
Method
1. Preheat skillet with 1/2 tbsp of oil then add in beaten egg to make omelette. Remove and set aside
2. In the same skillet sautÃ© half of the minced garlic till aromatic
3. Add in beansprout, fish cake and scallion follow by pinch of salt and drizzle of cooking wine
4. Still and cook for 30 seconds, remove and set aside
5. Next sautÃ© remaining minced garlic with prawns (including the prawn heads), scallop, sliced pork and cabbage for a minute before adding water or stock
6. When the mixture comes to boil, remove cooked prawns and scallops
7. Add in noodles (rinse and drained), toss well then mix in seasoning sauce
8. Cover and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes till noodle absorb the sauce
9. Toss in seafood, beansprout and omelette. Mix well and braised for another 30 seconds
10. Remove and serve with cut or sambal chilli
