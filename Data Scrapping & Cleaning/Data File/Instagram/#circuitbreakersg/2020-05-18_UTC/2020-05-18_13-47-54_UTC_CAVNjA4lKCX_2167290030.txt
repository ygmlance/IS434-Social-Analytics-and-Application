Black Pepper Fried Rice

Simple and easy. It works well if you have leftover rice. Great way to use it again.
3 cups of cooked rice
1tbsp crushed pepper
1 tbsp light soy sauce
1 tsp dark soy sauce
2 tbsp sesame oil
1 small onion - sliced
4-5 flakes of garlic, sliced
1 tsp vinegar
1 egg, beaten lightly
Salt if needed
Vege or cooked chicken (optional)
Saut√© the onion & garlic in the oil, add all other ingredients and mix well. Add egg last. Cook on slow fire and stir gently till the egg has coated the rice and got cooked.
