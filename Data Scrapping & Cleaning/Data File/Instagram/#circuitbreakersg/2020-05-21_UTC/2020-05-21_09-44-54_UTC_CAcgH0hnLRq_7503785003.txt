CIRCUIT BREAKER MEALS:
GARLIC STEAMED PRAWNS
#omxdefoodieVERSION

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED!:
1. PRAWNS (500G)
2. GARLIC (CHOPPED)
3. GINGER (SLICED)
4. SCALLIONS
5. PEPPER
6. SOY SAUCE (FOR STEAMING FISH)
7. SESAME OIL
8. SHAOXING WINE

1. CUT THE PRAWNS, DEVEIN
2. PLACE SLICED GINGER AND SCALLIONS ON THE PLATE
3. MIX GARLIC, SESAME OIL (1 TEASPOON), SOY SAUCE (2 TABLESPOON), SHAOXING WINE (1 TABLESPOON)
4. ADD GARLIC MIXTURE TO PRAWNS
5. DASH OF PEPPER OVER THE PRAWNS
6. POUR SOME SOY SAUCE OVER PRAWS (ABOUT 2 TBS)
7. STEAM PRAWNS FOR 13MINS AFTER WATER BOILS
8. TIME TO EAT!
