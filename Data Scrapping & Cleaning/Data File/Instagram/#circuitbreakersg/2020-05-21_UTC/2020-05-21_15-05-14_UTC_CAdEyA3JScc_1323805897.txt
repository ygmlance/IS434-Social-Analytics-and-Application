#bananachocolatecake 🍌🍫 with 4 ingredients?

#cb #circuitbreakersg has turned Mum 👩‍🍳 into a baker. More #desserts 🍪🍩🍮 #cake 🍰 ?

1. Pre-heat oven at 180 degree 
2. Separate egg white and yolk 🥚
3. Whisk egg white till foamy / soft peak
4. Melt butter & dark chocolate 🍫
5. Mash bananas🍌
6. Mix all ingredients
7. Pour mixture into greased tin & bake 25 min
