⁣⁣ Ingredients: ⁣⁣
🥯⁣ 120g almond flour ⁣
🥯 0.5 tsp coconut flour ⁣
🥯 1. 5 tbsp psyllium husk ⁣
🥯 2 tsp baking powder ⁣
🥯 1 cup hot water ⁣
🥯 3 egg whites (slightly whip with pinch of salt) ⁣
🥯 2 tsp apple cider vinegar⁣
⁣
⁣
Instructions:⁣
➡️ Mix everything with a mixer.⁣
➡️ Then transfer to blender or food processor. ⁣
➡️Split into 10 portion⁣
➡️Preheat the oven at 170 degrees for 10 minutes and bake for 35 minutes. ⁣⁣
