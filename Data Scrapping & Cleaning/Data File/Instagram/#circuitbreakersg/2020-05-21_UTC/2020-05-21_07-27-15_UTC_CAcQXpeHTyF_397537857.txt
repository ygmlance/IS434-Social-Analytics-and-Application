Yam cake is another of my family's favourite. We always 'order' from mum whenever cravings kick in. So when Soo Leng posted hers, I know it's time to make my 1st Yam Cake.

Recipe adapted & modified from @sooleng_ng's. Please check out her account for the video & more yummy sharing üíù

Ingredients (A)
300g Rice Flour
80g Tapioca Flour
850ml Water + soaking liquid
2.5 tsp Salt
1 tsp 5-spice Powder
1/2 tsp White Pepper

Ingredients (B)
300g Yam, diced
300ml Water

Ingredients (C)
400g Yam, diced
5 Shallot, sliced

Topping
1 Lup Cheong, sliced into strips
4 cloves Garlic
100g Dried Shrimp & Scallops, soaked & diced
50g Sweet Preserved Radish
2 tsp Soya Sauce
Dash of White Pepper

Garnish
Spring Onion
Red Chilli
Shallot

Instruction:
1. Mix ingredients A together until well combined. 
2. Blend ingredients B till smooth.
3. Combine blended yam mixture with flour mixture.
4. Fry shallot with heated oil till fragrant. Add diced yam and fry till browned. Add in the mixed batter and cook till a paste is formed. 
5. Pour paste into a greased pan and steam for 45 mins.
6. Stir-fry lup cheong & garlic with some heated oil. Add dried shrimp & scallop followed by preserved radish. Add seasoning to taste.
7. Add toppings over steamed Yam Cake, garnish & serve.

Made 2 versions today; steamed & pan-fried as we have two camps here. Which camp do you belongs to?
