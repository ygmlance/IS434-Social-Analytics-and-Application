Prawn Sambal 🔥🌶🍅 A must have for special days in most Indian and Malay homes in Singapore and Malaysia. Added 2 chilli padi for extra spice this time 😀🤪
.
. 🌶 Prawn Sambal:
🌼 Prawns, devined
🌼 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
🌼 1 tsp tumeric powder
🌼 2 tsp red chilli powder
🌼 salt

Marinate Prawns with above ingredients. .
.
🌼 1 tomato, chopped
🌼 1 onion, chopped
🌼  salt
🌼 1 tsp lakanto sweetener

1. Heat oil in pan on stove.
2. Fry onion well.
3. Stir tomato in and cook on medium flame.
4. Add in prawns and cook well.
5. Season with salt and sugar when well done.
.
