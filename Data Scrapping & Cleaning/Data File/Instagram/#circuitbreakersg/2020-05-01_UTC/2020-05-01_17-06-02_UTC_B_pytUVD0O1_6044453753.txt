BRUNCH ，月光寶盒🤣，不是patties。日本may queen 薯仔🥔メークイン自家製薯盒。餡料有🥒🥑🧀🍖，健康有益，適合一家大小。 🥔大概600克，先去皮蒸熟，之壓成薯蓉。加入香草(選了basil)、少許鹽及黑糊椒，還有麵包糠和🥚。拌勻後，便可做盒底，再放入材料。芝士可放盒面或盒內。完成後，再搓一塊盒蓋，之後煎熟便可。

A healthy and special homemade brunch ~Potato box. 😋 Don't get it wrong.  They are not patties.  Used japanese may queen potatoes 🥔 and stuff with cucumber, avocado, cheese and japanese ham. 🥔 about 600g.  Peel off and steam🥔 then mash it.  Put herbs, (basil was the lucky one), pinch of salt and black pepper,  bread crumbs and egg.  Mix and stir it well.  Grab the mixture to make the potato box base and put those ingredients on top.  After stuffing, grab some to knead the top and seal it.  Pan fry for about 10 mins will do.
