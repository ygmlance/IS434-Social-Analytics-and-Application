Singaporean ‘carrot cake’ is no dessert. It is a savoury fried radish cake with mushrooms, dried shrimp and Chinese sausage. This homemade version was super yummy when fried in sesame oil with egg, chye poh, garlic, dark soya sauce and chilli. First version in previous post!
