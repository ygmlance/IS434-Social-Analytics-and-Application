𝗙𝗲𝗲𝗹𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗯𝗼𝗿𝗲𝗱? Let’s make Kimchi! ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
Here is the recipe. (Reference from @maangchi) Please refer to her website if you want the original recipe.  As I tweaked and modified the recipe to suit my preferences and taste.  I also simplified the steps and weigh the ingredients by metric. ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
𝕂𝕚𝕞𝕔𝕙𝕚 ℝ𝕖𝕔𝕚𝕡𝕖⁣⁣
𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤:⁣⁣
2kg of Napa cabbage ⁣⁣
1 cup Sea salt (200g)⁣⁣
50g Rice flour ⁣⁣
30g Sugar ⁣⁣
500ml Water ⁣⁣
16 cloves of minced Garlic ⁣⁣
2 inches Ginger ⁣⁣(sliced)
1 Yellow onion⁣⁣ (sliced)
200ml Fish sauce ⁣⁣
8 stalks Green onion(spring onion) ⁣⁣
1 medium size Carrot⁣⁣
1 medium size Radish ⁣⁣
220g Korean Pepper Flakes ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
𝕊𝕥𝕖𝕡𝕤: ⁣⁣
1. Cut the cabbage lengthwise into 6 part and remove the cores. Chop it up into 1 to 1.5 inch bite size pieces.⁣⁣
2. Soak the cabbage in a large pot or basin and add 1 cup of sea salt. (soaked 1.5 hours)⁣⁣
3. Turn the cabbage every 30 minutes.⁣⁣
4. 1.5 hours later, rinse the cabbage with water 3 times to clean it thoroughly.⁣⁣
5. Drain the cabbage and set aside.⁣⁣
6. To make rice mixture. Put 500 ml of water, 50g of rice flour and 30g sugar into a saucepan. Boil and continue to stir until translucent.  Set aside to cool.⁣⁣
7. Shred the carrot and radish. (Set aside)⁣⁣
8. Slice the green onion diagonally. (Set aside) ⁣⁣
9. To make kimchi paste. Blend garlic, yellow onion and ginger in a blender or food processor. Scoop out the mixture and set aside. ⁣⁣
10. Add fish sauce, hot pepper flakes (depending on your taste) garlic mixture into the rice mixture. ⁣⁣
11. Mix all ingredients well and your kimchi paste is done.⁣⁣
12. Add sliced green onions, radish and carrot into the cabbage. If your pot or basin is not big enough, please do it in a few batches. (I split the veggies into half) ⁣⁣
13. Mix the kimchi paste (I split into half) with the cabbages. (See video) Mix it by hand with a pair of gloves. ⁣⁣
14. Continue the same for the other half portion. And mix it thoroughly. ⁣⁣
15. Transfer the kimchi into an air-tight sealed plastic container or glass jar.⁣⁣
16. Kimchi is done! ⁣⁣
