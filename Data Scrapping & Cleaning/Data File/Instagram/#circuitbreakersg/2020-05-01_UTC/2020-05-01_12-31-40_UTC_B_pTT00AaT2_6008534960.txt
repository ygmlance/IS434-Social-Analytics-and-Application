Keto Porky Belly Satay marinated with turmeric, herbs and species

The son approves!

#porksatay

Ingredients
800g of pork belly
1 shallot
20g ginger
1 tbsp Cummin powder
1 tbsp turmeric powder
2 tbsp cooking cream
25g butter

Methods
1. Chop belly into cubes
2. Blend up all marination
3. Marinate belly for at least 2 hours.
4. Insert belly into bamboo skewers
5. Oven at 180DegC, 15min on side A and side B.

Note
- fatty pieces taste better
- include cucumber and onion would balance off the taste
