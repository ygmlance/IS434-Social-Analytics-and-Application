SOUP OF DAY
🍲 Winter Melon Porkribs Soup
250g soft pork bone
1 piece (about 200g) winter melon
1 medium carrot
1/2 sweetcorn (optional)
2 dry scallops
1 slice ginger, optional 
6 red dates
1 tbsp wolfberries
1.2L wAter
.
Method
1. Blanch pork ribs, rinse and set aside
2. Cut winter melon, carrot and sweet corns into chunks
3. Bring 1.2L water to boil add in all ingredients except wolfberries
4. When mixture comes to boil, lower the heat and simmer for 30-45 minutes on low heat
5. Season to taste, turn off the heat and add in 1 tbsp wolfberries and serve
.
