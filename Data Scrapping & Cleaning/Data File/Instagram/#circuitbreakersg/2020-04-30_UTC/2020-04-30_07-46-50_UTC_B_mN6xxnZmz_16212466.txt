Very simple to make white pepper Bak Kut Teh. Start with pork or chicken stock. Dry fry white peppercorns for a little while till fragrant, then placed it in the stock with whole garlic cloves and pork ribs. Season with salt, rock sugar, soy sauce and fish sauce. Easy right? 👍🏻👍🏻
