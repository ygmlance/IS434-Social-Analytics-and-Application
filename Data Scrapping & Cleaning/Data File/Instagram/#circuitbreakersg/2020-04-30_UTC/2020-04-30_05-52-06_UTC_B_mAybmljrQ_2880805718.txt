Allgäuer Edelbräu and Flensburger Pilsener 🇩🇪🍺 ⁣
⁣
The Edelbräu combines the characteristics of a German helles lager and pilsner, while the Flensburger is a more straightforward German-style pilsner. The Edelbräu tastes like sweet lager malt layered over a pilsner's bitter hop base, while the Flensburger is decidedly hoppy, with a crisp bitter bite that invigorates but does not overwhelm. Great brews to enjoy on a chill night!
