Lala clam noodles soup 
Broth made from anchovies, garlic, ginger, chinese wine. Stir-fry a mix of flat yellow noodles n thick white noodles with garlic, break an egg in; add bean sprouts, sliced fish cake, pork and prawns with a dash of soya sauce. Once fragrant, add in broth. Bring to a boil n add in clams n caixin vege. cook till clams open. Garnish w spring onions n serve.
