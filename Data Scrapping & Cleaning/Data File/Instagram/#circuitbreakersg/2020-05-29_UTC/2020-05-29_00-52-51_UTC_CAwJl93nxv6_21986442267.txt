Running low on canned tomatoes from your local grocery store? We’ve got you! Add Big Tom to fried onions, garlic, chilli and bacon for a delicious arrabbiata sauce and serve with Spaghetti.
•
Alternatively, chop up some cucumber and red pepper and serve on ice for a spicy Gazpacho.
•
Whether it’s a base in the bolognese or the heart of your spicy tomato soup, Big Tom has been a useful assistant in kitchens across the country for over 25 years.
