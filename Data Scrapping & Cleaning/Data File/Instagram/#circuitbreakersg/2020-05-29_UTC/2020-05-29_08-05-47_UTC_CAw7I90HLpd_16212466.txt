Easy Kimchi Pork Belly recipe and very shiok with rice! You get spiciness, sweetness, savoury and tang all in one dish.😋😋😋⁣
⁣
1️⃣Firstly, simmer the pork belly for 30 minutes and then slice it thinly. ⁣
2️⃣In a wok or pan with oil, fry sliced yellow onions and minced garlic for a few minutes. ⁣
3️⃣Add pork belly, sliced Kimchi, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, minced ginger and gochujang sauce.⁣
4️⃣Fry till fragrant and enjoy!😉
