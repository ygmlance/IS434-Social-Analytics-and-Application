🥣SOUP OF DAY
This soup helps to boost up immunity, clear phlegm and remove heatiness trap in the body
.
Ingredients
1 Snow Fungus with Lady Bell Soup Pack
500g soft pork bone/pork fillet 
1.2L water 
1 tbsp wolfberries, optional
.
Method
1. Rinse all the herbs and soaked white fungus for 12-20 minutes. Remove and tear into pieces
2. Blanch pork ribs and in a soup pot bring 1.2L of water to boil
3. Add in all ingredients (except wolfberries) and simmer on low heat on stove for at least 1.5 hours 
ALTERNATIVELY
- you can add everything in slow cooker and cook on auto mode for 2-3 hours
4. Lastly season to taste, add in wolfberries and serve
.
