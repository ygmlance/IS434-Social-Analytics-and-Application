This killer home Fat burner workout you can do everywhere , you just need a resistance band, a object to attach it and off course the will and perseverance to finish it.⁣
⁣
Don’t just go through the motion but Try to put all your effort in each rep you do!⁣
⁣
The work👇⁣
———⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣
✖️ Band jumps ⁣
3 x 10⁣
✖️ ⁣Band sprints ⁣
3 x 20 sec ⁣
✖️band crawl ⁣
3 x 10 ⁣
✖️ Band forward taps⁣
3 x 10 ⁣
✖️ Band side taps⁣
3 x 10 each side ⁣
⁣
———⁣⁣⁣⁣
⁣
👉 hit these exercises back to back with Minimal rest , if you finished the first set then take 1 min rest before going to the next one💪⁣
