名章的字数通常在2-4字之间，是篆刻中比较常见的。篆刻家在以诗文摘句入印之时则会碰到较高字数的印题，在排印过程中有了更高的难度。⁣
⁣
张财的这枚印章把“出”字拉长，以让印面对称协调。⁣
⁣
The most common form of seal, name seals, usually involves 2-4 characters. When the seal carver chooses to carve longer phrases (such as those from poems), the amount of characters poses a greater challenge to the seal’s composition. ⁣
⁣
Here, Chong Choy’s seal stretches the middle character to achieve a balanced symmetry.⁣
