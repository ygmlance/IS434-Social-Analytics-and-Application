Huge chocolate fan? You’ve got to try this out! 🍌🍫⁠
So good, it’s hard to believe this is 100% plant based 🌱 “It’s like goreng pisang in a chocolate bread cake without the outer breading” this is the perfect description to how it tastes!⁠
This will leave you coming back for more 😉⁠
Serves - 8-9⁠
⁠
𝐈𝐧𝐠𝐫𝐞𝐝𝐢𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐬:⁠
There are 3 types of flours you can choose to use for this⁠
1/4 scant cup coconut flour (used this)/2 scant cups almond flour/1 scant cup all-purpose flour⁠
2 large ripe bananas⁠
2 flax eggs (1 flax egg: 1 tbsp flax to 3 tbsp water, refrigerate for 15 mins to set)⁠
2 tsp @nuzest_singapore Good Green Stuff (makes it extra yummy and healthier!)⁠
1/2 cup coconut sugar⁠
1/2 cup cacao powder⁠
1 tsp baking powder⁠
1 tsp vanilla extract⁠
1/4 cup dark choc chips⁠
⁠
𝐌𝐞𝐭𝐡𝐨𝐝:⁠
Preheat oven to 180 degrees C and grease an 8 inch skillet with oil (for almond flour or all-purpose flour use 10-12” skillet)⁠
In a large bowl, whisk together bananas, eggs, vanilla extract, coconut oil and coconut sugar⁠
Mix in the almond flour, cacao powder and baking powder until smooth.⁠
Fold in dark chocolate chips then add batter to skillet and bake in oven for 45-50minutes (or until toothpick comes out clean)⁠
Dig in and enjoy!⁠
