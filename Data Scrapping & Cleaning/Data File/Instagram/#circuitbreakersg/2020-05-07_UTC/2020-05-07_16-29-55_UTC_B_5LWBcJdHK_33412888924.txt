Uni Spaghetti with Russian Caviar 
1. Cook pasta in pot of hot water
2. Medium high heat pan, 2 tbsp butter in, 100ml milk in, cook and mix well 
3. 2. tbsp Japanese Uni in, cook and mix well. Season with salt to taste 
4. Once pasta 90% cooked, put pasta into butter milk mix, cook and mix for 1 min
5. Pasta on plate, 2 tbsp Japanese Uni on top of pasta, 2 tsp caviar on top 
6. Drizzle remaining sauce on top
Serve
