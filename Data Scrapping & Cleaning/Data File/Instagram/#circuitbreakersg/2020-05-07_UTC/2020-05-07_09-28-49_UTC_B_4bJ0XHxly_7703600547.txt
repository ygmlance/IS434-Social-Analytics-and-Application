Big Box of Little Word Puzzles 
Play with 180 word and photo puzzle pieces and learn how to spell 60 consonant-vowel-consonant words. Help children learn to read and spell 60 consonant-vowel-consonant (CVC) words with Big Box of Little Word Puzzles. The game set includes 60 three-piece puzzles that form words and photographic representations when each is assembled correctly. It also supports NAEYC standards.

Features & details
180 word and photo puzzle pieces
Self checking
Learn to identify initial and final consonant sounds
