Japanese rice is commonly cooked plain for everyday meals, whether it is for Japanese curry or donburi rice bowls. 
When it comes to sushi, the rice is steamed and flavoured with vinegar-based seasonings. In Japan, this vinegared rice is known as “sumeshi” and it is only used for making sushi.
