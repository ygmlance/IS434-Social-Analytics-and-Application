Easy recipe ➡️ Spicy Lala:⁣
Ingredients:⁣
🔴 Fresh Lala⁣
🔴 Minced garlic⁣
🔴 Sugarless sambal ⁣
🔴 Parsley ⁣
⁣
⁣
⁣
Instructions:⁣
1. Heat wok, add pork lard. ⁣
2. Fry garlic till fragrant. ⁣
3. Fry sambal. ⁣
4. Pour Lala in and give it a stir. ⁣
5. Lala is ready when all open up. ⁣
6. Garnish with parsley. ⁣
