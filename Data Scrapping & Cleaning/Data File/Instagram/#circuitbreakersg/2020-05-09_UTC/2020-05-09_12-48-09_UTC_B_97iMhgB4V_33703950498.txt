A simple exercise to improve hip stability in the frontal plane, especially if you find yourself falling sideways when balancing on one foot. Strengthening the gluteus medius muscle is also important to prevent injuries when engaging in lower limb activities
.
Additional tips:
- Lay your head on your arm or a pillow for more comfort
- Keep your core neutral and engaged during the exercise
- Supplement this exercise atop hip abduction and close-chain leg exercises to enhance effectiveness .
Beginner tips:
- Start without using the band
- Lay your back against the wall if you are struggling with keeping your back stable (but remember to train your core more :)
.
