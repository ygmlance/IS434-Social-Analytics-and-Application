CIRCUIT BREAKER MEALS (DINNER RECIPE):
STIR FRY GINGER SCALLION FISH
#omxdefoodieVERSION

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED:
GINGER (FINELY CHOPPED & SLICED)
SCALLIONS
TIOMAN FISH
OYSTER SAUCE
LIGHT SOYA SAUCE
HUA DIAO JIU
GROUNDED WHITE PEPPER
SESAME OIL
CORN FLOUR

MARINATE THE FISHüêü
1. SLICE THE FISH
2. ADD:
SOY SAUCE (2 TABLE SPOON)
3 DASHES OF PEPPER
SESAME OIL (1 TEASPOON)
HUA DIAO JIU (1 TEASPOON)
CORN FLOUR (2 TEASPOON)
3. MIX WELL

TIME TO COOK!
1. FRY THE CHOPPED GINGER AND SET IT ASIDE
2. ADD IN SLICED GINGER, FRY TILL FRAGRANT
3. ADD IN MARINATED FISH, STIR FRY TILL ALMOST COOKED
4. ADD IN SCALLIONS
5. ADD LIGHT SOY SAUCE & OYSTER SAUCE (APPROX 1 TABLE SPOON EACH)
6. ADD A DASH OF SESAME OIL AND MIX WELL
7. FINALLY ADD IN A MIXTURE OF CORNFLOUR AND WATER
8. SERVE AND TOP IT OFF WITH THE FRIED MINCED GINGER
