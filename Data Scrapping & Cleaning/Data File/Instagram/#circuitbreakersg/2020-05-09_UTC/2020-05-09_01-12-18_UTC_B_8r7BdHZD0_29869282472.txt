【Penang Hokkien Char】⁣⁣
⁣⁣
Love eating Hokkien Char, flavoring the noodles with the pungent and spicy sambal. Tasty to max!⁣⁣
⁣⁣
INGREDIENTS ⁣⁣
▫️Rice vermicelli, soak in water until soft⁣⁣
▫️Low alkaline Hokkien noodles, rinsed and drained⁣⁣
▫️Fish @9sseafood , sliced⁣⁣
▫️Pork shoulder, sliced⁣⁣
▫️Green vegetables ⁣⁣
▫️2 cloves garlic, minced⁣⁣
▫️2 tbsp light soy sauce ⁣⁣
▫️1 tbsp dark soy sauce⁣⁣
▫️1/2 tsp brown sugar⁣⁣
▫️1 tbsp shaoxing ⁣⁣
▫️3/4 water⁣⁣
▫️Fried shallot crisps ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
Marinating fish and pork:⁣⁣
▫️1 tsp light soy sauce ⁣⁣
▫️1 tsp sesame oil⁣⁣
▫️1 tsp @liloikanbilis mushroom powder⁣⁣
⁣⁣
INSTRUCTIONS ⁣⁣
1️⃣Marinate pork with seasoning as mentioned above for 15 minutes.⁣⁣
⁣⁣
2️⃣Heat the pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.⁣⁣
⁣⁣
3️⃣Add the pork slices, fish into the pan and stir-fry until they are half-cooked. Add dark and light soy sauce, brown sugar and water in. Mix well. ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
4️⃣Add rice vermicelli and noodles and toss well. Add shaoxing. ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
5️⃣Add in green vegetables. Quick stir with fried shallot crisps. ⁣⁣
⁣⁣
