Methods
1. Boiled the pork belly with ginger and soy sauce
2. Chopped pork into small cubes, and stir fried till fragrance with added ginger and garlic
3. Fried till golden brown
4. Add in your preferred marination such as sesame oil, soy sauce, dark soy, oyster sauce, Chinese wine, oil and some water.
5. Cook over high heat till it becomes sticky.
