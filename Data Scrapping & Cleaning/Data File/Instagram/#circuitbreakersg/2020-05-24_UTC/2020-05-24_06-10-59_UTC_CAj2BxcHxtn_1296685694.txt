Egg & Cheese Rosti Galette, my girl‘s improvised creation for our breakfast. Super delicious!!!👍👍😋😋😋.
.
Here’s sharing her creation:.
. 【𝐄𝐠𝐠 & 𝐂𝐡𝐞𝐞𝐬𝐞 𝐑𝐨𝐬𝐭𝐢 𝐆𝐚𝐥𝐞𝐭𝐭𝐞 𝐬𝐡𝐚𝐫𝐞𝐝 𝐛𝐲 𝐁𝐚𝐤𝐢𝐧𝐠 𝐓𝐚𝐢𝐭𝐚𝐢】- please watch cooking video above for reference.
.

𝙄𝙣𝙜𝙧𝙚𝙙𝙞𝙚𝙣𝙩𝙨 𝙛𝙤𝙧 𝘾𝙧𝙚𝙥𝙚: (makes 4 pieces)
240ml fresh milk
60g Blue Jacket all purpose flour
1 x 60g egg 
1/8 tsp salt.
.

Whisk the ingredients well together until smooth. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan and scoop 1/4 batter in. Tilt pan with circular motion so that batter costs surface evenly. Cook till both sides are slightly brown and it’s done.
.

𝙄𝙣𝙜𝙧𝙚𝙙𝙞𝙚𝙣𝙩𝙨 𝙛𝙤𝙧 𝘾𝙝𝙚𝙚𝙨𝙚 𝙍𝙤𝙨𝙩𝙞: (makes 4) 
2 big Russet potatoes 
2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
1/4 tsp salt 
shredded cheddar cheese.
.

Cut the potatoes into thin matchsticks using a julienne attachment on a mandoline. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan. Season the potatoes with salt and cook until soft. Shape into round flat pancakes and cook until golden brown. Placed shredded cheese on top of each rosti and cooked till it melts.
.

4 eggs cooked sunny-side up style .
.

𝘼𝙨𝙨𝙚𝙢𝙗𝙡𝙮:
Place crepe onto a plate after cooked, followed by cheesy rosti and sunny side up. Fold in the 4 sides of crepe and sprinkle some dried parsley over it. Serve and enjoy!
