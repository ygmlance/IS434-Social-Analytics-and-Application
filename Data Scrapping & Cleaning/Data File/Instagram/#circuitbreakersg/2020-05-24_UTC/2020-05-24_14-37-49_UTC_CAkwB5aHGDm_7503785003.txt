CIRCUIT BREAKER MEALS:
COLD SOMEN WITH UNAGI
#omxdefoodieVERSION

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED!
1. SOMEN
2. FROZEN UNAGI
3. CABBAGE
4. EGG
5. SOBA SAUCE
6. GARLIC
7. CONCENTRATED DASHI
8. SEAWEED / SEAWEED SEASONING (OPTIONAL)

1. WRAP FROZEN UNAGI IN FOIL AND BAKE FOR 30MINS
2. ADD SALT AND 1 TBS OF OIL TO WATER, BRING TO BOIL
3. ADD SOMEN INTO BOILING WATER
4. ONCE COOKED RISE SOMEN UNDER TAP WATER / ADD SOMEN INTO ICE BATH
5. STIR FRY CABBAGE WITH GARLIC AND ADD DASHI (1-2TBS, ADJUST TO YOUR PREFERENCE)
6. BEAT EGGS, PAN FRY IN PAN
7. ROLL THE EGGS AND CUT THEM
8. POUR SOBA SAUCE INTO SEPARATE CUP, ADD WATER (2:1 RATIO, ADJUST TO YOUR PREFERENCE)
9. PLATE AND EAT!
