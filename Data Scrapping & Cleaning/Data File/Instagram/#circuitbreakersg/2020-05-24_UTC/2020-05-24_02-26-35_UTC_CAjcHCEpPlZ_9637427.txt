Here’s a video on how to bake these light, fluffy, burger buns at home .
Ingredients you’ll need: 
35g / 3 tablespoons sugar 
7g / 2 tsp of instant yeast 
45g / 3 tablespoons of milk 
236g / 1 cup of water
2 eggs 
50g / 3 tablespoons of butter 
360g / 3 cups of bread flour 
10g / 2 tsp of salt .
Enjoy!
