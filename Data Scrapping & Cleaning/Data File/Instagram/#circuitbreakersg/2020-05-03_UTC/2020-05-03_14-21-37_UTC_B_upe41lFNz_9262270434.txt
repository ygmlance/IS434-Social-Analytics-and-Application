Wat Arun 📍Bangkok, Thailand
..
Wat Arun, one of the most beautiful temple in bangkok also known as temple of dawn. It is located on the banks of Chao Phraya river.
..
Its absolutely stunning at sunset, particularly when lit up at night, even on day light its quite beautiful.
..
You can also take the river cruise at late evening, which takes through some popular landmarks illuminated at night
..
Architecture and craftsmanship of this temple is stunning, beautifully decorated with tiny pieces of coloured glass and Chinese porcelain.
