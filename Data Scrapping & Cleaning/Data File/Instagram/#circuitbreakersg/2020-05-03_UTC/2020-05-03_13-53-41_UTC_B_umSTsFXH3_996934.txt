#circuitbreakerday27 // A gift from some bird’s morning toilet, a beautiful thing from base beginnings. This #murayapaniculata lives up to its Chinese name - #七里香 (seven mile fragrance). After the night’s rain, its lily-jasmine fragrance permeates the air for several miles around. .
Am torn between attempting to capture (poorly) this heady scent in a bottle, and just enjoying its ephemeral perfume.
