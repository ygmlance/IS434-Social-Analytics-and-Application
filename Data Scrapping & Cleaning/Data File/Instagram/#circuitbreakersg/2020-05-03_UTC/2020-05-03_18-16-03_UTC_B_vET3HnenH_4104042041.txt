When comparing specific heat capacities of different states/phases, compare the slopes! The steeper the slope means it takes lesser time to heat up and hence the specific heat capacity is smaller. (Smaller “capacity” to store heat).
.
To compare specific latent heat, look at how long it takes to change state. The longer it takes the greater the specific latent heat of that state/phase change.
