Homemade Char Siew

#charsiew

Ingredients
2 slabs pork collar
2 tbsp dark soy
2 tbsp light soy
1 dash pepper
1 dash salt
1 dash 5 spice powder
3 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
2 clove chop garlic
2 pc chop ginger

Methods
1. Chop into long slices
2. Marinate few hours to overnight with all the marination
3. Place into oven 180DegC 20min both side, final 5min of each side use grill mode.

Note
- for glossy effect, coat honey mixed warm water on pork surface every few minutes towards the end.
- fatty piece of meat would be better, such as pork belly skin removed.
