1. For the pastry: Sift flour, sugar & salt. Add in softened butter and bring mixture together using hands. (DO NOT over knead or pastry will be tough).
.
2. Add in beaten egg to form a dough. Cling wrap then refrigerate for 30 mins till firm. .
3. For the custard filling: Melt sugar & salt in hot water. Let cool. .
4. Add in eggs, sugar water, evaporated milk & vanilla paste. Stir & combine well. .
5. Strain the mixture to ensure no lumps. Chill in fridge. .
6. Preheat the oven top and bottom heat 200Â°C.
.
7. Divide dough into 13 portions. Take a portion, roll round, flatten, place in tart shell and press around with fingers. .
8. Pour custard filling into the shells until about 80% full. .
9. Bake for 15-20 mins until a toothpick insert can stand in the egg tart. .
