Pulled Pork Burger
•
We don’t have a slow cooker therefore I was using rice cooker to cook this dish. More work to monitor them but definitely worth the trouble. With whole-wheat buns, this dish is husband approved. •

- Onion, garlic
- Salt, Brown sugar

- 1 bottle of Apple Cider - 2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
- Smoked Paprika Powder
