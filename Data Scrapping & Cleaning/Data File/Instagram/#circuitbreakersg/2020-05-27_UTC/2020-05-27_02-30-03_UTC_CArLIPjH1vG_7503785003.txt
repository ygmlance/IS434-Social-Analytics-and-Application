CIRCUIT BREAKER MEALS:
MENTAIKO PRAWNS
#omxdefoodieVERSION

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED!
1) PRAWNS (I AM USING XXL TIGER PRAWNS)
2) MENTAIKO
3) KEWPIE MAYO
4) SALT

1) CUT PRAWNS AND DEVEIN
2) MARINATE WITH 0.5 TEASPOON OF SALT AND SET ASIDE
3) PLACE THE PRAWNS ONTO THE BAKING TRY AND SPRINKLE SOME SALT OVER
4) PREHEAT OVEN AND BAKE PRAWNS AT 200DEG FOR ABOUT 15-20 MINS (TILL COOKED)
5) REMOVE MENTAIKO FROM ITS SAC, ADD KEWPIE MAYO AND MIX (CHECK OUT MY VIDEO FOR IDEAL CONSISTENCY)
6) REMOVE PRAWNS FROM OVEN AND SPREAD THE SAUCE ON THE PRAWNS
7) TORCH THE PRAWNS WITH A BLOWTORCH
8) ENJOY!

NO BLOWTORCH? NO WORRIES!
ADD MENTAIKO TO THE PRAWNS WHEN THEY ARE ABOUT 70-80% COOKED AND CONTINUE BAKING!

NOTE: THIS METHOD WILL CAUSE THE MENTAIKO SAUCE TO BE ON THE DRIER SIDE!
