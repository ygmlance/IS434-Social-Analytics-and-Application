Fast Fried Mee Tai Mak
.
Ingredients:
250g of Minced Pork
2tbsp #kwongwohhing Steam Fish Sauce
Cracked White Pepper
(Marinate this 3 together and leave aside for an hour)
.
2 pkts of Mee Tai Mak (abt 800grms)
1/2 tbsp of chopped garlic
3 sprigs of Spring Onions slice 2cm slant 
2 Red Chilli, deseeded and slice
1 pkt of Fish Balls, halved
3 tbsp of Cooking Oil
1 cup of Ikan Billis (dried Anchovies) optional
.
Method:
Add oil to pan and heat up
Add Ikan Billis and fry till dry and crispy. Remove and set aside.
.
Oil into a heated pan again. 
Add garlic, fry till fragrant
Add mince pork, fry till 3/4 cooked
Add halved fishballs, fry to cooked
Add Mee Tai Mak & mix well gently, don’t overfry/overcook. 
Taste. Add light sauce if needed otherwise, turn off the fire, add the spring onions and Chillies and mix well. Sprinkle with fried Ikan Billis and it’s ready to serve.
.
