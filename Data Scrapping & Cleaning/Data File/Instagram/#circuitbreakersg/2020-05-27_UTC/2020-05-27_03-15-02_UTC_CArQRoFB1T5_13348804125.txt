Fret not😃. By default, sound from the Raspberry Pi goes through HDMI to your audio/visual device like the LCD monitor.🖥️ Just update the settings to direct sound out to the audio jack!🔊
.
.
Here are the setup instructions: https://www.raspberrypi.org/documentation/configuration/audio-config.md
