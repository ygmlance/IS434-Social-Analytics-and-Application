Steam Salted Fish Minced Pork⁣
Ingredients :⁣
⚪ Fresh minced pork⁣
⚪ Salted fish⁣
⚪ Freshly grated ginger⁣
⚪ Fish sauce⁣
⚪ White pepper⁣
⚪ Chilli padi⁣
⚪ Spring onion⁣
⁣
⁣
Instructions :⁣
1. Seasoned minced pork with a dash of pepper & some fish sauce.⁣
2. Fry salted fish, shred them and pour into pork mixture. ⁣
3. Add freshly grated ginger⁣
4. Top with chopped chilli padi and steam. ( 😂 I didn't time, use this time to cook fish.)⁣
5. Garnish with chopped spring onion before serving.⁣
