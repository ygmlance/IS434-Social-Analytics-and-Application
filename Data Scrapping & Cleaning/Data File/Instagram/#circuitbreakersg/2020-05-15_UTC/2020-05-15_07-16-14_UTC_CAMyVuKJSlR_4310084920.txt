The said brown butter pecan bread realised ..
.
200 g flour (13% whole spelt, 87% Japanese bread flour) 
58g pecan (29%, happens to be all that I hv left in one bag)
Brown sugar 6.3% (dissolved in water)
Brown butter 7%
Salt 2%
Starter 18%
Water 70% 
Handmixed, 30 min ‘autolyse’, 4.5 hr bulk, 15 hr cold proof. Lowish temp bake at 190C. (Adapted from the demi baguette recipe in the Bouchon Bakery cookbook)
