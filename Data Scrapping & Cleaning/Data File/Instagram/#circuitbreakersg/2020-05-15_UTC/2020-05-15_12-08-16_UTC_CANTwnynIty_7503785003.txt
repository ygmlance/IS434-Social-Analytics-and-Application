CABONARA

INGREDIENTS YOU NEED:
GARLIC (CHOPPED)
BACON
BUTTER
MOZZARELLA & CHEDDAR (SHREDDED)
PARMESAN
HEAVY CREAM / WHIPPING CREAM
SPAGHETTI
EGG YOLK
PEPPER
