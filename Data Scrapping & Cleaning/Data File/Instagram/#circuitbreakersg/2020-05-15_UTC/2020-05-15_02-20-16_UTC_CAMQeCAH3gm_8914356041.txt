INGREDIENTS 
100g rice vermicelli, soaked
1 pack @primataste Mee Siam goreng paste
6-8 medium prawns, shells removed
1/2 cup beansprouts, tail removed
1 small firm beancurd, cut into cubes
1 piece bean curd puff, cut into cubes
20-30g Chinese chives, cut into sections
2 garlic cloves, minced
3 shallot, sliced
1-2 eggs
150ml water or chicken stock
lime wedges
extra sambal chilli, optional
.
Method
1. Soaked vermicelli in warm water for about 10 minutes, drained well and set aside
2. In a skillet sauteÌ firm beancurd and beancurd puff till brown on all sides. Remove and set aside
3. Use one egg to make omelette the shredded and set aside or you can use 2 eggs to make hard-boiled or omelette
4. SauteÌ minced garlic and shallots with prawns till aromatic, remove the prawn then add in Mee Siam paste and fry with the prawn shell for 30 seconds
5. Add water and let it simmer for a minute or two on low heat before adding vermicelli and cook for another 2 minutes
6. Lastly add in beansprouts, chives, prawns, beancurd and puff. Mix well, cover and simmer another minute or 2
7. Serve with prepared egg, lime and extra sambal chilli
