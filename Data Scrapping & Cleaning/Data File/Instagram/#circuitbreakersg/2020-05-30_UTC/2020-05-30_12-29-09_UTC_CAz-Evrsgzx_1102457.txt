Steamed eggs with spam 
3 eggs (about 180g)
230ml water
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp wine
A pinch of sugar

1/3 can spam (finely diced)
Some spring onion & fried shallot for garnishing

1. Prepare and standby steamer.
2. Mix the eggs well with seasoning and add spam. 
3. Cover with foil and steam for 15 min at medium low fire.
4. Garnish with spring onion and fried shallot to serve. *you can sieve it for a smoother egg but today I am lazy ðŸ˜‚
