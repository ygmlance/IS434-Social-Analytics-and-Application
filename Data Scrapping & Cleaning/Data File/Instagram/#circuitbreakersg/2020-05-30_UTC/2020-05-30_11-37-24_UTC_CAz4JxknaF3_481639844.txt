The Daily Scoop | Flavour Spotlight

Pulut Hitam, Orh-Bee-Bay, Bee Koh Moy or Black Glutinous Rice? What name does this well loved dessert go by in your family?

This week’s flavour in the spotlight is our spin on the Asian dessert we have aptly named Pur-Pearl Rice.

We churned up a tasty ‘lemak’ coconut ice cream, slow cooked black glutinous rice with Grandma’s recipe and swirled the two together for a glorious gem of a dessert.
