Guidelines to prevent a pandemic in Islam

In this 4-minute video, RRG Co-Chairman Ust Hj Ali Hj Mohamed, shares his thoughts on how Muslims should respond to a pandemic. More importantly, Muslims should heed the advice of medical professionals, policymakers and the national fatwa committee such as from MUIS.

Panduan untuk mengharungi wabak

Dalam video 4 minit ini, pengerusi-bersama RRG, Ust Hj Ali Hj Mohamed, berkongsi pendapatnya tentang bagaimana umat Islam harus bertindak-balas terhadap sesuatu wabak. Yang paling utama, umat Islam harus memperhatikan nasihat dan arahan dari pakar perubatan, kerajaan dan jawatankuasa fatwa nasional seprti MUIS.
