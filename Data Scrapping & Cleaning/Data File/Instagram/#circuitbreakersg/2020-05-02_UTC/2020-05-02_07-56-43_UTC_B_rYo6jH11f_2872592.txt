We’ve made vanilla chocolate chips melting cookies. It was fun and enjoyable time spent in the kitchen. There are not pretty because kids prefer to have it in different shapes and sizes but there were all super yummy!
.
[Ingredients]
- 3 1/4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/3 cups butter, softened
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 4 teaspoons vanilla essence
- 1/2 pack of Hersheys semi-sweet chocolate chips
.
[Method]
- Preheat oven to 190°C. Mix flour, baking soda and salt in a bowl. Set aside. - Beat butter and sugars in large bowl until light and fluffy. Slowly add eggs and vanilla and mix well. Slowly beat in flour mixture on low speed until well mixed. Stir in chocolate chips.
- Drop by rounded tablespoons about 2 inches apart onto baking sheets.
- Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on wire racks.
