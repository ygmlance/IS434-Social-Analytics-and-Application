🥚Homemade miso based ramen with Grilled Chicken and Ramen eggs! Don’t you love your eggs with yolks that are runny?! These Ramen eggs are to die for. I can eat 3-4 at one seating🥰 It’s not hard to make them at home, you just have to set aside time for the egg to absorb the flavors in the soy sauce marinade⁣
⁣
Ingredients:⁣
💗4-6 eggs at room temp⁣
💗1 cup water⁣
💗1/3cup light soy sauce⁣
💗1/3cup mirin⁣
💗1 tbsp sugar⁣
⁣
Steps:⁣
1)In a saucepan, add water, soy sauce, mirin and sugar and bring to a boil. Set aside and cook completely⁣
2)In another saucepan, bring water to a boil then add eggs in gently and boil for 6mins⁣
3)Prepare a bowl of ice water to submerge the eggs in after 6 mins to stop the cooking process⁣
4)Leave in ice water to cool for 30mins before gently peeling off the shell⁣
5)Add eggs into soy sauce mixture and refrigerate for at least 6 hours⁣
