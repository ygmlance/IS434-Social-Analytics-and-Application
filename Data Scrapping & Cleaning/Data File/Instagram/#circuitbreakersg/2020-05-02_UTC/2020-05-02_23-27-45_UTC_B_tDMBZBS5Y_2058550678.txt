Lockdown day 18 tasting @ministryofcloudswines I’ve been craving for something refreshing, fresh and crisp for the past non-alcohol diet days and think I’ve had it enough with non-alcohol therapy now 😝 So I popped a bottle of Ministry of Clouds 2019 Clare Valley Riesling. Small white flowers, kaffir lime, citrus on the nose. On palate I found it quite juicy (juicier, fruitier than 2017, 2018) and loved the vibrant acidity and chalky minerality with long finish ! I had paired it with mildly spiced Cajun chicken and shrimp tacos with fresh tomatoes and guacamole ! It was perfect drinking wine to start up happy (?) lockdown life and appetising your palate 🥂 Chloe’s Score 3.8/5 available @straitswinecompany ⠀
