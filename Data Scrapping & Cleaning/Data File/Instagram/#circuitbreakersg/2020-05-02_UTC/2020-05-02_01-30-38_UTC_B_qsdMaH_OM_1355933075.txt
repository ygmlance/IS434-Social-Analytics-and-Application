Almond Mlk Tips!

Prepare your favourite cold brew coffee into convenient ice cubes that you can drop into your iced almond milk latte! Adjust the strength of your coffee by the number of coffee cubes you dunk in. Add maple syrup for a little sweetness if you wish. And enjoy!
