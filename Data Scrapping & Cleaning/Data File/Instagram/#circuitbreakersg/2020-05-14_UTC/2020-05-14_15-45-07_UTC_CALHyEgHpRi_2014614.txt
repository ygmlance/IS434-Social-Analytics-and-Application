𝐂𝐁 𝐃𝐚𝐲 𝟑𝟖 - Running out of ideas for quick lunch fix, so here’s a bowl of to-Thai-for 𝘾𝙧𝙚𝙖𝙢𝙮 𝙏𝙤𝙢 𝙔𝙪𝙢 𝙉𝙤𝙤𝙙𝙡𝙚𝙨 just cos I needed to use the Tom Yum herbs set that has been sitting in my fridge for the past week or so. 🤭⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣
𝟏𝟎-𝐌𝐢𝐧𝐮𝐭𝐞𝐬 𝐂𝐫𝐞𝐚𝐦𝐲 𝐓𝐨𝐦 𝐘𝐮𝐦 𝐍𝐨𝐨𝐝𝐥𝐞𝐬 (serves 2)⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣
𝙄𝙣𝙜𝙧𝙚𝙙𝙞𝙚𝙣𝙩𝙨⁣⁣⁣
1 @dancingchef_sg Tom Yum Paste ⁣⁣⁣
1 Tom Yum Herbs Set ⁣⁣⁣
@ayambrandsg Coconut Milk⁣⁣⁣
450-500ml water⁣⁣⁣
Fish sauce ⁣⁣⁣
*Prawns⁣⁣⁣
*Squid⁣⁣⁣
*Pork ⁣⁣⁣
*Cherry Tomatoes ⁣⁣⁣
2 @nuyolksg egg yolk ⁣⁣⁣
2 Instant Noodles (No MAMA at home so I used Cintan)⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣
*Optional and can be changed to your preferred ingredients. Also, I prepared my ingredients the night before. ⁣⁣⁣
⁣⁣⁣
𝙈𝙚𝙩𝙝𝙤𝙙⁣⁣⁣
🍜In a pot, add water, Tom Yum paste, everything (except kaffir lime leaves and lime) in the Tom Yum Herbs set and bring to a boil. ⁣⁣⁣
🍜 Once boiled, add kaffir lime leaves, pork slices, prawns, squid and cherry tomatoes. ⁣⁣⁣
🍜 Once ingredients are cooked, take them out of the pot and set aside. ⁣⁣⁣
🍜Add a splash of fish sauce and coconut milk to the soup. ⁣
🍜 Cook instant noodles till desired texture ⁣⁣⁣
🍜Serve the noodles with an egg yolk⁣⁣⁣
