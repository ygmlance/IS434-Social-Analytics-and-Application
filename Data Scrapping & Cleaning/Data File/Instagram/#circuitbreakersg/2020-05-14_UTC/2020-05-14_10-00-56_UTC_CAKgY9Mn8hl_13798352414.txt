Bok Choy with Oyster Sauce
Bok Choy (Xiao Bai Cai) - 250g⠀
Garlic - 7 clove⠀
Mushroom Oyster Sauce - UP TO YOU!⠀
Salt, Pepper & Sugar⠀
Oil (Olive & Sesame)⠀
⠀
⠀
Boil bok choy for a minute >  rinse boy choy in ice water & plate it >  heat oil > add oyster sauce > add water > mix well > add salt, pepper & sugar > mix well and pour on plated boy choy > heat oil > add garlic and saute in low-medium heat till golden brown > done!⠀
⠀
⠀
