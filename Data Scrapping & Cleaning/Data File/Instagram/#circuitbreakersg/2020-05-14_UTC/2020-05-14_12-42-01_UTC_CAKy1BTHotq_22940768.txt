Baking notes:
125g fed active starter
370g water
450g plain flour
50g wholemeal flour
Autolyse for 1hour
10g sea salt
25g water
Bulk rise 24hours in fridge
6 turns of "stretch n fold" 1 hour interval
Shape, place in cast iron pot, dust top with flour, slash, preheat oven
Bake in covered cast iron pot 250C for 40min
Bake without cover 220C for 40min
Remove from pot, cool completely before slicing.
