Recipe for Sandwich Loaf using sourdough starter⁣
⁣
𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤: ⁣
340g Bread flour (Use 350g if make plain loaf)⁣
10g Taro Powder ⁣
30g Sugar⁣
150g Water ⁣
240g sourdough starter (Levain)⁣
1/2tsp salt⁣
20g butter ⁣
⁣
𝕊𝕥𝕖𝕡𝕤:⁣
1. Add bread flour, sugar, water and sourdough starter in a mixing bowl/bread machine. Knead for 10 minutes.⁣ 2. Add in salt, butter and Taro powder. Continue knead till smooth and achieve window pan.⁣
3. Remove dough from mixer/BM and shape the dough into a ball.⁣
4. Let rest in a bowl and covered it with cling wrap. Proof for 1hr to 1hr 30min or until it has double in size. (I proof in the microwave at 40C)⁣
5. Gently deflate the dough, fold and shape. ⁣
6. Place the dough into a bread tin. (Don’t cover the lid) Leave it for final proofing for 30minutes to 40minutes or till double in size. (I proof it in the microwave at 40C)⁣
7. Bake in a preheated oven 180°C middle rack for 25 minutes.⁣
8. Remove to cool on rack immediately after baking.⁣
9. You can eat it plain or spread it with your favorite jam/butter.⁣
