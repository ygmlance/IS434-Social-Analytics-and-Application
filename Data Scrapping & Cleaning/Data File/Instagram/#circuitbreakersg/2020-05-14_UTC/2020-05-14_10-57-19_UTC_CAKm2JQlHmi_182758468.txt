Really enjoy shopping at @theladyboymarketplace , so here is a recipe for you to cook during this circuit breaker. Hope you enjoy this recipe! ❤️ Pan-fried Salmon with Asparagus on a Bed of Garlic Mashed Potato

Ingredients
For Salmon:
- 1 Salmon fillet
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp Black pepper
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder (optional)
- 1/4 tsp Paprika (optional)
- 1 tsp oil

For Garlic mashed potato:
- 1 Potato, peeled & cubed
- 1 clove of garlic, peeled
- 1 tsp salt
- 20g butter
- 80 ml milk

For asparagus:
- about 10 stalks of Asparagus, trimmed
- pinch of salt
- pinch of pepper

Steps
1. Season a pot of water with salt and boil the potato & garlic. Cook for about 20 mins until you can easily pierce with a fork.
. 
2. While waiting for the potato, pat dry salmon with kitchen towel. Season salmon with salt, black pepper, garlic powder & Paprika.
. 
3. When the potato are done, drain the potato and garlic. Mash the potato & garlic. Put in butter and mix well. Over low heat, add milk slowly to the mashed potato and mix well until the consistency you prefer. If required, add more salt to taste.
. 
4. In a pan, heat up oil. Put in the salmon with the skin side down and cook for about 5 mins until crispy. In the same pan, put in the asparagus and season with salt and pepper. Flip the salmon and sear the other side for about 4 mins.

To serve, place mashed potato onto a plate, then place asparagus on top of mashed potato. And finally, place salmon on top of asparagus.
