Cos d’Estournel 1996 tasted on the last day of May in 2020. Ruby, Purple colour with pinkish rim. Clean, clear with some sediment. Aromas of cassis, plum, dark berries, cranberries, blueberry jam, violet flower intertwined with chocolate, tobacco, coffee, toffee, melted tar and graphite. 1996 Cos d’Estournel was aged in 65% new oak (unlike 1995 was 100% new oak), a blend of 65% Cabernet Sauvignon and 35 % Merlot. Medium bodied yet dense and focused with long finish on the palate. Acidity is medium to high and well-balanced with mellow tannin. Very good example of sublime Cabernet blend of 1996 !
