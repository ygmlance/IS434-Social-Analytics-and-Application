Maakouda is a sort of Moroccan potato beignet.
Here I made a chessy Maakouda filling in a crusty homemade baguette.
They are super fluffy and smooth on the inside and crusty on the outside. Maakouda in a sandwich is one of the most common street foods in Morocco. When i was kid I used to buy maakouda sandwiche for less than $1. The sandwich usually comes with lemony tomato sauce. Sooooo tasty & yummy
