Our salted egg porridge with pan-fried minced meat patties -肉饼 recipe by our mum. My late grandma and mum always cook this dish whenever we have plain porridge for meal. Simple and easy recipe that everyone can do it! Best to dip with tomato ketchup or chilli sauce..Yummy!.
Tips: Use 水豆腐 for smoother texture.
INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Chop spring onions with minced meat and beancurd together
2. Marinate with salt, pepper and soy sauce, add corn flour and 1 egg. Mixed them well
3. Scoop with spoon/ free and easy to fry in medium heat until golden brown. #hzcooks #hzfooddiary #肉饼
