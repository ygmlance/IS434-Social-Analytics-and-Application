Seafood Pineapple Fried Rice using brown rice
Ingredients: 
8 Shrimp (leave 4 whole, and cut the rest into small pieces) and 2 squid and cut into pieces
Pork slices 
2 egg
2 cups of rice 
1/2 cup small diced onions
1.5 Tbsp Thai soy sauce
1.5 tsp fish sauce
1.5 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp curry powder and 1 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp White pepper
1 cup Pineapple, fresh if you can, cut into small pieces
1/2 cup roasted or fried cashews, unsalted2 green onions, chopped
¬Ω cup tomatoes, seeds removed, cut into half inch cubes
Cucumber slices for serving

Methods 
In a large frying pan, heat 2 Tbsp of vegetable oil until hot. Sear the whole shrimp until done; remove and set aside. In the same pan, add the pork slices and squid, cook until done; remove and set aside.
Add more oil to the wok if needed, then add the eggs and scramble slightly. When the eggs is half way cooked, add the rice and toss to mix. Add the diced onions and toss to mix. Add curry powder, turmeric powder white pepper, sugar, salt, soy sauce, fish sauce, toss until the rice grains are separated and the seasoning is well mixed. Add the pineapple, cashews, pork slices and squid toss until the pineapples start to darken slightly and the rice is dry. Turn off the heat and toss in tomatoes and green onions.
Serve on a plate or in a pineapple bowl with some cucumber slices. Top with the whole shrimp and extra green onions.
