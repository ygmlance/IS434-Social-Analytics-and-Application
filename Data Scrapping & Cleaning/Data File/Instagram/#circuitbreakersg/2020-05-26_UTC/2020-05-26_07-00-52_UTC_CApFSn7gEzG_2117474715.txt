𝐏𝐞𝐫𝐫𝐨𝐓𝐈𝐏𝐒: 4 dental tips for cats! 🐱🦷⁠
1. 𝐋𝐞𝐚𝐫𝐧 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐬𝐢𝐠𝐧𝐬 𝐨𝐟 𝐝𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐚𝐥 𝐝𝐢𝐬𝐞𝐚𝐬𝐞. Some symptoms are: ⁠
- Bad breath⁠
- Excessive drooling⁠
- Even though your cat looks hungry, your cat does not want to eat⁠
- Inflamed or bleeding gum. Cat's gum should be pink!⁠
⁠
2. Purchase a 𝐩𝐞𝐭 𝐝𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐚𝐥 𝐤𝐢𝐭 which consist of a proper cat toothbrush and pet-safe toothpaste.⁠
⁠
