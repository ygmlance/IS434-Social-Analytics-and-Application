Black Sesame Keto Buns with Bordier Smoked Salt Butter & 🍋curd with a cup of coffee for brunch.⁣⁣
⁣⁣
⁣⁣
Dry ingredients :⁣⁣
🔸150g Black sesame powder⁣
🔸28g Psyllium husk powder⁣⁣
🔸0.5 tsp double acting baking powder or 1 tsp baking powder⁣
🔸0.5 tsp pink salt⁣⁣
⁣⁣
Wet ingredients :⁣⁣
🔹3 egg whites⁣⁣
🔹2 tsp ACV⁣⁣
🔹200ml hot water⁣⁣
⁣⁣
⁣⁣
Instructions :⁣⁣
1. Preheat oven 180 degree.⁣⁣
2. Mix all dry ingredients till well combined.⁣⁣
3. Add all wet ingredients and mix into a dough then rest 5 mins and let it firm up. (No need mixer at all)⁣⁣
4. Wet hands & divide dough (I divided to 9 mini ones).⁣⁣
5. Place dough on baking paper and sprinkle sesame seeds (Optional)⁣⁣
6. Bake at lowest rack for 35 mins.⁣⁣
