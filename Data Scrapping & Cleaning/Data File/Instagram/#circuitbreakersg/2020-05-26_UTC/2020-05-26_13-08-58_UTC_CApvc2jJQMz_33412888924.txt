Spicy Chicken Hotpot 奇味鸡

1. Marinate chicken - 1kg chicken - 2 tbsp oyster sauce - 3 tbsp dark soy sauce - 2 tbsp light soy sauce - 2 tbsp chinese hua diao wine
- Marinate for 1 hr or more

2. Hot pan with 3 tbsp oil, add in the following ingredients
- 2 tbsp sesame oil - 2 tsp Sze chuan pepper corn
- palm size ginger sliced
- 12 piece garlic cloves
- 6 chili padi
- fry all the above till fragrant
3. 20 pieces dried chili (hydrated) in
4. 2 tbsp spicy broad bean paste
5. 2 onions sliced, stir fry
6. Add marinated chicken
7. 1 chopped capsicum, mushroom 150g in
8. 1.5 liter water (about 6 cups), boil for 20 mins 
9. Add chicken stock and cook for another 5 mins
10. Serve as a hot pot and add any steamboat ingredients you like!
