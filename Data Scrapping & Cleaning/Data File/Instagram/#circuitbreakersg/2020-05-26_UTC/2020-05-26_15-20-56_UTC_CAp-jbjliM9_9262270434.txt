Amazing landscape📍Wanagiri Hidden hill, Bali
..
Wanagiri Hidden Hills in particular is one of the most popular spots on the island for photographs with pacifying atmosphere. It just a short drive from bedugul area.

If you love nature and lots of pictures for your Instagram, This can be a perfect place to add this to your bali itinerary.
