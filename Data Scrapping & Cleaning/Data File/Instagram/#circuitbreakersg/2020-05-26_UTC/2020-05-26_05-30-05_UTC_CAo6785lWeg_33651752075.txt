What’s the workout?
Myofascial Release and Stretch with Leandra

Learn how to release tension and discomfort in the body with simple Myofascial Release (MFR) techniques. After releasing tension in targeted areas with MFR, we will stretch the body out to increase circulation. This class is suitable for anyone; whether you’re a yogi, athlete, desk-bound worker, parent or more. Come give yourself a relaxing massage and rejuvenating stretch!

All levels are welcome.

Props: Two tennis or lacrosse balls, yoga blocks or books, and a pillow.
