Sweet Potato Fried Rice (serving size 3~4 minion or 1 GIANT 😆)⠀
Japanese Sweet Potato - 3⠀
Red Capsicum - 1 / est. 200g⠀
Broccoli - 1⠀
Brown / Yellow Onion - 2⠀
Paprika Powder - UP TO YOU!⠀
Soya Sauce - UP TO YOU!⠀
Pepper - UP TO YOU!⠀
Olive Oil - UP TO YOU!⠀
⠀
⠀
blend sweet potato chunks in a food processor until they reach your desired rice consistency > heat oil > add onions cook until translucent > add sweet potato rice > add seasoning > cook till tender > add veggies > add soya sauce > combine > done!⠀
